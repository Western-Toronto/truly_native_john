Making Jesus Christ Visible in Unexpected Ways

Another way that 24-7 Prayer ministries differ from a traditional church, is that they make Jesus Christ visible in unexpected ways. The most obvious way is by offering dedicated prayer space, which is rare but needed ministry in todays world. People need place to seek God and spend time in His presence. Such prayer space not only makes Christ visible, but also accessible

In addition, 24-7 Prayer ministries are also called to be devoted to hospitality, the arts, and social justice projects. In hospitality ministry, acts of kindness that make the love of Christ felt and appreciated. In the arts, the story of the life of Christ can be shared, as well as in expressions of the beauty of life and the world around us. In social justice projects, the love of Christ can be felt as the needs of others are met.

In each of these areas of 24-7 Prayer ministry we move beyond just talking about Christ to making it possible for others to encounter Christ through our actions.

While these things can be and often are present in traditional church life, in 24-7 Prayer ministry this is our focus, emphasis, and main activities in ministry. Rather than placing all of our attention on building up a large group gathering, we place it on activities that make Christ known in the world around us.

Sermons FORBIDDEN!

In addition to using gathering time and space in a more open ended way , Alt.Worship gatherings also differ from a traditional church service in that there typically is no preaching. In fact in our 24-7 Prayer Chapel ministry sermons, homilies, teachings, or lectures of any any kind are strictly FORBIDDEN .

The reason for this prohibition is that the main purpose of our gatherings is not to gain more content or information, but rather to spend time with God as we interact with the prayer space. This is an invitation for God to work on transforming our hearts, rather than to fill our minds with more facts.

That said, we do not mind doing Liturgy of the Word in terms of the weekly Old Testament, Psalms, New Testament, and Gospel readings. However, rather than following this with a traditional homily, we will have a scripture Reading Station with thought provoking and life application questions. This will give people more time to sort through the weekly scriptures in a more reflective and transformational way.

We want to Gods Word to speak for itself and for people to have more meaningful and personal interaction with it. Sometimes this approach can have a much more powerful impact on a person than a sermon.

Ever when we do spiritual formation small groups, we will use the approach of Alpha and IVP Inductive Bible Study, which lets the group sort through the issues and questions on their own, rather than telling them what to believe of how to apply it.

Through all of this, we hope that we are also helping to teach people who to seek truth on their own and giving them an important skill in the Christian life.

Spending Time With God and the Nature of Alt.Worship

Two of the main things that make a 24-7 Prayer Chapel different from a traditonial church are the time and space factors. Its about spending time with God and letting Him speak to us, heal us, guide us, transform us into Christ-likeness, and to call us to share the love of Christ in the world around us. This happens as we spend time in prayer interacting with the creative arts stations, scripture stations, multimedia stations, the altar, the Lords Table, and in soaking prayer in the chill space.

For those of us who are in need of a deep physical, emotional, or relational healing, we need to spend time with God seeking that. In the case of all of us who make mistakes, struggle, fail, are apathetic in helping others, and are otherwise not yet prefect saints, we need to spend time with God working on that. For those few who naturally want more of God in their lives, they also need a place to seek that.

Group actives such as Taize, Jesus Prayer, Liturgy of the Table, Daily Office, and small group prayer time can be and should a part of prayer gathering, as they help bring focus, structure, and content to the events. However, they should never take up more than 1/3 of the gathering time. The other 2/3 of the time should be devote offering a deep reflective quite time with God, as we interact with the prayer areas.

Likewise, about a 1/3 of the prayer space should be devoted liturigcal and church calendar related stations, to help us focus on the life of Jesus Christ in an instructual yet interactive way. The other 2/3 of fhe space should be devoted to our life in Christ, calling us to either healing and personal transformation or calling us to transform the world around by sharing the love of Christ with those in need.

Achieving this balance between structured and focused activities and stations, with more open ended time and space is the key to successfully providing alternative worship in the 24-7 Prayer tradition.
