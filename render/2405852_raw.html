Listed: Worlds Best Hotel Pools

andrea June 3, 2014 Listed

On a grey midweek morning, you need a little wanderlust in your life. Incredible infinities, magicallocations and always the most breathtaking views, these poolsare worth booking a ticket for

Marina Bay Sands, Singapore

Although Ive heard its fantastic, Singapore just wasnt that high on my bucketlist until I learned about the infinity pool at the Marina Bay Sands hotel. Now I desperately want to visit. The power of the pool. www.marinabaysands.com

Chongwe River House, Zambezi

Watch an elephant stroll by for a drink from the river as you take your morning dip. No big deal. www.chongweriverhouse.com

Grand Hotel Tremezzo, Lake Como

Its a pool in a lake. Fabulous! I wonder if resident George Clooney stops by for aswim at the Grand Hotel Tremezzos WaterOnTheWater floating pool? www.grandhoteltremezzo.com

The Grace, Santorini

There are too many stunning pools with views of the Caldera to choose from in Santorini! I think the Grace tops the lot. www.gracehotels.com

Delamore Lodge, New Zealand

I included Delamore Lodge in my recent destination guide to New Zealand with good reason. Check out those views! www.delamorelodge.com

Hotel Caruso, Ravello

What is it about these infinity pools? They just do it for me. Throw in unparalleled views of the Amalfi Coast, and Im yours. (Full disclosure: Hotel Carusos pool has been my screen saver for over a year. Its a longterm love). www.hotelcaruso.com

The Spa by Espa, One&Only Reethi Rah, Maldives

The One&Only Reethi Rah hotels pool at The Spa by Espa is almost too good to be true. But then, its in the Maldives, where such beauty is pretty standard. I could spend a day here or a lifetime. www.reethirah.oneandonlyresorts.com

Hanging Gardens Ubud, Bali

I know I included the Hanging Gardens Ubud in my post on The Coolest Hotels Around the World but its infinity pool suspended above the jungle was what clinched it. So it had to be shared here too. Dreamy! www.hanginggardensubud.com

The Cambrian Hotel, Switzerland

High on a hill is an incredible pool, with a view of the Swiss Alps that is to die for. www.thecambrianadelboden.com

Evason MaIn Hot Springs, Jordan

A hidden retreat near the Dead Sea with healing waters. Nuff said. www.sixsenses.com

Hotel du Cap-Eden-Roc, Cap dAntibes

Its as exclusive as it gets here. One of the most-loved hotels by celebrities for its privacy and luxury, the Hotel du Cap-Eden-Roc cliff face pool oozes glamour. www.hotel-du-cap-eden-roc.com

Velassaru, Maldives

Another Maldives pool. This one I cant tell if its the pool or the sea. Which is always a wonderful problem. www.velassaru.com

Amankila, Bali

Because sometimes, one pool isnt enough. You need three. www.amanresorts.com
