Logo Giant Acquires Web Design and Development Firm: Introduces Referral Program and 3 in 1 Bundle For $299

Logo Online Pros Strengthens Domain Expertise, Celebrates By Offering Logo, Banner, Web Design with Free Domain Registration & Hosting

We will now able to design, market and deliver competitively priced, end-to-end web solutions to our customers as well as prospectssomething I see predominately missing in the market today.

Dallas, TX (PRWEB) April 14, 2014

Logo Online Pros, a web design and graphics agency, today announced the acquisition of Web Design Bizz, a web development startup based in Dallas, TX. Logo Online Pros, which has been in service since 2010, helps small and mid-sized businesses around the world with corporate identity design, web and graphic design, andto some extentweb development.

With this acquisition, Logo Online Pros will establish its footprint in the domain of modern web development. Dan McLeod, vice president of Logo Online Pros, said We are excited about integrating the team at Web Design Bizz with ours. This will not only help us strengthen our service portfolio, but will enable us to diversify and tap into a wider market. Most important of all, we will now able to design, market and deliver competitively priced, end-to-end web solutions to our customers as well as prospectssomething I see predominately missing in the market today.

On this occasion, Logo Online Pros has also launched a referral program for its Design2Launch service plan . The firm will reward referrers an Amazon gift card worth $20, and referees a $20 discount, for its Design2Launch plan. With no payment tiers or restrictions, the referrer will earn a $20 gift card for every successful referral. The plan itself, priced at $299, includes custom logo, web and graphics design, HTML/CSS conversion, domain registration as well as web hosting and maintenance.

With this integration, Logo Online Pros intends to offer a single window web services solution by including corporate identity, web graphics, template-free website design, PSD to HTML, domain registration, hosting and maintenance in a single plan.

For more information on the Design2Launch plan, visit http://bit.ly/design2launch or follow us on Twitter @logoonlinepro .

ABOUT LOGO ONLINE PROS:
Logo Online Pros , established in 2010, is an eminent web design and graphics agency. Having completed over 500+ projects for diverse industries including fashion, entertainment, financial services, food & beverages and real estate, Logo Online Pros strives to deliver cost-effective services to small and mid-sized firms.

ABOUT WEB DESIGN BIZZ:
Web Design Bizz is a web development startup that specializes in bespoke web 2.0 projects. During the past two years, the team has worked on web development projects for many small firms, as well as well-known brands such as Pizza Hut.
