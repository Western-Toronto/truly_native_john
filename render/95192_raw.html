Features

Simple Control, Easy Setup, Multi-Room Solution


Qualcomm AllPlay

Panasonic ALL series speakers with AllPlay lets listeners easily configure music play over multiple rooms from multiple digital music sources, all over an existing Wi-Fi network. A smartphone allows wireless enjoyment of music from music subscription services and Internet radio in any room of the house. In addition, the same songs can be simultaneously played over multiple speakers.

* The availability of subscription services and Internet radio may vary depending on the country.

Control Directly from Online Music Services App


Enjoy AllPlay with Controlling Original Apps

Because control is possible directly from the GUI of Internet radio or an online music service app, AllPlay can be enjoyed with constantly updated screens and functions. Also, after listening to your smartphone away from home, you can conveniently switch to other compatible speakers right from the screen when you return home without changing App.

*The availability of online music services and Internet radio may vary depending on the country.
More Detail of Compatible Music Service App

5 Speaker Construction
(1 subwoofer/ 2 woofers/
2 tweeters)

The 5 Speaker construction with dedicated subwoofer produces a room filling, distortion free sound with excellent clarity and powerful bass.

Panasonics own LincsD-Amp TM 2nd generation

Reduces Sound Distortion for a clearer Sound

The distortion that occurs during digital amp processing is compensated to reproduce an acoustic waveform that is faithful to the original sound. By compensating for distortion, including that of the electrical circuits, it provides purer sounds with greater depth.

Double Layer Nano Bamboo Cone Speaker

Clear Vocals are Produced with Minimal Distortion

The use of a robust yet lightweight material creates a more responsive sound, giving clearer vocals more dynamic sound, and crisper dialogue.

Wi-Fi Easy Setup


No Complicated Settings Required

It's WPS compatible, so all you have to do for Wi-Fi setup is press a button. Even Wi-Fi routers that don't support WPS can be set up by selecting "ALL" on a smartphone or tablet and inputting the password, making it just as simple as ordinary Wi-Fi connection. Setup is easy enough for anyone, so Wi-Fi music can be enjoyed all through the house.
