6 Reasons Canadian Vacations are Awesome

0
By Darrell on December 5, 2013 Archive , Travel tips

Canada is a beautiful country thats known for its glaciers, lush forests, massive lakes, and epic accents. But theres a lot more to Canadian vacations than the chance to get to know Mother Nature on a personal level. There are many more things about Canada that make it a pretty awesome place to go on vacation. Heres a look at everything thats awesome in Canada.

Natural Beauty is the Best Kind

The Canadian Rocky Mountains in Western Canada are arguably even more spectacular than the American version to the south. The national parks in this area are perfect for hiking and camping, or simply a scenic drive. Parks such as Banff, Jasper, Yoho, and Kootenay stretch across Alberta and British Columbia, and attract visitors year-round. Physically challenging trails take hikers along paths that feature breathtaking views, making it an experience of a lifetime.

Niagra Falls has been an iconic tourist spot for decades, and for good reason. Many people argue that the view of the falls is far more spectacular from the Canadian side than from the New York Side. Youll never know until you venture over there to check it out yourself though. Those arent the only naturally beautiful spectacles in Canada. Dinosaur Provincial Park in Alberta is another cant miss, as is Cabot Trail in Nova Scotia.

These parks are the perfect place to bring the family and enjoy a picnic amidst the natural wonder of your surroundings.

Theres a lot of Festivals

Canadians dont mess around when it comes to their history and culture. They celebrate their heritage with festivals all year round. The Calgary Stampede, the Celebration of Light in Vancouver, and the Quebec Winter Carnival in Quebec City are all great attractions for visitors to take part in. The Quebec Winter Carnival features the opportunity to skate outdoors on lakes, as well as observing and partaking in ice carving events.

The Toronto Film Festival is one of the most influential gatherings in North America, drawing the worlds finest actors, producers, writers, directors, and more to Quebec.

There Are So Many Activities!

Outdoor activities abound across the Canadian countryside. Just head outside and youll find activities to keep you busy for days on end. If cross-country skiing is your thing, strap on a set of skis, gather up some friends, and get to it. Winter in Canada means snow sport enthusiasts come out of the woodwork.

You just cant go wrong with snowboarding, downhill skiing, and, of course, hockey. Youre in Canada, after all. Did you actually think youd get through your vacation without playing (or at least watching) a thrilling game of hockey? If youre not a fan, you will be before you leave this magical place. The Hockey Hall of Fame in Toronto is a great place for enthusiasts of the beloved sport to gather and pay tribute to their favorite players and team. While youre there, pick up tickets to a Toronto Maple Leafs or Calgary Flames game. Even if they lose, youll still have an amazing time!

Canada is pretty awesome the rest of the year, too. Just because it rocks the winter months doesnt mean its dull and boring while the sun is shining. Those amazing national parks are even more beautiful in the spring as the blooms are peeking out, and in the fall when the colors paint the countryside with rich, hues that make everywhere you look like a giant postcard.

Theres also fishing. Halifax, Nova Scotia, and Newfoundland are known to be areas with exceptional fishing opportunities. No matter which season it is when you venture into Canada, youll find plenty of bites on the end of your fishing lines. Whether its ice fishing in the winter months, on a boat or off-shore in the summertime, youll be sure to walk away with quite the catch.

Also, Canadians Can Party

You may not believe it, but Montreal, Toronto, and Vancouver have crazy nightlife scenes. They really are wild and exciting cities to party in, but Calgary, Quebec City, Victoria, and Halifax are must-see cities, too. Countless clubs, live shows and entertainment, along with great food, and some of the nicest, most accepting people in the world make Canadian cities some of the best places to let loose.

You Can Actually Afford It

Canadian vacations are some of the most affordable for Americans. While the strength of the dollar isnt as impressive as it used to be, its still a bargain in Canada, and its nice to know you dont lose money when you transfer your American dollars to Canadian funds. Without the burden of a low conversion rate, you dont have to worry about pinching your pennies while youre on vacation in Canada the way you would in Europe or other destinations.

Generally speaking, the cost of living in Canada is comparable to the U.S., so you shouldnt end up paying an arm and a leg for a hotel room there. You might think that a flight to Canada would be expensive, but Air Canada has flights to fit any budget.

The People There are Super Awesome

Its commonly said that Canada is filled with some of the most accepting, open-minded people in the world. Its just part of the culture. Part of their niceness has to do with the huge immigrant population here. Canada has so many different cultures that are all so vastly different, that we have no choice but to accept each other. Being polite and nice is just socially expected and encouraged in Canada.

Canada really is a pretty awesome place to go on vacation. Theres plenty of ways to enjoy the outdoors and get in touch with nature, festivals to attend, tourist attractions to see, and best of all, its affordable. Next time youre planning an enjoyable trip with your closest friends or your family, dont overlook Canada, eh? Its a great place to live, and its absolutely chock full of really cool places to go and things to see. Best of all, it wont break the bank to get there!
