NO PURCHASE NECESSARY TO ENTER OR WIN

1. DESCRIPTION OF THE CONTEST: The JotForm Web Form Design Contest is designed to encourage and inspire anybody to begin participating in building and designing forms with JotForm. Judges as selected by JotForm will be from the JotForm community, JotForm team, and select designers. They will choose winning designs in accordance with these JotForm Web Form Design Contest rules. Prizes will be awarded to eligible winning participants.

Immediate family means parents, children, siblings, spouse or individual living in the same household. 5. SUBMISSION OF DESIGNS:

5.1 Sign in to your JotForm Account. If you do not have a JotForm Account, you can create one without charge from JotForm. If you already have a JotForm account, you can just sign in to your JotForm account.

5.2 Visit the JotForm Form Designer landing page to get an idea what you can do with it. Then follow the steps below to enter:

a. Decide on a Form Design Idea: In order to submit a form design to the contest, participants must decide on a form design idea to work on. Participants are free to come up with their own ideas to work on.

b. Language: All form designs are preferred in English. Designs in any other language will be considered but hard to judge because of language complications. Designs that are lewd, obscene, pornographic, racist, sexist, or otherwise inappropriate to the contest will be disqualified at JotForms sole discretion.

6. JUDGING: Each form design will be judged by a panel of JotForm experts which exists of the JotForm Team, JotForm Community, and select designers. Each form design will be judged according to the following factors:

(1) Design
(2) Functionality
(3) Ease of use

Each form design determined by the judges to be successfully completed will be deemed a completed form design. If a participant wishes to challenge the judges determination as to whether or not their form design should qualify as a completed form design, he/she must do so through the email link provided on the Main Contest Page. In this event, JotForm will re-examine the relevant form design and make a final determination, at its sole discretion, as to whether the form design should be classified as a completed design. The decision of JotForm in this matter is final and binding.

In the event that no completed form designs are received, no prizes will be awarded. Determinations of judges are final and binding, except as set forth above. Winners will be notified by email and a list of category and grand prize winners will be posted on the main contest page on or after Dec 11, 2014 for a period of at least 1 week.
