How #Lyfie Works

Have you ever wanted to find a specific photo that you posted on Instagram but there are so many posts you can't. How about photos from your family trip or particular event that you want to share with friends? #Lyfie can help! It's an easy way to make Instagram albums and slideshows that you can share with friends. Just sign in with your Instagram Account. It's Free.

Sign in with Instagram



It's simple and easy to enjoy #Lyfie

Just do what you do now, take great photos on Instagram. Then just sign in to #Lyfie to build custom albums and slide shows of your photos.

Enjoy #Lyfie and tell your friends to use #Lyfie too!

Sign in with Instagram


#Lyfie is simple to use

Just visit Lyfie.mobi and click the Sign In With Instagram button.

Sign In with Instagram

Sign in to #Lyfie using your Instagram username and password. Don't worry the log in is done with Instagram's API so we DO NOT know or keep any of your Instagram log in information.



#Lyfie builds your page for you automatically!

Once you log in, we display all of your Instagram photos for you. These are photos you have uploaded to your Instagram account.

#Lyfie gives you control

We give you the ability to add private descriptions for your photos and also to place them in to photo albums. You can group photos together based on event, people, or however you'd like. Your photos can also be a part of multiple albums. You decide. Just click on one of the buttons under a photo to get started.



#Lyfie Albums are easy to make and share

You can choose the album name from a list of your existing albums or you can create a new album by typing in the new album name. Then click ADD TO ALBUM and your done!

#Lyfie descriptions are personal

Click the Add Description button under a photo and you can add notes, descriptions, or anything that will help you remember this moment later. These descriptions appear in your photos details page for you, and they DO NOT get posted to Instagram. Kind of like a private message to yourself :)



#Lyfie Albums are unlimted

You can categorize your photos and others photos you find on Instagram in unlimited ways. By using the search page you can even find the best photos on Instagram and save them in an album for yourself to view and share later.

#Lyfie Slide Shows are automagic

We automatically make slideshows of your albums for you. Once you are done making an album, just click the View As Slide Show button and your phone screen will display a cool slideshow of your album! Pretty cool huh!



Share #Lyfie with friends

After your done making an album or slideshow, just view them on your phone and you can share them with your friends! Just hit the Share on facebook link to share to facebook, or if you want to email, text or share on another social network, just click your phones built in share button :).
