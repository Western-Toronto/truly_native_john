Description

KeepTrax automatically creates a digital journal as you go about your day. No check-in is required. Key details such as your location, address, arrival time, departure time, duration, and the photos you take, are attached to each visit, so you will always have a digital record of the places you've been. All the data is stored privately and safely, just for you.

You can easily update the details on the places you visited, add notes, apply star ratings, see calendar info, and view your visits by location or by date.

KeepTrax also allows you to easily share your places and journeys with friends and family. You an share in real time or you can share historical pins (or sets of pins) and associated photos, comments, and other context. You decide what to share, how to share it, and even for how long. Easy.

Make KeepTrax your digital journal for personal or business use.

Here are some ways you can use KeepTrax:

- Going on vacation? Use KeepTrax to automatically create a travelogue with your places, dates, times, and photos. Easily share your journey either in real time or after you've returned home from your trip. Capture your moments!

- Does your job take you into the field? Use KeepTrax to remember client visits - where, when, who. Easily share where you've been been colleagues and managers - save time on weekly status reports. Jog your memory!

- Always on the go? Use KeepTrax to get a clear record of the places you visit, when you're there, and how long you stay.

- House or Apartment hunting? Use KeepTrax to automatically collect a searchable list of the properties you've seen. Easily share details of your property visits with your realtor so they can always actively help you on your search. Recall the details!


Note: KeepTrax uses location data. It works actively to conserve your battery, but continued use of location data could reduce battery life. You are able to turn on/off location tracking from within the KeepTrax app settings screen.
