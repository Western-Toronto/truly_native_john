It is not What it Seems Painted foods

Mar 11, 2014 by Neha in Art

Japanese artist Hikaru Cho came up with her amazing camouflage painting series of food , named Its Not What It Seems . She painted different food items to other food products like the exterior of banana as cucumber , a tomato into orange , she used acrylic paints to create this amazing art to show it is not what it seems.

Art credits : Hikaru Cho
