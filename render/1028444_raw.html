This is your one stop source for natural and quality henna and mehndi supplies.

I test every paste on myself, and I take pictures of the result of each test I do. I have been in the henna business for 10 years, I want to supply everyone with an honest and informative experience. Thank you for choosing Red Head Henna!

Henna is one of the safest body arts out there, using only natural products to create temporary designs on the skin. Our henna paste consists only of the natural henna powder, lemon juice, essential oils and sugar.

Henna is a plant found in the Middle East and Africa. It holds natural staining properties which have been used for centuries to create intricate patterns on the skin. These patterns only remain for about 1-3 weeks depending on skin type and design location.
