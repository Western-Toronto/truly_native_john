Why orange maize?

Your support of Grow Hope will make a real change for children in Zambia, where we'll be providing families with orange maize seeds to grow healthy, nutritious crops to ensure children don't grow up undernourished, with all of the detrimental effects that follow, like cognitive impairment and stunting.
