Tyra Banks' Changing Looks

Share
Share
1 of 15
LIST VIEW SLIDE VIEW GRID VIEW

  • 1. 1990

    Banks modeled couture on the Paris runways, but her daily wardrobe was strictly down-home. "I had on my big overalls, hiking boots, a plaid shirt and no makeup," Banks has said. "I wasn't sleek. I was a bum."

  • 2. 1991

    The up-and-coming model nailed her senior yearbook photo at L.A.'s Immaculate Heart High School.

  • 3. 1994

    A beautifully braided Tyra Banks learned to do her own makeup backstage at fashion shows. Her favorite look? Lots of mascara and orange blush, "because I have golden skin tones."

  • 4. 2002

    "Curling the lashes is essential," makeup artist Billy B. has said. "I do it all the time on Tyra Banks. It gives the eye a lift, and makes mascara look prettier."

  • 5. 2004

    Banks went high glam as the host of America's Next Top Model , donning "hair weaves and false eyelashes."

  • 6. 2005

    The model mogul produced a second TV hit: The Tyra Banks Show . Her secret to staying fresh-looking? "I love Vaseline. My mom used it forever and has not a wrinkle on her face."

  • 7. 2006

    The talk show host went Old Hollywood for the Daytime Emmy Awards, wearing her hair in bouffant Marilyn Monroe curls.

  • 8. 2007

    The ANTM host played up her green eyes with black liner and smoky shadow.

  • 9. 2008

    Banks lightened up her look at the Emmys with curly blond tresses.

  • 10. 2009

    Sporting a shoulder-length straight 'do, Banks picked up her second Emmy for Outstanding Talk Show.

  • 11. 2010

    Banks revealed to WWD that she went with a clean look for the 13th cycle of America's Next Top Model : "I am wearing a chignon every single day. No earrings, no nothing."

  • 12. 2011

    For the opening night of the Metropolitan Opera season, Banks played up her eyes with cat-eye liner.

  • 13. 2012

    Banks hit the Time 100 Gala wearing a bright pop of fuchsia lipstick.

  • 14. 2013

    Leaving her strands sleek and straight, Banks lets her classic red lip and winged eyeliner take center stage.

  • 15. 2014

    Banks keeps it simple with soft waves.
