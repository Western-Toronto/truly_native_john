The City of Hull-ture

October 14 2014

  • - Tags:
  • hull
  • infographic
  • capital of culture
  • city of culture
  • hull 2017

At First Hull Trains, we are kicking off the celebrations early! To celebrate Hull being named the City of Culture we've put together an interactive infographic all about Hull. You can check out the events happening in 2017, benefits of being City of Culture, travel into the city, and lots more.

Hover over the infographic to find out more information and see how many days are left until we are the City of Culture!

For more information on booking trains to Hull for events in 2017 then please see our timetables nearer the time! Also keep a look out for updates and travel information during the course of the year.

By Jennifer Handley
