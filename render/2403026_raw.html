Vintage Games for the Vintage Gamer: Part I

Remember When Gaming Was Hard?

Gather around! Im about tell all of you young whippersnappers a little something about gaming.Back in my day, you really had to love gaming because it was work! HARD. GRUELING. WORK. When playing Nintendo, there wasno such thing as Save Game. You sat down in front of your TV and you had to go big or go home (unless you were some kind of spoiled brat with Game Genie, in which case dont touch me). You could get to the very last level; the very last boss and die. Guess where you started? Thats right my friends, back at the very beginning; but kids from my generation, we didnt cry about it, because we had been battle hardened. In fact, there are many NES games to this day that I NEVER conquered: Mario Bros. 3 , Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles , and Little Nemo: The Dream Master to name a few. However, there was also no feeling more satisfying than when I did beat them so suck on that Mario 1 & 2 and also Ducktales . I owned you. With that being said, I wouldnt take any of those hard times back. I would keep all the wins and all the heartbreaking defeats because all of those vintage gaming hours I logged made me the gamer I am today. So lets check out some of those games that grew me up right!

Oregon Trail (PC)

Every fucking time.

Oh no! Ma just died of dysentery. Well shit, you tried to ford the river and drowned all but one of your oxen. Hells bells, your wagon caught fire and now youre out of axles. Looks like its time to go into town and make some really horrible deals where some asshole will give you one wagon axle for all your food. Seems like a fair tradenot. Well now that I have no food I guess Ill go hunting. Hallelujah! I shot a three-ton bear! Oh, I can only take 200 lbs of it back to my wagon? Not wasteful at all.

These my friends, are all the trials and tribulations of getting from Independence, MO all the way out to Oregon City, OR on what is known as The Oregon Trail . You will battle illness, mother nature, and just plain ole bad luck. You will take ahellish trip all in the name of education! Developed originally in 1971, this game was created to teach little school children the realities of what it might have been like to be a pioneer on The Oregon Trail back in the 1800s. Since its original inception, the game has been updated several times and released not only on PC but also on iOS and Android format as well. In all seriousness though, this game is fun as hell yet a bit challenging. So jump in the wagon and lets get to learnin about old timey pioneer struggles!

Wolfenstein 3D (PC)

Get dead asshole.

Now many of you are probably familiar with the newest release of this game called Wolfenstein: The New Order , but you should know this all originally started back in 1992. The original game was called Wolfenstein 3D and you play William BJ Blazkowicz, an Allied spy whos being imprisoned by the Nazis in Castle Wolfenstein. The premise of the game is you are trying to escape your imprisonment as well as carrying out secret missions against the evil Nazi regime. Each level essentially is a floor in which you have to go through a series of heavy iron doors. Within these rooms, you will face several different variations of Nazi scum who will scream at you in the scariest sounding German you have ever heard. As you go through the rooms, you will kill any and alldouchey Nazis and take the treasure that they are hoarding. At the end of each level, you will face a boss who is always super-roided up and ridiculous looking. However, after you kill each and every boss, you get to watch what is called Death Cam. It essentially shows your nemesis melting into a puddle of bloody goo. Maybe Im a horrible person but I find it extremely gratifying. No Death Cam is more satisfying than the last one though, in which you get to kill Adolf Hitler himself. Just take a look at it in all its gloriousness. What a chump.

Where in the World is Carmen Sandiego? (PC)

We need your help to catch this notorious criminal.

Carmen Sandiego is the sneaky trench coat rocking, fedora-wearing gangster and the criminal mastermind behind the international crime syndicate V.I.L.E. I started out my career in law enforcement as a gumshoe, but many years later I am an experienced detective. Ive traveled all over the world and Ive caught a number of her underlings but to this very day, Ms. Sandiego still evades my grasp. Which begs the question, Where in the world is Carmen Sandiego?. No one is talking so if you have any clues as to her whereabouts please contact me @TheNeonScene on Twitter. Your tip could be the one that cracks this case wide open!

Mario Kart 64 (N64)

This might be considered a newerish game on a newerish console but my blog, my rules.DEAL WITH IT!So who doesnt like Mario Kart ? Oh, Ill tell you whono one because it is flawless and amazing. I have raced my way to fame and glory with Yoshi. Our personal favorite track is Warios Raceway and we have won the Gold Trophy in every single Cup. We kicked all the asses and took all the names and no even one stands a chance. I will proclaim here and now that I am the best ever at Mario Kart . I am a legend. There is no man, woman, child or otherworldly being that could challenge me when it comes to this game, and yet people try to do it all the time. My personal Twitter account is @Andii_Renee , please come and fight me about it if you think Im wrong.

Yoshis coming in hot bitches!

Toejam and Earl (Sega)

Now I didnt play a ton of Sega Genesis games because I never owned one. All my Sega hours were logged in at the house of friends and Im gonna be honest with you, I have no idea what the point of this game even is. I just remember my friend and I used to sneak play this on her brothers Sega when he wasnt around. Basically Toejam is a three-legged, red alien who is all decked out in THUG LYFE gear and Earl is his overweight friend who is wearing killah sneakers.

So, I had to do a little light research to find out what the game is actually about. As it turns out,ToeJam and Earl have crashed their ship onto Earth. Throughout the game, you will wander about picking up all the pieces of your spacecraft so you can get back into orbit and to their home planet of.wait for itFunkatron #classic. During your venture back to Fuckatron, you will wander about and have to avoid pesky Earthlings, who are out to interrupt your groove. This game is eccentric and weird and everything I love. Ive even heard talks that there might be a reboot in the makings!

Pump up the volume! Pump up the jam!

Well, this concludes Part I of Vintage Games for the Vintage Gamer! I was originally going to do one giant post until I realized it would take me until the end of my natural life to complete. So what do you think? What were some of your favorite vintage games ? By all means, please let me know and if its not already on my list, I will certainly add it :). If you havent been satisfied please check out Part II and stay tuned for Vintage Games for the Vintage Gamer: Part III!
