How Does It Work?

What is CLEP?

CLEP tests are basically like taking the final exam without going to the class. You study however you want, at your own speed. When you are confident that youre ready, just go to your local testing center and take the two hour test.
If you get a passing grade (normally about 60%), CollegeBoard will recommend you for college credit. You will need to transfer this credit to your college (make sure you check with your college before taking the test though).

CLEP exams can benefit everyone. Military people, homeschooled students, working adults, and normal college students have all used CLEP to gain valuable college credits.

Why SpeedyPrep?

We give students a fast and easy way to learn. Each of our courses includes a brief CLEP study guide to read first. Then youll want to dive into our practice questions. As you answer questions, youll get to see the correct answers and explanations. Even better, many of our explanations include short instructional videos that make learning fun.

As you go, our progress bar will measure your knowledge. Once you reach 90%, you can take the CLEP test.
