Published on May 22, 2013

Written & Directed by an 8-year old - this LEGO Star Wars Stop Motion Animation covers much of the beginning of the Return of the Jedi, centered on Jabba's Palace and the Rancor pit. It follows the Star Wars movie fairly closely, but includes some new humor.

Includes LEGO Luke, Princess Leia (in and out of Boushh disguise), Han Solo (in and out of carbonite), Chewbacca, Boba Fett, Lando, C3PO, R2D2, Jabba, Salacious Crumb, Gamorrean guard, the Rancor and more.

Much of the audio comes from the movie - in no way should this be confused with the actual Star Wars movie from Lucas Films (now owned by Disney). This is simply a fun parody and should only be treated as such.
