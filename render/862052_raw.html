Share a pic of your #poochpassenger and win!

Got a four-legged friend who just loves a road trip? Then send us a fun photo of your doggy driving companion for a chance to win a 50 Pets at Home voucher. Plus well donate 1 to Manchester Dogs Home for every entry we receive*!

Take a quick snap of your furry travel buddy in the car, either when youre both out on the road or all ready to set off (be sure to keep everyone safe in the process, though we definitely dont want any accidents).

Then use the hashtag #poochpassenger to share the picture on Twitter or Instagram remember to make sure any posts are set to public so well be able to see your photo on our scrapbook of entries below .

The competition closes at 23:59 on the 16th November 2014 so get those pics in quick
