Why try Findmypast?

Findmypast can give you all the tools and resources you need to discover your family history.

  • We have the largest online collection of UK parish records , which give information on births, marriages and deaths from as far back as the 1500s
  • We have the most comprehensive collection of UK military records anywhere online, and our census records can help you to trace your family back to 1841
  • Our newspaper archive includes over 10 million newspaper pages from 1710 to 1955, offering insights into the world in which your family lived
  • With our Hints feature , also free this weekend, we search our records on your behalf and suggest potential matches to the people you add to your family tree
