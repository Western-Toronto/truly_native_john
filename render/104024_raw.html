Bold and Fearless: Katniss from The Hunger Games

The Hunger Games series broke out in the book world with the sort of youthful momentum that hadnt been seen since Harry Potter. Likewise, The Hunger Games movies have been a commercial success. At the center of this phenomenon is Katniss Everdeen (played in the films by Jennifer Lawrence).

Shes the rugged rule-breaker who has had to struggle to survive. Shes economical, street savvy, and a warrior. Her wardrobe isnt glamorous but then, shes fighting for her life. Her clothes are suitable for the wooded arena in which she has to camp, climb, hunt, and kill. They reflect the forest: greens, browns, and other dark colors so that she can blend easily into her surroundings.

Katniss is stylish, but thats not her first priority. Ive gathered together some pieces that are understated and utilitarian, while still being attractive.
