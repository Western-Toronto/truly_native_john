Published on Jul 1, 2014

The Hyundai iX35 Crossover SUV is a new crossover vehicle from Hyundai. Built for drivers with all level of experience, the Hyundai iX35 is just as suited for an adventure in the countryside as it is for the school run. It is an ideal car for families or couples or anyone wanting to experience the versatility of a crossover vehicle. The Hyundai iX35 has earned a 5 Star Euro NCAP score safety rating, the maximum safety rating it can achieve. With a combined mpg of 41.5, the Hyundai iX35 is not only stylish it's also efficient.
