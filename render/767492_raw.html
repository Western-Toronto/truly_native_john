10 Better 10,000 Investments Than a Gold Apple Watch

Follow us and your life will never be the same again (potentially)

The new Apple watch is out and the price tag on the gold option is crazy, $10,000-$17,000 to be exact. It doesnt matter if its pounds, dollars, or mint sweeties, thats alot of money. So we decided to sit down and work out what we would do if we had a spare 10k lying about in our bank accounts rather than buy a gold Apple watch.

1. A real throne

Every house has a throne room but rarely a throne. For just 10,500 you could buy a real wooden throne to rest your royal buttocks on while youwell, drop the kids at the pool? Were willing to fork out the extra 500 for this beauty. We can see ourselves lording it up over the household whilst going about our daily business on that throne. We particularly like the candle holder and ceramic pull chain. Very quaint!

2. A hoverboard

If you are familiar with a little movie called Back to the Future: Part 2 you will have seen (and fallen in love with) the hoverboard. Hendo Hoverboards are currently developing a working hoverboard so that you can be as cool as Marty McFly. If you were lucky enough to be part of their Kickstarter campaign then you could have bagged yourself one for $10,000 (just under 6,500).We would certainly sacrifice a gold Apple watch for one of these badboys.

3. Oval Office Desk

Im pretty sure Bex has her eye on this one for her office, but who wouldnt? For approximately 6,000 you can get a full-sized replica Oval Office desk ,so you can feel like the President whilst sorting through your junk mail, unpaid bills, and catalogue subscriptions.

4. 3D chocolate printer

This needs no explaining. And why would it? For a bargain 4,000 you can get your very own 3D chocolate printer that will print any chocolate design you want. At that bargain price you could invest in two with some leftover money to buy a hefty supply of chocolate. Have both printers working hard at once, so you never run out of 3D chocolate in fancy designs.

5. Outdoor cinema

Watching a movie outside is all the rage at the minute so why not get your own cinema kit? For just over 2,000 you can get a complete outdoor cinema kit to wow your friends with. All you need is some good weather and some friends to enjoy it with. At that price you could afford to buy every single DVD ever (well, nearly) instead of investing in a gold Apple watch. Outdoor cinema with friends? Lonely and expensive Apple watch time?

6. Kids ride on car

10,000 wont get you a real Bugatti but you can get one for your kids. Junior can roll around in style for the cool price of 180 . For that price you could kit every kid that you know out with some fancy wheels. We can just see the scene on your street now dozens of kids driving around in their ride on cars. That sight alone is worth every penny of 10,000.

7. A real car

For less than 10k you can also get yourself a brand new car. A real one! Some basic models start at 6,000 depending on the make, so there is still plenty of spare cash to kit it out with extras. If you wanted to go for something a bit flashier you could even just put a 10,000 deposit down on a luxury motor. Your new car will be able to take you places, which is something were willing to bet the gold Apple watch cant do. You also wont have to update your new car every few months or have to take it into the Apple store to get it repaired when all hell breaks loose.


8. Once in a lifetime holiday

You could jet off to New York, or just spend two weeks in Spain with a heap of spending money. A holiday for a family of four can be as low as 2,000. Thats five family holidays! Or if you fancy something for you and your better half, you could join the Mile High Club, twice! Wish.co.uk offer a luxury mile-high package on a private plane for one hour of air nookie. Oh Er matron! You can also buy round the world tickets for around 4,000 meaning you could literally travel round the world 2 and a bit times for the price of a gold Apple watch. New experiences or new watch? Tough one.

9. A wedding

A couple of us here have upcoming weddings and 10,000 could easily pay for a great day,with all the trimmings AND an open bar. According to statistics the average wedding in the UK is in the region of 14,000 but we reckon we could do it for far less (isnt that what Pinterest is for?!). No need to worry about cutting people off the guest list, everyone is invited! If youre thinking about buying a gold Apple watch consider donating to our wedding funds instead. Well even put you on the guest list if youre lucky.

10. A LOT of Designer clothes

How often do you get to blow this amount of money on yourself? Probably never so why not kit yourself out with some new threads, bags, shoes, hats. You could buy 33 Michael Kors bags or 31 pairs of Louboutins or two of the dresses Grace Kelly wore to The Oscars. Why would you need a gold Apple watch when you could dress like Grace Kelly?
