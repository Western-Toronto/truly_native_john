The Wildest Things To Ever Happen To A Pizza

Categories: Features , Food | by Brittany High

  • Tweet

SpikeTV just aired the premiere of their brand new cooking show called Frankenfood. Creative folks compete to win $10,000 by putting together outrageous ingredients to create the most unique and kooky concoctions. In addition to the dough, the winner also gets their dish added to the menu at a local restaurant. Cool! Missed the first episode, youll have to wait until Sunday, June 29th to catch the next one. In the meantime, lets drool over:

Presented by Spike TVs Frankenfood . Sundays at 10/9c.

Scorpion Pizza

This pizza doesnt just burn the roof of your mouth, it stings too!

Pizza Cake

Marie Antoinette is all Let them eat pizza cake! and the French are like Okay, actually? Lets NOT cut off her head that sounds delicious!

Thanksgiving Pizza

Basically Thanksgiving on a pizza. Topped with smoked turkey, corn souffle, sausage stuffing and cranberry relish. Sounds iffy, but hey, its pizza!

Macaroni & Cheese Pizza

Cant decide between pasta and pizza? Why not have both?!

Ramen-crusted Pizza

Two college student staples come together for this funky pie.

Pizza Cocktail

Pizza that you can drink and might even get you a little sauced. Wait, was that a pizza joke? Maybe!

Burger Pizza

Look! Its a burgerIts a pizza Its a burger pizza!

Pizza Beer

Its all the fixings of a Friday night in one sip! Efficient.

Pizza Pancakes

Wouldnt be the first time you ate pizza for breakfast! (And lets be honest, probably not the last.)

Pizza Ice Cream

I scream, you scream we all scream for pizza-flavored ice cream!

Pizza Cupcakes

Dinner and dessert in one delicious bite.

Pizza with Cheese Burger Crust

Hi, cheese burger-crusted pizza! Would you kindly get inside my mouth, please?

Pizza with Chicken Nugget Crust

You too, chicken nugget crusted za! You complete me!

Hot Dog-Stuffed Crust Pizza

The best way to eat a hot dog is inside of a pizza. With extra mustard.

Cicada Pizza

They finally found a use for those annoying noisy bugs: pizza toppings!

Pizza Sushi

Because who hasnt dreamed about eating pizza with chopsticks?

Double Sensation Pizza

The insanity of this pizza is only matched by how delicious it probably is.

Squid Ink Pizza

Hah! And youve got friends who complain about mushrooms

Pizza In a Cone

Eating pizza has never been more convenient!

Pizza On Chicken

Because comfort food is best served in pairs.

Everglades Pizza

If youre in the mood for a pizza that will bite you back, this za topped with alligator and pythons should do the trick!
