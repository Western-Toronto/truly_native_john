Ginger Puffs

1 3/4 cups all-purpose flour

1 1/2 tsp. ground ginger

1 1/2 tsp. cinnamon

1/2 tsp. allspice

1/4 tsp. baking soda

1 stick butter

1/2 cup brown sugar (firmly packed)

1/4 cup molasses

1 large egg

1/4 cup minced candied ginger

Sift flour, ginger, cinnamon, allspice, and baking soda together. Set aside.

In a bowl, use an electric mixer to beat butter and brown sugar until fluffy. Add molasses, egg, and candied ginger, blend well. Gradually add flour and egg to form a dough. Shape into a large ball, wrap in plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 2 hours.

Preheat oven to 375 degrees. Scoop 1 TBS of dough and roll into a ball. Coat in sugar and place on a greased baking sheet. Using the bottom of a cup, press balls into 2 inch circles. Bake 10-12 minutes, turning halfway. Let cool before adding frosting.

Egg Nog Frosting

2 oz cream cheese, softened

2 oz salted butter, softened

1 teaspoon vanilla extract

1/4 cup eggnog

1 tsp. nutmeg

1 1/4 cups powdered sugar

Whip softened cream cheese and butter together. Mix in vanilla extract. Gradually add eggnog and powdered sugar and mix until blended.
