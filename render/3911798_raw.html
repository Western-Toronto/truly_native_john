Why do research for Debt Consolidation?

There are many ways in which your debts can be consolidated. Consolidation means to have all your outstanding dues and payments brought under one umbrella, a single debt to say so. With a single debt to repay, repayment is done easily and the interest rates for the same would be very low as well.

What Debt Experts Say?

  • As a debtor, you could opt to repay low or pay less, but dont make the folly of doing both at the same time. Lets take for example, most of your debt burden is due to credit cards, the premium for the same would take a long time to complete being paid. The same would be for personal loans as well. Now if you would like to repay both debts together and fast, along with a low rate of interest, repayments would thus need to be more. This would be when you repay low individually on both, but not if the debt is consolidated as one and repaid low as one.
  • When you consolidate debts into a home loan, the repayments for the same would be low. This is because the interest rate is low and the term for repayment extends. When the loans tenure is extended on personal and credit card loans, the amount you repay would be high, so be careful
  • In the case of a personal loan that is unsecured, which is usually taken to pay off smaller debts on payday loans and cards, you could use this money to pay off creditors. This would bring in low rates of interest, repayment would be single and one time, term would be shorter too.
  • When consolidating credit cards, the rate of interest would be more than what the personal loan consolidation would be. It would be the amount difference between credit cards and that of the new loan, which would decide if you would save anything at all after paying off the monthlies.
