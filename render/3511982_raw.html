About NV Organics

Promoting wellness and natural beauty, only the highest quality grape seed oils and extracts are used in all products and pure, milled grape seeds are used for body muds and scrubs. We supply the finest quality spa and personal care products which deliver the unique health benefits of grape seeds straight from sacred Indian lands, the vineyards of the Napa Valley.

Our grape seeds are uniquely milled to protect the nourishing properties of grape seed oil. Milling whole grape seeds provides the purest, most healthy grape seed oil. Our skin care products contain a truly cold pressed grape seed oil.

Grape Seed Benefits

Research clearly confirms the benefit of red wine grape seeds as a powerful antioxidant. They contain high levels of vitamins E and C and the benefits are highly touted in vitamin industry products and publications.
