WHY, a Dream Yacht

September 04, 2013

Share this content.

  • email
  • facebook
  • twitter
  • google+
  • pinterest

Wouldnt it be marvellous to live on an island, without pressure and completely free? WHY , created by Wally Herms Yachts , is the spacious and peaceful yacht inspired by the island of our dreams.



WHYs luxurious architecture blends perfectly with the environment. No detail is superfluous. This unique boat is equipped with solar panels that reduce energy consumption.




This imposing yacht costs between 60 and 100 million Euro, depending on the amenities selected. With an impressive living area of 3,400 m 2 , WHY can accommodate some thirty people, allowing for a sea of possibilities, one more exclusive than the next. Its three decks include a steam bath, a sauna, a gym, a dining room, a library, a massage area, a bar, and a media room: plenty of space for relaxation and fun!
