    The Whos Who of UK Banknotes

    Published 15 January 2014 by Linzi Nuttall

    Tweet

    You probably see them every day, but can you put names to the faces printed on your banknotes? These people are among the most important figures in British history but many of us only recognise a couple of them.

    For example, did you know that Elizabeth Fry (5 note) led a campaign that helped to transform prisons? Or that Adam Smith (20) helped revolutionise economics and is regarded by some as the first free-market capitalist?

    We all know the importance of paying attention to our money, carefully budgeting and only spending what we can afford. However, it seems that despite all the attention we give to money management, were not very attentive when it comes to the actual cash in our wallets and the mini history lessons these notes contain.
