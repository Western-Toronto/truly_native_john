11 Things a Woman Should Never do for a Man ...

Holly Riordan 23 comments 1.1k likes

In order to live a fulfilled life, there are things a woman should never do for a man. Love is powerful, but it shouldnt dictate your every move. Its important to care about others, but you shouldnt sacrifice your happiness for someone elses. If you want to live comfortably, here are a few things a #woman should never do for a man.

1. Give up Her Dreams

If youve always wanted to be a teacher, dont let him talk you out of it. You shouldnt have to choose between your man or your dreams. If you love one another, you should be able to find a way to make #things work. If you decide you no longer want to follow a certain career path, thats fine. But one of the things a woman should never do for a #man is let go of her dreams in order to be with him.
