Lovers of Light Instagram competition: win an exciting trip to New York

Weve been running the Lovers of Light photography competition for three years now, and continue to be blown away by the incredibly high standard of entries.

This year, we want to get social snappers involved, and as an incentive were offering a fantastic prize to our Instagram winner: a guided photo safari of the ridiculously photogenic New York City.

Exploring the city through the eyes of a lens, the winner and their guest will be guided around some of New Yorks most striking and unusual landmarks. This all-expenses-paid trip includes three nights accommodation in the four-star Row NYC Hotel in Manhattan.

First stop on your tour is the New York High Line, which offers uninterrupted views of the city from an elevated park transformed from a 30ft-high disused train line. Next up is the Urban Theatre, where youll watch New York life go by through a giant glass window. Of course, no trip to New York would be complete without visiting the Empire State Building; from 1,224ft, youll see NYC in a whole new light.

So get your phone out and start snapping to reserve yourself a seat on that plane
