10 Fastest Diets to Lose Weight

10 Fastest Diets to Lose Weight

Many people have complaints about excess weight. Having a fit body makes the person more attractive for the other people around. Weight problems become more important for the person especially during summer months. In such case, you should try to lose weight as soon as possible while keeping the saying everything has a cure in mind. With different diets, you may put the weight problem out of sight however, going on every diet that you see may give you trouble rather than benefit; so consulting an expert before going on a diet is important for your health.

Some diets have been tried by a lot of people and have been satisfactory. These diets also helped people lose weight faster . We have made a research and prepared this article about 10 most tried and benefited diets for you:

1) Alkaline Diet

With 14 kg weight loss in 2 months, alkali diet requires eating alkaline-rich nutrients more with respect to acidic nutrients. In this diet, nutrients are determined with respect to its PH values and high PH valued nutrients should be avoided. For example, in alkaline diet, we should stay away from nutrients like convenience food, refined meat, baked goods and foods with saturated fat.

What Are Alkaline Nutrients?

Main consumables of alkaline diet are all raw vegetables like cucumber, parsley etc. Besides this, nutrients like hazelnut, cider vinegar, pear and soy are also on this list.

How to Apply Alkaline Diet?

During alkaline diet, nutrients like milk, meat, caffeine and alcohol should be avoided while consuming fruit juice and dried fruits instead so that the PH value of the body can be maintained at a constant level.

2) Dukan Diet

Consisting of four stages, dukan diet is the secret of French women maintaining their slimness. With these 4 stages, the people who are on dukan diet can lose weight as if they are not on a diet at all.

Stage 1 of Dukan Diet

The other name of this stage is Attack stage of dukan diet and it provides fast weight loss by 72 different high-protein nutrients. People who want to lose weight fast should apply this diet for 5 days if they want to lose 10-20 kilograms and for 3 days if they want to lose less than 10 kilograms.

Stage 2 of Dukan Diet

In this stage, protein-rich alimentation with 28 different vegetable is procured so that fast weight loss can be maintained. You can consume vegetables as much as you want in this stage.

Stage 3 of Dukan Diet

This stage is also called as consolidation stage. In this stage, fruit consumption and carbohydrate intake are allowed as well as protein and vegetable consumption.

Stage 4 of Dukan Diet

This is the last step, and the people who were successful in the first 3 stages can eat and drink as they want because they managed to have their desired weight. This stage is the stability stage and maintaining the desired weight is ensured.

3) Ender Sara Diet

This diet, suggested by Ender Sara, can help you lose 5 kilograms in one week. During the first three days of this diet, you should consume only potato. Women, who apply Ender Sara diet completely, have nice results. However, a person who have gone on this diet must wait at least 6 months before going on it again. Because Ender Sara diet makes you burn 600 calories daily, on average.

7 days of Ender Sara Diet;

For the first three days, you should consume 2 boiled potatoes in the morning, 2 boiled potatoes on noontime and 3 boiled potatoes in the evening. On the fourth day, again 2 potatoes should be consumed on noontime and as difference, 2 oranges in the morning and 150 gram grilled chicken with salad in the evening should be consumed. On the fifth day, orange and apple should be consumed in the morning and at midday whereas beef or fish (150 g) with salad should be consumed in the evening. 6th day is the same with the 5th day. Finally, 2 oranges and 1 apple in the morning, potato salad and garden rocket salad at midday should be consumed on the 7th day.

4) Belly Fat Burning Diet

Although exercising is the most effective way of losing fat from belly, belly fat burning diets are ready to solve womens this kind of problems. There are some nutrients that exists in all belly fat burning diets. I want to mention highly effective nutrients about losing belly fat one by one:

Oat;

Choice of the breakfast, oat becomes tumid in your stomach, gives you the feeling of fullness and helps you burn belly fat.

Almond, Hazelnut, Walnut;

Dried fruits like almond, hazelnut and walnut are very effective on giving the feeling of fullness and making the belly thin. You may get into the habit of consuming dried fruits as snack.

Olive oil;

You should compensate your oil requirement with olive oil, which keeps cholesterol under control.

Legumes;

You should add legumes in your diet list as they have high protein and fiber content and they are low calorie.

Strawberry and Raspberry;

There is high amount of fiber in red fruits like strawberry and raspberry. Consuming these fruits naturally will help you greatly for losing belly fat.

5) Pregnancy Diet

Our first suggestion for the women who want to be slim during and after the pregnancy period is standing clear of sugar. Sugar consumption has no benefit both for you and your baby. Pregnant women must be extremely careful about their refreshments. Mothers who eat well in their refreshments will sit the dinner table full and so they will not sleep as gorged. Women who want to go on pregnancy diet must increase the amount of water they consume. Because the aim is keeping the fitness while giving no harm to the baby during pregnancy. As digestion rate increases with water consumption, keeping the fitness becomes much easier.

Women who are in pregnancy diet should stay away from fatty foods. Salt and baked goods are the other foods that you should stay away from during pregnancy period. Breakfast is very important in pregnancy diet. Pregnant women should consume milk and cheese together with food stuff like tomato and cucumber in their breakfast.

Women who are on a diet during pregnancy period should consume green tea instead of black tea. As green tea is iron-rich and it helps losing weight, it should be preferred during pregnancy period.

6) Sverige Diet

This diet is very popular in our country recently and it helps us lose weight fast. As this diet has altering and permanent effects on metabolism, it keeps your calorie intake at low levels for a few years.

Who Should Go On Sverige Diet?

First of all, sverige diet should not be applied by people who are over the age of 35 and have health problems. Except this, it can be applied by people who want to lose weight and or by people who need to lose weight fast before a surgery.

How to Apply Sverige Diet;

People who are on this diet should drink 2 liters of water daily. This diet should not continue more than 13 days and after it is completed, it can be repeated after 3 months. During this diet, no beverage including tea and coffee should be consumed. People who are on sverige diet and have high cholesterol problem can consume the white of an egg during their diet. As sticking to this diet is very hard, people who cant stand may give up on the sixth day.

7) Karatay Diet

Created by Prof. Dr. Canan Karatay , this karatay diet offers advantages and easiness with respect to the other diets and make it possible to lose weight as well. Lets mention some features of karatay diet:

1-) Kebab, Lahmacun* and Dried Fruits Make You Slim;

As lahmacun is thin, unfermented and it contains different vegetables in it, it is a weight loss food according to karatay diet. Kebab helps losing weight when it is consumed without bread and legume can make you lose weight as they contain carbohydrates.

* Very thin Turkish pizza covered with seasoned minced meat and onions

2-) Egg and Meat Dont Make You Fat;

As egg is hard to digest and meat of the animals, which were fed with natural foods, does not increase cholesterol, they dont make you fat according to karatay diet.

3-) Being Slim Is Possible with Olive Oil and Butter

As these are rich in omega-3 and omega-6, they should definitely be consumed, according to karatay diet.

4-) Regular Walkers Can Eat As They Wish

According to karatay diet, people who walks 5 kilometers daily can eat as they wish; because they burn everything they eat by such regular walking.

8) Ketogenic Diet

Diet type in which most of the calorie intake of our body is supplied by fats instead of carbohydrates is called as ketogenic diet . In this diet type, consumption of sugar and starch-containing foods is strictly restricted and protein consumption is kept at low level.

What Are The Advantages Of Ketogenic Diet?

Human body use fat and carbohydrate to produce energy and more energy can be obtained from fat. When fat is digested, ketones are formed. As ketones are used to produce energy during the ketogenic diet, inflammation in our body decreases and our immune system gets stronger. Ketogenic diet is applied to treat Alzheimers disease patients, to decrease Parkinson patients trembling and keeping blood sugar under control for diabetic people.

Nutrients in Ketogenic Diet

During ketogenic diet, foods like fatty fish types, meat, butter, peanut and egg are used. Additionally, low-carbohydrate containing foods like mushroom, spinach and green vegetables are frequently consumed in this diet.

9) Fat Burning Diet

People who want to go on fat burning diet should keep on exercising in order not to lose muscle while burning fat and they only need to add one more thing to their diet list that they apply regularly. Our first suggestion to burn fat is squeezing a lemon in a glass of water and drink it just after waking up. Additionally, lemon and olive oil should be added to salads.

People on fat burning diet must pay attention on what they eat during the day. They should consume smaller portions, keep away from high-calorie fried foods, concentrate on consuming green vegetables and prefer fruits containing fat-burning enzymes like pineapple. You should concentrate on exercising also in order to lose weight. Exercising helps you a lot about losing weight. When you crave for sweet food, you can fulfill your need in a healthy way by adding sugar to yoghurt.

10) eyda okun Diet

Diet programs that are applied without care and cause rapid weight loss threat your health seriously. However, with eyda cokun diet, you can lose weight in a healthy way in contrast to the other diets. The secret of 6-day eyda cokun diet is exercising consciously and alimentation in the right way. During this diet, at least 1 hour of walking is required in a day.

Day 1

On the first day, 3 tablespoon of buckwheat is boiled sharp and mixed with peeled and grated apple and cinnamon and this mixture is consumed in three meals in the day.

Day 2

In the morning, omelet prepared with 1 egg white and mushroom is consumed. Grilled fish at lunch and grilled turkey meat at dinner should be consumed.

Day 3

Detox salad should be prepared in the morning. To do so, apple, lemon, parsley and ginger are mixed. At lunch, a special soup should be consumed. This soup should be prepared by using lentil, dill, artichoke, onion, zucchini and beet. This soup should also be consumed at dinner.

Day 4

Tomato soup in the morning, kebab roll at lunch, gumbo and green salad at dinner should be consumed.

Day 5

Fruit salad in the morning, zucchini salad at lunch and roasted aubergine over lettuce at dinner should be consumed.

Day 6

In the morning, spicy Turkish omelet; at lunch, stuffed chard and at dinner, gumbo food with salad should be consumed.
