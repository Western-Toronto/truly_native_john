Wordgram is a mobile app with unique features:


creative

Create and edit your own translations, as many as you like. Learn them later.

patient

Repetition is the mother of study. Repeat the words from your vocabulary set, as many times as needed.

supportive

Wordgram tracks your progress and provides beautiful reports with your performance.

intelligent

Wordgram builds on Pimsleur and Leitner methods, which are very effective for language learning.

reliable

Wordgram won't forget words that you've learned so far, so you'll be able to continuously improve and build on that.

talkative

Wordgram is able to speak in many languages, to help you with pronunciation. It can also recognize your voice.

unforgettable

Wordgram can set automatic reminders, so that you can learn a little each day.

readable

Use built-in ebook reader, translate words you don't know, and learn gradually while reading.

film-loving

Watch foreign movies with understanding. Wordgram automatically creates a list of words you should know.

Wordgram browser extensions allow you to learn languages, while surfing the web. On a web page, double-click the word you don't know, then click on the Wordgram icon. If you want, you can use automatic translations, or enter your own phrases. All words added via the browser extension will be available on your mobile device.

Our vision

We believe that learning a foreign language is like going on an adventure. It's an experience that can open your mind to other cultures, other people and other ways of life. A language can broaden your horizon, and influence the way you think. Our goal is to make people more open-minded and in a way, like-minded. Our purpose is to become lifelong learners. Our dream is for people to understand each other. Once they see their thoughts, there will be nothing left to do than to share the words.
