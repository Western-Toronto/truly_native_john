How to Use Airplay and Bluetooth to Stream to Your TV and Stereo

By Erik Caso 3 Comments
Home / Mobile / How to Use Airplay and Bluetooth to Stream to Your TV and Stereo
Rate this post

Were pretty happy that because of younity we can take all of our files and media with us anywhere in the world without having to sync or store them in some companys cloud; we imagine you feel the same way. However, we wanted to share with you one of our favorite things to do with younity stream that media right to our TV or stereo. If youre like us, you often find yourself sharing your media with people around you or wanting to immerse yourself in a movie or music playlist. Because younity integrates Bluetooth and Apples AirPlay, you can easily push the music and videos you stream to younity right to your TV, stereo or other AirPlay-enabled or Bluetooth-enabled device.

Youll see we often have contests and giveaways where we give people an Apple TV. We love the little device because it is the easiest way we know of to stream movies and music to our home theater. We also find Bluetooth products like our Polk Audio Woodbourne to be great devices for high-quality audio streaming. In these cases, we get the benefit of serious audio and video quality without the cords or having to cart around the endless amounts of media spread across our computers.

If you were wondering how to use AirPlay or Bluetooth, accessing both is easy. Simply start playing music or video and if an AirPlay-enabled device (or Bluetooth-enabled device for audio) is nearby you will see the small streaming icon in the lower right of the younity UI (see image to left). Tap that icon and youll see a list of all devices nearby that can accept a stream using AirPlay or Bluetooth from younity (see image to the right). This applies to a lot of cars these days too, so if your car stereo is Bluetooth-enabled it means younity lets youplay your whole music library while on the road!

We hope this helps you learn new ways to access and share your media with those around you. Were always on the lookout for new technologies like AirPlay and Bluetooth to improve your ability to access your media. If there are others out there you think we should integrate with, please suggest them to us on our support site where you can suggest new features or vote on existing suggestions.

Otherwise, happy streaming from Team younity.
