Get Baby Chic with a Modern Nursery Design

Date: 15 Jan 2014
By: Kelly Warren
Tags: modern design , modern nursery
Comment: 0

Having a baby is one of the biggest moments of your life, and you want your little boy or girl to have a modern, luxurious nursery that they can grow up in. Decorating the nursery has become just as important as updating the rest of your home, and there are some great things to keep in mind to give your new baby a chic, modern nursery everyone will love.

First, remember that simplicity is key. You want to make sure that you have everything you need to keep yourself and your baby comfortable in the room before you start worrying about adding in extra items. Go for a crib, changing table, comfy recliner for rocking the baby to sleep, and any other items that you absolutely need, first. Then, if there are some decorations you want to add on, go for it!

In the past, the color palette for a babys room has been determined a lot by the gender of the baby. These days, keeping the nursery gender neutral is a more popular way to go. This way, the room can still be used if you have another baby in the future, regardless of the gender. It also allows for easier decorating overall. Adding in pops of color throughout the room is a great way to bring more personality while still going with a gender-neutral pallet.

If you want to go for a modern look, another good idea is to avoid a themed room. By doing this, the room can still be relevant for future children and it will be a lot easier to find different decorations. It also makes it easier for the child to grow with the room if you go with a childish theme, he or she might want to change it after a certain age. Similarly, try to choose items that you can use long-term. Dont be afraid of spending a bit more money to make an investment that will last, like a crib that can later be converted into a bed. This way you are maximizing the money you are spending by creating long lasting value.

Finally, turn to paint to customize the room. It is much easier to purchase bedding and dcor that you like first, then finding the right paint color that matches what you have purchased rather than choosing the paint color and trying to find dcor you love after. Dont be afraid to go with a fun accent wall or stenciling.

Creating a chic, modern nursery doesnt have to be hard or expensive! Keep these tips in mind and your baby will sleep soundly in style.
