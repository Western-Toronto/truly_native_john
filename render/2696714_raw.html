The 2014 Ford Escape. The vehicle of choice for any good #escapeartist.

Even though we live for Mondays, it doesnt mean we cant get the most of our weekends. Theyre for excitement, exploration, and experiencing new things. We work our behinds off so we can enjoy our time off; and the 2014 Ford Escape is designed to help us get the most of our free time.

From getting out of town for a weekend escape, to getting around town for work, the Ford Escape is equipped with versatile performance features for weekend warriors. The available foot-activated liftgate 1 lets you open the trunk by kicking your foot under the rear bumper. The available Intelligent AWD gives you the confidence to go almost anywhere from rain-slick streets to snow-covered roads. Cargo to go? Fold down one or both sections of the second-row seat for flat-fold storage and fit everything you might need for a great weekend at the cottage.

With hands on the wheel and eyes on the road, the available SYNC with MyFord Touch 2 uses wireless Bluetooth 3 technology to let you make calls, control your music, adjust the climate and use the available navigation system with voice commands. And if youre driving up to the cottage during the evening, the Ford Escape Titanium has available HID headlamps that illuminate the road ahead.

A true #escapeartist does more than just get away; they get away in style. Book a Ford Escape test drive here
