The Social Media War Continues Between Ellen DeGeneres and Portia De Rossi

Oh how we love it when a couples high jinks play out through their social media pages. Especially when that couple is lesbian idols, Ellen DeGeneres and Portia De Rossi .

First it was Ellen posting a video of Portia bending and snappingto a vintage Jane Fonda work out tape.

. @PortiadeRossi said I could watch her do her @JaneFonda workout. She didnt say anything about filming & tweeting it http://t.co/9d30nps3pN

Ellen DeGeneres (@TheEllenShow) January 4, 2015

However, if Ellen thought she could pull a playful prank on Portia without getting a little reprisal in return, she was wrong.

Portia was quickly on the attack, tweeting a video of Ellen singing while running goPortia!

I got you back @theellenshow ! Dont believe me? Just watch. https://t.co/ERQqZbYrUz Portia de Rossi (@portiaderossi) January 5, 2015

The winner? Everyone watching. Heres hoping the pranks continue, and they keep sharing the results.
