What Your Coffee Says About Your Personality

Youve heard it said that you are what you eat, but I tell you that you are what you drink. At least you are what kind of coffee you drink. While there may not be any scientific data to support this fun topic the type off coffee you drink may actually describe who you really are as a person. For example if you like Iced Coffee you might be assertive and outspoken. On the other hand if you like Espresso you are likely to be friendly and adaptive.
