Mirrors & Music





I love it when everyday objects are made with an eye towards utility and design. Take headphones - my friend Sami recently wrote about them taking street style by storm (when Sami's right, she's so right ). These Flips function as regular headphones, then rotate outwards to become speakers - talk about fashion and functionality!
