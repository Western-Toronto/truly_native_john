There are a few destinations that hold a special place in my heart. Los Cabos is one of them. Cabo was one of the first international destinations I visited as a young college student with my husband. Since then, it has served as an easy beachdestination for a long romantic weekend or fun with friends.

Last fall, I was heartbroken to see the video and reports of Hurricane Odile and the resulting damageto manyCabo hotels . I couldnt help but feel forthe hotels that were wiped out and closed. I empathized with hotel staffleft withoutjobs,and all of the local families that were impacted. I wanted to do something. So I went back.

After this most recent visit to Cabo just a few short months from the stormCabo is back in business and looking better than ever. Storefronts look cleaner. Beaches are filled with soft sand. Great beachfront hangouts, such as The Office, have rebuilt. Many of the hotels hit hard by the storm have reopened, and are rolling out red carpets for guests.
