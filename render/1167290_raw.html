Does Your Home Security Strategy Include A Worst Case Scenario Like An Electromagnetic Pulse Attack?

Unless you are seriously preparing now for this type of a catastrophic event, you and your family would not be able to survive an EMT electromagnetic pulse attack.

To help you understand how close to a reality this may be, just read this article that appeared July 31, 2014, on Forbes.com titled Failure to Protect U.S. Against Electromagnetic Pulse Threat Could Make 9/11 Look TrivialSomeday written by Peter Kelly-Detwiler. It provides a chilling scenario of what we may be facing in the future. In the article, he discusses the impact of an EMP assault and why our entire electric power grid is at risk. He also outlines possible ways the attack might occur and who would most likely be behind the assault.

A thoughtful home security strategy must include emergency preparedness for a worst case scenario , like an EMP .

Have you considered what you would do if something catastrophic occurred in your community? Consider the possibilities if your community was hit by a devastating storm, a massive earthquake, or a terrorist attack, leaving you unable to access the basics needed for survival.

How would you survive if the power grid was destroyed by an electromagnetic pulse attack , causing all electricity and power to cease for months or even years throughout the United States?

What would you do if your water supply was cut off?

How long could you and your family survive without food?

What would you do for cash if all of the banks were closed and every single ATM did not work?

If there is no power, how would you fill your car with gasoline if the pumps were shut down?

While these examples may seem severe, they represent a worst case scenario, and that is something that has become more probable today than ever before in our technology driven society.

It is far better to be prepared for a worst case scenario than to be blinded by wishful thinking.

Perhaps the most challenging problem we will face in a worst case scenario is our ability to survive. Food and water become the first priority. If all supermarkets and stores are unable to operate because of a total collapse of our power grid, and there is a complete shut down of transportation, where will your food come from? How will you get water if all power and utility plants cannot function because their entire computerized infrastructure was destroyed?

It sounds scary, but this is a reality that we may face one day, especially since terrorists with the financing and means to carry out such an attack present a real threat to our country.
