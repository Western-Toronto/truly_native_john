Built from game used composite hockey sticks, Original Stix phone cases were engineered with hockey players and fans in mind. In a world of mass-produced, generic phone cases, Original Stix combines authentic styling and attention to detail to deliver a unique piece of the game. We teamed up with some of Detroit's most talented automotive engineers to design a product that exceeds even the highest quality standards. Starting with the iPhone 5 and 5s in Spring 2014, our mission is to bring this concept to a number of popular electronic accessories and authentic apparel. All Original Stix products are manufactured and assembled in Detroit, Michigan...we appreciate your support of American made products and thank you for being a fan of Original Stix.

Motor City Made Rugged TPU Frame Endless Possibilities. Easy Access.
Designed by former auto industry engineers, each case is hand crafted with the highest levels of quality in Detroit, Michigan. Our impact-resistant TPU frame was built tough to resist scuffs and bumps, while the raised bevel helps prevent scratches on the screen. Interchangeable design allows you to swap out your sticks to create a truly one-of-a-kind case. Seamless, one-piece frame allows for easy access to all ports and buttons.
