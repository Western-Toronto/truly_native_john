What is the Garrahand?


A melodic, electro-acoustic percussion instrument that you can intuitively play with your hands .

A lightweight drum you can connect with loopers and FX pedals through its patent-pending isolated microphone to create inimitable sounds.

Thanks to its proprietary tuning system and its 7 tongues (14 combining 2 drums) you can set up the Garrahand to virtually any scale and perform over any style of music.

Utilized by musicians performing with mega-stars like Sting, Shakira, Bjrk, and Peter Gabriel , more than 2000 units have been sold since 2007 .

New 2014 model

Easier tuning, more color

  • NEW: Red Wine and Purple colors.
  • NEW: easy access to the tuning mechanism - no screws to unmount.
  • Proprietary tuning mechanism. You can build almost any scale/mode you want within 2+ octaves of range.
  • Patent pending microphone technology captures only the pure sound of the tongues and isolates it from all other sources. Sounds great unplugged too!
  • XLR balanced and 1/4 jack unbalanced outputs - direct box circuitry included within the intrument.
  • Panasonic and Neutrik Japanese-made electronics.
  • Volume control.
  • Cymbal holder included: it can be held by any standard cymbal holder for use within a drum set.
  • Minimal size and weight.
  • Adjustable internal damper to modify the timbre of your instrument.
  • 2 year international limited-warranty
