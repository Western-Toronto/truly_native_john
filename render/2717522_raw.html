What are LeanPops?

LeanPops are popsicle molds made out of BPA free food grade silicone. They are extremely durable, flexible, mess free and easy to clean. There is an endless amount of recipes you can use to make delicious homemade popsicles. Mix, freeze, eat, and repeat.

What do I need to make LeanPops?

There is no special equipment required to make LeanPops, but we strongly suggest using a mixer or blender to make things easier. We recommend the Magic Bullet because its cheap, easy to use, and highly effective.
