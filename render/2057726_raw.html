What is a VPN?

A VPN is used to mask your IP address and create a secure connection between you and the internet. Think of it as a middleman, it will protect your identity and encrypt all traffic.

Why use a VPN?

A VPN can be used for many different things. The most popular reasons being to unblock sites like Hulu Plus and Netflix. It'll also encrypt your traffic so if you're using public internet people won't be looking at your stuff!
