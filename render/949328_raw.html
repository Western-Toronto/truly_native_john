U.S. News & World Report's #1 Ranked Online MBA Program, from the University of North Carolina Kenan-Flagler Business School

In carrying on UNC Kenan-Flagler's tradition of innovation, leadership and academic excellence, MBA@UNC blends the flexibility of online learning with the rigor and quality of a world-class business education. Students develop the strategic insights to lead top organizations, network face-to-face with accomplished business leaders and earn the same degree as students who attend on campus.

Why UNC Kenan-Flagler

At UNC Kenan-Flagler Business School, we take great pride in preparing students to become well-rounded, well-connected business leaders. Learn how our core values have helped us build one of the world's best business schools.

Academics

MBA@UNC classes and coursework are designed by our award-winning faculty and delivered in a flexible online format. Students attend weekly online classes and can complete coursework from anywhere.

Admissions

MBA@UNC students are held to the same elite admissions standards as our on-campus students. Class cohorts begin four times per year, allowing our applicants to get their education underway as soon as they are ready.

Career Management

The UNC Kenan-Flagler community is committed to helping students drive positive career outcomes. Learn more about the array of personalized career development services and leadership training resources available.

Meet Rahim Walker,an MBA@UNC Student

As a working professional who is balancing a career, family and a top-tier business education, Rahim Walker embodies the spirit of ambition that connects our student community. Learn how Rahim is getting a world-class MBA experience without sacrificing his commitments.
