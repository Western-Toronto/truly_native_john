The $16,000 Small Business Surprise

Post Date: September 30, 2013
Share:

Bill Rancic and Intuit paid a surprise visit to one unsuspecting small business as part of the Small Business Big Game program.

Rancic, the season one winner of The Apprentice and an entrepreneur, visited Elevenzees , a Chicagoboutique, and dropped a big surprise on its owner:Intuit would buymore than$16,000 in merchandise.

Its challenging to be a small business, but we have a good time, said store owner Patty Johnson. This is like a blessing from above.

Check out the video to see Rancics visit andJohsonsreaction. Feel free to share it on your social networks.

What is Small Business Big Game?

Small Business Big Game shines a light on small businesses and lets them know they are important to the economy. As part of the program, Intuit will give away more than $25 million in prizes, including a commercial in footballs biggest game on Feb. 4. Small businesses entered for a chance to win by sharing their story and basic business information.

Get Involved Today

Nearly 15,000 small businesses submitted their stories for a chance to win. Now they need your help to move on to the next round.

Check out small business entries between now and Oct. 13 and vote for your favorites. Visitors can vote once per entry per day;more than1.2 million votes have been cast to date. And no, Elevenzees isnt eligible.
