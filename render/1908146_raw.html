Top business hotels in Mauritius

When conference season comes around, rather than opting for an ordinary gathering in an ordinary venue, planners are increasingly looking to organise events which offer delegates an all-round experience with bragging rights. And for something like this, top business hotels in Mauritius certainly fit the bill. For what could be better than ending a hard days work with a well-deserved dip in the Indian Ocean?

Holiday Inn Mauritius Airport

Several hotels in Mauritius can accommodate conferences of all styles and sizes. Holiday Inn Mauritius Airport offers a range of meeting rooms which couldnt be more conveniently placed for visitors, with space for between 30 350 attendees in various room layouts. The great southeast location near to the town of Mahebourg puts delegates close to great dining, shopping and the aquatic delights of Blue Bay Marine Park, too.

Hennessy Park Hotel

Up in the information technology hub city of Ebene, Hennessy Park Hotel is a contemporary four-star choice for business travellers, with four conference and meeting rooms available including the large, multifunctional Ebony Ballroom. This 381 square metre space accommodates up to 400 guests and is suitable for a broad range of uses, from standard conference requirements to product launches, cocktail parties, award ceremonies and gala meals. The hotel also runs its own highly-anticipated events, parties and art evenings ensuring theres always plenty of pleasure to mix with work.

Labourdonnais Waterfront Hotel

Labourdonnais Waterfront Hotel is a premier five-star business property on the Caudan Waterfront in Port Louis the only such property in Mauritius and as to be expected at the high-end of the scale, it exudes sophistication and elegance.

Catering for both small group meetings and large corporate events, Labourdonnais has a capacity of up to 225 delegates, with five different conference and meeting rooms available. Theres also a health centre and wellness spa on site, plus all the retail and dining delights of Caudan Waterfront on the doorstep.

Le Suffren Hotel & Marina

Also on the Caudan Waterfront is the recently refurbished four-star Le Suffren Hotel & Marina , whose 12-person meeting room is great for smaller business get-togethers likeboard meetings or team-building sessions and if you need more room, all the facilities at the Labourdonnais are easily accessible 5 minutes away via the hotels direct boat shuttle service.

LAddresse

The newly refurbished LAddresse boutique hotel is situated on the river, at the heart of the village of Port Chambly, 10 minutes north of Port Louis. Contemporary design and a cosmopolitan feel, LAddresse is offering a fresh take on doing business in Mauritius. There are two boardrooms for 8 people each, and a 50-person conference room. These meeting rooms can be rented by the hour, half day or full day, while corporate lunches can be organised at the Mediterranean or Japanese restaurant. And when the meetings are over, Grand Baie is just 20 minutes north, there are boating excursions available, and a fully equipped gym and spa.
