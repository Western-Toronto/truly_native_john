Top ten actors who turned down epic movie roles

By Plato P. Jul 29, 2014 Celebrities , Cool Facts , Fun Facts , Human , Movie Facts 2 Comments
0 0

People doesnt always make wise decisions and when it comes to the Hollywood these decisions may end up making them regret a lot about the decision. In this article we will see the top ten similar turn downs by actors from Hollywood.

1. Jim Carrey as the Captain Jack Sparrow

Jim Carrey is definitely one of the best comedians from Hollywood and I simply love all his movies. But did you know he was actually considered for the roll of Jack Sparrow in Pirates of the Caribbean: The Curse of the Black Pearl? It would certainly have been awesome to see Jim Carrey in the pirate boots of Jack Sparrow, but I guess Johnny Depp proved to be perfect for the movie, he really made this movie to stand out from the crowd with his acting.

Jim Carrey turned it down because of the scheduling conflicts he might have had with Bruce Almighty if he had chosen to act in Pirates of Caribbean. Considering Bruce Almighty was a huge success I dont think Jim Carrey have any regrets about this.

2. Nicolas Cage as Aragorn in Lord of the rings: The Fellowship of the Ring

Nicolas Cage was considered for the role of Aragorn in the movie Lord of the rings: The Fellowship of the Ring, but declined due to family obligations. This role was offered to quite a number of people who either turned it down or not selected before the role landed on Mortensen. Do you think it would have been better if Nicolas Cage accepted this offer?

3. Clint Eastwood as James Bond

After Sean Connery declined the role of James Bond saying he dont want to play the same role again, the team considered Clint Eastwood for it. But Clint Eastwood declined it saying he believed the character should be played by an English actor. But I think it would have been awesome to see Eastwood playing as James Bond with a little American touch. Let us know what do you think by commenting below.

4. Sean Connery as the Gandalf in Lord of the Rings

Sean Connery turned down the role of the Gandalf in the movie Lord of the Rings. Reports say he was offered up to 15% of the worldwide box office receipts to play Gandalf. This one decision lost him around $400 million.

5. Sean Connery as a Manchester United player

Sean Connery have been a keen soccer player in his early days and used to play for local teams. After one similar match the manager of Manchester United who saw the match offered him a spot in the Manchester United team. Sean Connery was 23 at the time and he declined the gig for pursuing acting career. He later said this turned out to become one of the most intelligent decisions taken by him.

6. Jennifer Aniston as a featured player on Saturday Night Live

One of of my all time favorite sitcoms are FRIENDS and I loved the character Rachel played by Jennifer Aniston. She had declined the offer as a featured player on SNL for FRIENDS. I guess Anitson doesnt have any regrets by choosing the role of Rachel over SNL featured player spot.

7. Arnold Schwarzenegger as John McClane

Well, not actually. Schwarzenegger was offered a role in the sequel of Commando which he turned down. So the team behind the project reworked on the project and came up with a standalone movie idea and named it Die Hard. The role went to Bruce Willis. Die Hard sure is a great action movie and I dont know if I would have liked the sequel of the movie Commando that much, what about you?

8. Liam Neeson as James Bond

Liam Neeson was considered for the role of James Bond in Golden Eye but turned it down because he didnt want to act in action movies at that time. Considering he is acting in action movies nowadays, I guess he regret the decision of not choosing the role of James Bond.

9. Will Smith as Django in Django Unchained

Will Smith was offered the role of Django in the movie Django Unchained but declined because Django wasnt the lead. Jamie Foxx did the role perfectly but it would have been awesome to see Will Smith in this role for sure.

10. Will Smith as Neo in The Matrix

Will Smith was actually considered for the role of New on the movie The Matrix. Later he said he was not mature enough as an actor at that time, and that if given the role, he would have messed it up. He turned the movie down for acting in Wild Wild West.

So thats the top 10 turn downs in the history of Hollywood in our list. Have something to add, let us know by commenting below!
