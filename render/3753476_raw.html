#MEETBOLD with Movado

12.09.2014

I love a dose of gorgeous gold & diamonds to dress up any look (what girl doesn't?!) Naturally, I was thrilled when Movado asked me to create a look inspired by their Movado Diamond Mini watch. The design is ultra-feminin yet modern with 54 beautiful diamonds around the face with yellow-gold bracelet links, polished to an elegant finish. I loved the idea of pairing the watch with a bold midi-skirt and a classic black sweater. The look, like the watch is girly yet modern, a perfect Holiday look that stands out from cocktail dresses and sequins at any holiday fete. The watch is incredibly versatile transitioning perfectly from day to night. My favorite part of the watch is the single dot at 12 o'clock that symbolizes the sun at high noon. To echo the beauty of the watch, I shot this outfit at my favorite New York sanctuary - the Hudson River Path. Despite the overcast weather, the beauty of nature and city come together as hazy lights bring enliven the city. Discover the entire Movado Bold collection here .
Special thanks to Movado for sponsoring this post!
