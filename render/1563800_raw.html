Chloes Style Challenge: Christmas Day Lunch with the Family

Even that big fluffy hood cant hide Chloes lovely eyes

Christmas is coming so weve challenged our favourite international dressage rider and model, Chloe Vell to find a fashionable outfit for Christmas Day lunch with her family for a maximum budget of 100 . Chloe did a fab job, s pending just 94.92 on these five gorgeous items that will carry her beyond the festive feeding frenzy, right into new year.

Heres how Chloe stamped her equestrian mark on Christmas day

Chloes Christmas Lunch Outfit

This is my favourite time of year where I can spend quality time with my family and reflect on the year thats passed Chloe

Fluffy Coat: I have been looking for a coat like this. It is both glamorous and functional. I love the fur style and it is just right for Christmas Day. It definitely gets me in the mood for Christmas and the winter weather.

Faux Fur Hooded Coat 37 (was 49.99) at New Look

Faux Fur Hooded Coat 37 at New Look

Horsey Sweater: A definite time to wear your Christmas jumper! This is my equestrian version of festive knitwear. It is easy to wear and simple to dress up or down.

Horse Print Pattern Jumper 8.95 at Amazon

Horse Print Pattern Jumper 8.95 at Amazon

Flirty Skirt: I love pleated skirts as it is a change from the normal pencil skirt. This one is very flattering and has an elasticated waist, leaving room for me to indulge in Christmas lunch and all the trimmings.

Pleated Skirt 19.99 at H&M

Pleated Skirt 19.99 at H&M

Hi-Top Trainers: I love trainers because they are trending right now! I especially like these as they are more elegant but still just as comfortable as standard trainers.

High Heel Lace Up Wedge Trainers 12.99 (were 19.99) at Amazon

Hi-Top Wedge Heel Trainers 12.99 at Amazon

Beautiful Big Bag: I can never have enough bags and this horsey bag can fit all my essentials inside such as my tablet, phone and accessories. Plus a few gifts for the family!
