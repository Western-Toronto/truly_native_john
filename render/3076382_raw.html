How SPFy are you?

You're probably well aware that SPF (Sun Protection Factor) is the rating that's applied to sunscreen. The higher the rating, the better your skin is protected from the sun's damaging UV rays. With textiles, SPF becomes UPF , or Ultraviolet Protection Factor, and the same principle applies: the higher the rating, the better the sun protection. A sunscreen of SPF 30 has essentially the same protection as a shirt with UPF of 30 .

A standard white cotton t-shirt typically has a UPF rating of about 5 . Most people wouldn't bother slathering on a sunscreen with an SPF of only 5, in fact, most people typically use a sunscreen with an SPF of 30 or higher. The Skin Cancer Foundation states that: "UV radiation causes up to 90 percent of the visible changes commonly attributed to aging, such as wrinkles, brown spots, and sagging skin."

Skin damage from the sun is cumulative over your lifetime, so we want to encourage you to be as SPFy as you can when you're outside in the sun. Wearing your SummerSkin will help protect your skin from wrinkles, premature aging, and most importantly, skin cancer.

Our video above might be a little silly, but skin cancer is no joke. Skin Cancer is the most common type of cancer in the world, with over 3.5 million diagnoses every year , and increasing. Furthermore, melanoma is one of the deadliest forms of skin cancer:

  • EVERY 8 MINUTES one American is diagnosed with melanoma.
  • EVERY HOUR melanoma takes a life.
  • Melanoma is the most common cancer for 25-29 year-olds .
  • 1 in 5 Americans will be diagnosed with skin cancer in their lifetime.
  • A persons risk for melanoma doubles if he or she has had more than five sunburns.

We've spent years on fabric R&D and found the highest quality fabrics that provide unparalleled sun-protection with a luxurious feel , and no chemical treatments . SummerSkin's goal is to provide you with fashionable sun-protective clothing, that doesn't announce to the world that "HEY, I'M WEARING SUN-PROTECTIVE CLOTHING!"

The UPF of fabric is affected by the thickness of the fabric, the tightness of the weave, and the color . The trick is to balance the sun-protective qualities with the comfort and style of the fabric.

SummerSkin garments have the highest UPF rating possible, UPF 50+ , and are classified by the FTC as " excellent sun protection ," blocking over 98% of UV rays . We follow stringent industry guidelines and testing standards set forth by the American Society for Testing and Materials (ASTM), and the American Association of Textile Chemists and Colorists (AATCC).

Delicate fibers lend themselves well to blends for sun-protective clothing as they can be combined with other materials, such as cotton and spandex, to create a tightly woven fabric that is light-weight, breathable, and incredibly soft . Our blends are composed of bamboo viscose, spandex, cotton and tencel to achieve the highest possible UPF rating, with inherent sun-protective qualities without chemical treatments.

Most of our competitor's lines focus on rash guards for swimming or bulky cover-ups for your next safari exhibition , but SummerSkin stands alone as a line exclusively devoted to comfortable, luxurious clothing - tops, dresses, pants, and versatile scarves - that hang beautifully and feel incredible, providing UPF 50+ sun-protection, without sacrificing style.
