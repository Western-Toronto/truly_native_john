When it comes to retirement, it's normal to feel an array of emotions. You may be excited to embark on the next phase of your life, but also concerned about possible changes. Or, maybe you simply have questions about what's to come. At American Advisors Group, we make it our guiding purpose to serve our community of seniors to the best of our abilities. As such, we created this list of tools and resources to help streamline retirement and answer any questions you or your loved ones might have about the steps involved.

Read on for recommended tools related to long-term financial planning, budgeting, home improvement, and logistical procedures for baby boomers and retirees.

  • 401K

    Under a 401k, retirement savings contributions are provided and often matched by an employer. 401k contributions are deducted from your paycheck before taxation and limited to an annual contribution. 401k provide tax benefits now and allow for investment growth to be utilized during retirement.

  • IRA

    An IRA (Individual Retirement Plan) is a retirement investment option for individuals with tax advantages. Contributions are often tax-deductible for the year that contributions were made. IRA withdrawals during retirement are taxed.

  • ROT IRA

    A Roth IRA is a type of retirement plan where tax breaks are not realized during investment. A tax break is granted when the investment is withdrawn during retirement. Roth IRAs typically contain investments in common stocks and bonds or mutual funds.

  • ANNUITIES

    An annuity is usually when an individual pays a single premium to an insurance company that later distributes back funds over a certain amount of time. Annuities are usually guaranteed distributions over time until the death of the individual.

  • Reverse mortgage

    A reverse mortgage or Home Equity Conversion Mortgage (HECM) is a way to turn the equity locked in your home into cash without having to make any monthly mortgage payments. Companies like AAG provide reverse mortgages to retired individuals to allow them to receive cash payments for the equity in their home while also allowing them to stay in their home.

  • Social Security

    Social Security is a government run program that is provided to individuals in retirement. The amount an individual or couple receives depends on their contribution to the fund through their working years as well as the age at which they start receiving social security benefits. The average size of a social security check is $1,182.24 per month.

  • REAL ESTATE INVESTMENTS

    Selling your home or owning rental properties that have positive cash flow can be a great option for individuals in retirement. Additionally, the equity on the home can be used for access to cash through lines of credit.

  • SafelyFiled

    A resource that allows individuals a way to keep track of their digital and paper files in a single system. Created by two baby boomers, SafelyFiled is designed to help you keep important information and memories safe and organized.

    As we move to more electronic statements, the potential for you losing track of your assets increases, but seniors can prevent mistakes and streamline the process with SafelyFiled.

    Mark H. Snow,CEO, SafelyFiled.com, LLC

  • QuantumOnline.com

    An unbiased source for information on preferred stocks. QuantomOnline.com aims to make it as easy as possible for income investors to easily find opportunities.

    These are tools that teach and that help you build a portfolio.

    John Graves, Editor

  • Covestor

    Covestor allows you to find portfolio managers whose portfolios can be viewed, studied and mirrored. Find managers that invest with the same types of goals as you and mirror as much or as little of their activity as youd like.

    Covestor now offers more than 100 investment portfolios for Investment Retirement (IRA) accounts, making it a very flexible solution for people saving for retirement.

    Kalen Holliday, Communications Director for Covestor, an online marketplace for investing.

  • Homezada

    The day-to-day cost of living can add up quickly during retirement. Homezada helps retirees save money on home maintenance, renovation, and establishing home budgets.

    Homezada can manage multiple homes in one account, and keep an automated maintenance schedule and calendar, keep on online home inventory, and manage home improvement projects.

    John Bodrozic, Co-Founder, HomeZada

  • Self Directed IRAs and 401ks

    In addition to traditional IRAs and employer sponsored 401ks, self directed IRAs and 401ks allow you to have more control over how and where your retirement savings are directed.

    A self-directed retirement account is an excellent tool for building a more eclectic (and potentially more lucrative) retirement portfolio.

    Ms. Jaime J. Raskulinecz, CPM, CEO at Next Generation TS, LLC

  • Social Security Benefit Maximization Calculator

    This tool is designed for individuals and couples that have not yet claimed social security. It helps determine the best time for you to start receiving social security benefits.

    When it comes to retirement it is quite simple. Take personal responsibility for your retirement.

    Thomas Scanlon, CPA, CFP, Financial Advisor, Raymond James

  • Annuity & Retirement Calculators

    The Indexed Annuity Leadership Council provides a collection of annuity and other retirement calculators to let you determine how your finances will hold up during retirement.

    The Indexed Annuity Leadership Council at www.FIAinsights.org has interactive calculators that can help people decide how much they should save for retirement.

    Jim Poolman, Executive Director of Indexed Annuity Leadership Council

  • FutureAdvisor

    This tool provides a free service that analyzes your taxable and non-taxable income and helps you determine where to invest it during retirement.

    This tool pulls in all your existing taxable and non-taxable accounts and gives a tax optimized view of where you should put your money in retirement.

    Simon Moore, Chief Investment Officer, FutureAdvisor
