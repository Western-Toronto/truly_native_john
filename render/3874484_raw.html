Updating The Drone Blogs They Are Launching!

Weve featured several blogs during the past year that highlighted future technology as it applies to home security systems and other security applications for drones. These future trends seemed unlikely to some, but the reality is they are all in development and will be seen in the not too distant future.

As a reminder, here are some of the comments we made in previous blog posts about drones:

June 2 If you thought that your wireless home security system is about as good as it gets, prepare for the next innovation in Security Products The Home Drone. The same technology that has made wireless alarm systems so popular will soon allow home owners to have their own private drone that will activate when an unauthorized entry is made, either automatically, or if you prefer, when you give the launch signal.

June 12 Perhaps the most interesting concept that is being discussed is the integration of pocket drones with personal security features. In other words, your own pocket size drone that can hover over you as youre walking, capturing everything below on camera. A drone that is hovering over you at an altitude of 20 feet could act as a deterrent to a would be criminal. Its conceivable that a personal drone could also pursue an assailant, capturing vital identifying information. To prevent an assailant from stealing or operating the drone, the control device would have a built in biometric scanning system.

September 22 The control of your home security system, energy management, appliances and utility settings using your mobile device are already growing in popularity. Taking this one step further, you will soon be able to expand this control to your automobile, and your own personal home security drone that hovers over your home giving you a live view of your property wherever you may be.

The applications for drone use continue to amaze us, as does their size and innovative designs. We recently came across one that captured our interest and thought you would like to see it in action. Introducing the Wearable Drone .

As you watch the video in this link , imagine how you would use your wearable drone . The possibilities are endless. While you ponder them, were certain that you will come up with some very interesting and often hilarious scenarios. Let your imagination run wild!
