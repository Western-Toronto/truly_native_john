E-Cigarettes vs. Vaporizers: Whats the Difference?

Vapor4Life November 14, 2014
Nov 14, 2014 | News |

In the process of quitting tobacco, many smokers search for alternatives that can offer the nicotine and pleasure of smoking without all of the health risks. Patches and gums work for some, but others look to electronic cigarettes to replace the habit.

However, many new electronic cigarette users dont know is that theres a huge difference when it comes to the products on the market. What most refer to as e-cigs actually come in a wide range of varieties to suit the users preference. Typical e-cigs found in supermarkets and convenience stores, for instance, usually resemble traditional cigarettes whereas vaporizers, or vape e-cigs, have entirely different looks and functions, and are usually sold by specialty vaping establishments on and off the internet.

While e-cigs and vapor e-cigs have several major differences, they do have a few things in common. Each type of device is battery-operated and can be recharged using a USB device or wall charger. While disposable e-cigs do exist, many users choose rechargeable models to get the most out of them.

More importantly, both e-cigs and vape e-cigs produce a vapor rather than smoke. This is good news for users, who wont inhale the tar and carcinogens of a tobacco cigarette, and its also beneficial for those who are around the e-cig user, who wont breathe in any secondhand smoke. E-cigs and vaporizers dont produce the harmful fumes found in tobacco cigarettes, meaning that these devices are often far more welcome in public spaces.

But this is often where the similarities between e-cigs and vapor cigs ends. While both of these devices contain an e-liquid that is broken down and converted into a vapor, the difference is in the creation of that vapor. Traditional e-cigs heat the e-liquid, so it is already burning, so to speak. Vape e-cigs, however, gradually heat the e-liquid with warm air, passing through it at a set temperature. This vaporizes the e-liquid and allows it to be released into the air without any burning, thus making it last longer, as well.

So how else do vaporizers differ? For one, vape e-cigs are sometimes slightly larger than a regular e-cig and often resemble pens or small tubes. The Wall Street Journal compared vaporizers to large fountain pens capable of holding more liquid, producing larger vapor clouds, and lasting more hours than a standard e-cig.

In fact, this is a fairly accurate comparison. Where some e-cig users might choose cig-alikes to fit in with their smoker friends, vaping enthusiasts often want others to know that theyre not smoking a typical cigarette or e-cig. Vaporizers are often made to be customized, and users can mix and match e-liquids, refill cartridges, and accessories to create a product tailored entirely to them. While there are vape e-cigs available that are smaller and more portable for added convenience, many users like the unique shapes, sizes, colors, and capacities of their vapor cigarettes.

All of this customization often produces different results in a vaporizer. Some users prefer e-cigs because they can deliver a deeper throat hit akin to smoking an actual cigarette. Cloud chasers are a subset of the vaping community who look for a bigger, better throat hit and a larger vapor cloud, often through customizing the atomizers and mouthpieces of their vapor e-cigs.

Ultimately, however, the choice between e-cigs and vaporizers is completely up to the user. Both types of devices help users avoid the harmful effects of cigarette smoking, and they offer plenty of choices to suit just about any personal vaping preference.
