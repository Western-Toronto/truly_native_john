Portable lighting studio for photo & video

KICK is small and light-weight, but boasts high output and a wide range of lighting effects:

  • Adjustable brightness and color temperature
  • Digital color technology: full color spectrum no gels!
  • High output
  • Free app for iOS and Android
  • Sampling of light
  • animated light and built-in video effects
  • Multiple KICKs may be linked via WiFi
    and remote controlled via the iOS or Android app

KICK works with any camera device: smart phones, DSLR cameras, compact cameras and others. It can be handheld or mounted on a tripod.

Color technology

KICK can do any color you want. Without filters/gels.

With the free Kick iOS or Android app KICK can do all the colors of the rainbow and any color in between. Choose from a standard color picker, or sample the light effect you want the KICK to emit:

You may also sample colors and light effects, from an existing image or video or directly from the world around you via the phone camera (even while using it). Simply point the phone towards the light effect you want KICK to recreate, tap the screen and KICK will emit that light.

Video effects

Effects library & sampling of video effects: KICK excels at animated light.

Candle flicker. Blistering lightning storms KICK can produce any animated light effect. Choose one from the built-in effects library, or sample video effects from an existing video.

You can sample light effects from any video stored on your phone: From within the Kick app you open and run any video; simply move the cursor over the light effect you want to sample, and the app will stream that light directly to your KICK(s).


Sampled light effects are automatically stored in the apps effects library.

WiFi. Remote control

Link multiple KICKs via WiFi, and remote control them via iOS or Android app.

Multiple KICKs may be mounted to light up your object or scene from various angles. You can link them all via WiFi and remote control them via the Kick app to create any desired color or light effect.
