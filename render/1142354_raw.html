College To Career: Style on a Budget

by Jacqueline Ball

This is my first feature post for the College Girl Guide To Style Series. This photo is a college to career look which is perfect for going out on a friday night and is also appropriate for an internship or a day at the office. This entire outfit is under $75! You can have a great wardrobe on a college budget by mixing and matching high and low pieces. Retailers like Forever 21, H&M, Asos, and Top Shop are great stores fortrendy pieces at an affordableprice. I also love target for affordable shoes that don't fall apart.
