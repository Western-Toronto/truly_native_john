10 Most Beautiful Lakes Around The World

Posted on February 18, 2015 February 24, 2015 Author Nirajan Bom Malla Photography

Need some travel inspiration? Cant decide where to go? How about one of the world s most beautiful lakes ?. This list covers 10mostbeautiful lakes from around the world .

1.Phoksundo Lake, Dolpa Nepal

Phoksundo lake is an alpine fresh water lake in Nepal s Shey Phoksundo National Park, located at an elevation of 3,611.5 m above sea level in the Dolpa District.

Source 500px

2. Malingne Lake, Canada

Maligne Lake is a lake in Jasper National Park, Alberta, Canada . It is famed for the colour of its water, the surrounding peaks, the three glaciers visible from the lake and Spirit Island , one of the most photographed locations in the world .

3. Upper Arrow Lake, British Columbia

The Arrow Lakes in British Columbia, Canada , divided into Upper Arrow Lake and Lower Arrow Lake, are widenings of the Columbia River. The lakes are situated between the Selkirk Mountains to the east and the Monashee Mountains to the west. Beachland is fairly rare

4. Moraine Lake, Canada

Moraine Lake is a glacially-fed lake in Banff National Park, 8.7mi outside the Village of Lake Louise, Alberta, Canada . It is situated in the Valley of the Ten Peaks.

5. Lenis Maranhenses, Brazil

Brazils stunning Lenis Maranhenses is not one lake, but dozens of small, refreshing lagoons that dot a sprawling plain of sterling white sand dunes. All you need is a four-wheeler, a cooler, and some buddies to start discovering this amazing natural park that combines the best of the desert and the tropics.

6. Rara Lake, Nepal

The Rara Lake is the biggest and deepest fresh water lake in the Nepal Himalayas. It is the main feature of the Rara National Park, located in the Jumla and Mugu Districts

Source 500px

7. Fusine Lakes, Italy

The twin glacial lakes that make up northern Italys Fusine Lakes are a must for those in search of pristine, untouched nature . The surrounding pine forest are covered in snow for much of the year and the area makes for the perfect escape from Italys more chaotic big cities.

8. Medicine lake,Canada

Medicine Lake is located at Jasper national park in Canada . It is located approximately 12miles southeast of the townsite of Jasper, Alberta. Medicine Lake is approximately 4.3miles long and is a relatively shallow lake.

9.Lake Iseo, Italy

While many of these lakes are famous for their untouched beauty , Lake Iseo in Lombardy, Italy is best known for its history and culture (which isnt to say that its not gorgeous!). Between pine forest and hidden fishing holes, travelers can find dozens of medieval villages whose scenic steeples make the perfect addition to this stunning lake.

10.Yamdrock Lake, China

Yamdrock Lake is one of Tibets three sacred lakes and a place of pilgrimage among Tibetan Buddhists and adventurous travelers. As hundreds of travelers can attest, the feeling of being amidst so much lake, mountain, and sky is as inspirational and moving as it gets. Source 500px
