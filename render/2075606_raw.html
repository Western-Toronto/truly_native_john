It's Your Story

Memeoirs takes you by the hand and lets you make a book containing your Emails, Facebook chats or WhatsApp messages and photos. It's simple, and no design skills are required.

Make it unique

Choose a style that fits you and add some introductory words to set the mood. Your book will feature only the exchanges that matter to you.

Powerfully simple

Your messages are neatly set in chronological order and printed as a perfect-bound love novel, travel diary or testimonial of your life abroad. Also available in digital format.

Our Books

Each Memeoirs is perfectly bound and produced using the latest digital printing technology. High quality Munken paper is used for the interior, providing the touch of a real, bookshop book.
