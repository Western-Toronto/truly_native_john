Web Design - New Website

Q - How do I get a new website using a Template design?

A - Decide which CMS works best for your business. If you are unsure, Wordpress is our recommendation.

Go to our website templates page and click on the CMS type of your choice located on the upper left side of the page.

Select from the templates presented, view details and demos to see the theme in action.

When you decide on a theme, click on it's details, select additional installation options: logo, content, editors, training and then click "add to cart".

You can checkout immediately or continue looking at other site services such as web marketing.

We'll take it from there. We will install the theme and CMS of your choice and perform action for any additional options selected. Once the site is ready for your usage we will notify you via your client portal.

Q - How do I get a new website using a Custom design?

A - Fill out our website survey.

We will contact you for additional information regarding the look that you want for your site. Once we have all of the details, we will assign your website project to a designer. The designer will provide mockups of the design and can re-work the mockup as necessary to get the look that you really want, up to three times.

Once the design is ready, we can install it as a theme on your selected CMS or custom framework. The CMS installation is not included in the design cost. Additonal options, including: content creation, drag n drop editors etc. are also available.

    Content Management Systems
    We recommend using a content management system such as: Wordpress, Joomla or drupal. Using a CMS allows you to more easily manage your website.
    Most website owners do well with a Wordpress CMS. Joomla & Drupal are more difficult to manage but scale better for high volume, heavy content type websites.

    How it works
    Template Website
    Select a theme from the thousands we have available then add any options that you want for your website during checkout. One we receive your order we will install your theme along with a content management system and the most important plugins to keep your website running right.

    Custom Design
    We ask that you fill out our web survey. Once the survey is complete, we can discuss in more detail the requirements of your website.

    Template vs Custom Design
    Template:
    Faster website build
    Easier ongoing maintenance
    Custom Design
    Get exactly the look and feel that you want
    Usually more costly.
