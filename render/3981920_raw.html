Submission Guidelines

Mr. Costumes 2013 Halloween Photo Contest - Entry

  • Upload your image above.
    • Image should be no less than 1024 pixels wide
    • Only original photos will be accepted. Photos with a copyright mark, web address, physical address or any other content or graphics will not be accepted. They must not copied from another source, including but not limited to, websites, magazines, books,etc. Contestants may submit multiple photos per category and/or submit entries to multiple categories. Category winners will also be eligible for the Grand Prize Best Overall Costume category.
    • Include your full name and contact information on the entry form. Note: No email addresses or last names will be displayed on Mr. Costumes' website or social sites.
    • Write a brief description of your costume that will appear next to the photo
    • All photos that are submitted will be considered the property of Mr. Costumes and may be used by Mr. Costumes for promotional purposes as set forth in the Contest guidelines and terms and conditions.
      • Entries may also be sent via U.S. mail to: Mr. Costumes, 205 Curie Drive, Alpharetta, GA 30005
    • Like or Follow us on one of our social sites ( Facebook , Twitter , Pinterest )
