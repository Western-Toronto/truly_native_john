BEST CARRY-ON BAGS

How to Travel in Style

The best carry-on bags should fit all your essentials with easeand contrary to what you might think during the panic of packing, no those plastic grocery totes do not apply.

The glamorous days of travel may be long gone but, if youve been paying attention, you should know that the lost art of flying in style is making a comeback. (If you need convincing, check out our story on celebrity-inspired airplane outfits .)

So youve created the perfect vacation packing list , youve set aside an airplane-ready outfit, now what? Enter, the perfect carry-on bag.

A few years ago, I slyly stole my mothersa black, leather duffle doctor bag she purchased while on a trip to Turkey. It was large enough to fit more than all my essentials and, more importantly, small enough to get past security. Each trip I took, fellow travelers stopped to ask me where it was fromboth men and women, I might add. Clearly the search for the best carry-on bags is a journey were all on.

And not only did the bag hold up year after year, but the leather only got better with age. Unfortunately, for me, the bag is now back in my mother's possession. (She was so upset when I convinced her that she lost it, I had no choice but to come clean.)

Lesson learned: The perfect carry-on is an investment well worth making (just think of all the money you'll save on baggage fees!). A sturdy, go-to bag eases the stress of packing and is a piece youll keep for years to come.

And since theres no reason your vacation style shouldnt start at the airport,weve rounded up a selection of the best carry-on bags fit to fly. From Bottega Veneta's sleek, extra-large woven tote to Tory Burchs wanderlust-worthy suitcase, these TSA-approved bags will show everyoneyou know a thing or two about how to travel in style .
