Rapid Weight Loss Tips, Tricks, and Traps

by admin on Thursday, June 5th, 2014 | No Comments

We all have an idea of a better us, less weight, more muscle, tighter skin, and the list goes on. The problem is that in order to achieve these things, its almost like we have to have a 180 degree lifestyle change; become a new person so that we can be disciplined enough to stick to our new years resolution for the next 5 years to get the weight off that we want, build the muscle to keep it off and then start doing everything else that brings the results.
And that could be fun, having a completely different daily routine, being focused on different things, finding new hobbies, etc. But more realistically, we need something that we can incorporate into our already hectic lives that will help us to lose weight and get healthy without breaking the bank and our lifestyle.

Weight Loss The Slow Way

When we first decide to embark on this journey, were motivated and want to make things happen. BUT:

  • If we dont see any results -> were discouraged -> and we stop.
  • If holidays come up -> we forget our diet -> and we stop.
  • If its too difficult to follow -> we lose track -> and we stop.
  • If life happens -> were distracted -> and we stop.

See a pattern here? Thats why its a good idea to capitalize on the fleeting motivation that you have when you first start your weight loss trek and make the most out of it. Because although it would be nice, weight loss is not a steady decline in body weight over the course of a year or two. Rather, we see rapid weight loss followed by small drops, plateaus, and even slight increases. And thats okay, thats just how lifes routine works.

But knowing this pattern of dramatic drops, then plateaus, we should make the most out of the drops and get the most off while we can and while were motivated! Lets check out some rapid weight loss tips, tricks and traps to get us started REALLY losing weight!

Weight Loss The Rapid Way

Portion Control

There are many things that a person can do in order to lose weight quickly without making major life style changes. There are however minor changes that should occur which will lead to losing weight quickly. One of the first steps to starting a healthy diet is controlling the portions of food we eat. Portion control has become very difficult because it takes time to measure food out properly, time which we rarely have, and whenever eating at a restaurant it seems that the plates just get larger and larger with way more food than is necessary. Dr. Michael Roizen said, Portion control is the key to losing weight because most of us eat enough until our stomachs cant hold anymore. That is, we dont eat until we are full by the number of calories we need to live; thats the real way that we used to eat, but now we eat until our stomachs are absolutely stretched, a la Thanksgiving.

So what are the correct portions of food that we are supposed to eat ? In order to save time and not have to measure everything out, here is a list of helpful visuals from WebMD to better understand correct portions and save time.

  • Vegetables or fruit is about the size of your fist.
  • Pasta is about the size of one scoop of ice cream.
  • Meat, fish, or poultry is the size of a deck of cards or the size of your palm (minus the fingers).
  • Snacks such as pretzels and chips are about the size of a cupped handful.
  • Apple is the size of a baseball.
  • Potato is the size of a computer mouse.
  • Bagel is the size of a hockey puck.
  • Pancake is the size of a compact disc.
  • Steamed rice is the size of a cupcake wrapper.
  • Cheese is the size of a pair of dice or the size of your whole thumb (from the tip to the base).
