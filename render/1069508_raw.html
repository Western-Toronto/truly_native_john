#PopsicleRules for being a kid: #1 NEVER grow up! @NickMom #Ad

May 21, 2014 by Jen Jamar 28 Comments

FTC Disclosure: I received compensation consideration from NickMom as part of my relationship with the QualityBlue Community. All opinions expressed are my own.

In case you havent heard yet, Popsicle is having a really fun social media contest. Now through May 31st, 2014, all you have to do is share your favorite rules for being a kid for the chance to win some pretty great prizes, including $500!

As someone that sincerely hopes to never grow up, being a kid is something Im passionate about. Naturally, my #1 rule is this: NEVER grow up! Or at the very least, if you do have to grow up, dont forget that youre never too old to act like a kid.

Last night we took the kids to the park and they challenged me to prove I believed in my own rule for being a kid. Amid much taunting and singing of anything you can do, I can do better, they put me to the test at the playground.

Check out my #PopsicleRules video to see whether I passed all 4 playground tests they gave me. (Hint Im 30. And monkey bars arent my friend. Its ok to laugh.)

Even though I didnt quite pass all of their tests, I think I did a pretty good job keeping up with them. We had a blast hanging out on the playground together. I might not be a real kid anymore, but I know Im still a kid at heart.

Share your Rules For Being A Kid for a chance to win!

As I mentioned earlier, now through May 31st 2014, you can share your own rules for being a kid as part of the #PopsicleRules contest.

Using Facebook, Twitter, Instagram, or Vine, you can upload a photo, text, or video that shares one of your rules for being a kid with the hashtags #PopsicleRules and #Contest to enter.
