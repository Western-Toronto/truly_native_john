Watch Valeria Ochoas Golf Trick Shot Challenge at The Crown Penthouse!

By One Queensridge Place
Posted February 10th, 2015
In Miscellaneous
1
0
Recently Pro Golfer Valeria Ochoa stopped by One Queensridge Place for a visit and received a friendly challenge from our very own Senior VP of Operations, Randy Char. The venue? The three-story, 15,000 square foot Crown Penthouse!

After keeping up putt for putt over a flurry of creative shots that bounced and weaved through many of The Crowns featured spaces, Char decided to up the stakes. What happens next is one for the history books at One Queensridge Place!
