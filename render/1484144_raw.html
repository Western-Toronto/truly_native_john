The DJServicepack.com 1st Quarter Guide to the hottest 7 songs from the hottest 7 cities. Atlanta, DC (DMV), New York, Chicago, San Francisco, Los Angeles, and Dallas/Houston

DJServicePack.com has created the quintessential breakdown of the hottest songs in hip-hop clubs across the country, city to city.
