The Story Of

Mark Paul

27 Dreadlocks Cut, Countless Children Helped

Though it was a routine overnight procedure, Mark Paul hated every minute that his three-year old was in the hospital. My son was so small lying on that big hospital bed, Mark remembered. With tubes in his arms, he looked incredibly weak and scared. To top it off, I couldnt do anything to cheer him up, especially behind a thick slab of glass.

  • The Spark

    After seeing his three-year old go through an overnight hospital procedure, Mark Paul decided he could not take his childrens health for granted anymore and wanted to do something for the many kids who are suffering from terminal or life-limiting conditions.

  • The Action

    Wanting to raise funds for The Hummingbird House, Mark kicked off the fundraiser by cutting off each one of his 27 dreadlocks for every $1,000 donated.

  • The Result

    Mark surpassed his expectations by raising $22,000 dollars. Using the everydayhero platform gave his campaign an added boost by helping him to receive donations from the local mayor and celebrities. Mark is now addicted to fundraising and finding ways to help children in need.

Thankfully, his tonsillitis surgery went according to plan and his son returned home happy and healthy. But Mark was deeply affected by the experience. He felt that he could no longer take the health of his three children for granted.

He couldnt get those other kids in the hospital out of his head, those suffering from terminal or life-limiting conditions. Their lives were filled with exhausting hospital visits, and procedures that wouldnt necessarily lead to recovery. The hours spent coping with pain and selecting treatment plans would compound, for kids and parents alike. With zero help, a terrible diagnosis can become a financial and emotional disaster for the entire family pushing them to a breaking point.

Mark couldnt get those other kids in the hospital out of his head, those suffering from terminal or life-limiting conditions.

Mark saw a way to help when he learned of the Hummingbird House through a close family friend. The organization provides a home away from home for families that can no longer rely on the hospital for help, and have difficulty supporting the child from their own residence. The Hummingbird House needed help to grow in size the United Kingdom has 63 million people and 49 hospices, whereas Australia has 23 million residents and only 2 hospices, none of which were in Marks home state of Queensland.

To kick off the fundraiser in a unique way, Mark decided to give up something only he could 27 dreadlocks, each dread 12 years old and down to his waist. Placing a lofty (and unanticipated) goal of $1000 dollars per dread, Mark surpassed his expectations by raising $22,000 dollars. everydayhero gave his campaign an added boost by helping him to receive donations from the local mayor and celebrities.

Now, Mark is addicted to fundraising and finding ways to help children in need. And anytime he needs a motivation refresh, he looks at his three gorgeous kids.

If anything were to happen to one of them how could I cope with that without any help? Theres no way that I cant help support this cause!

About Mark Paul

Mark Paul's devotion to his family drives everything he does. Mark is addicted to fundraising and finding ways to help children in need.
