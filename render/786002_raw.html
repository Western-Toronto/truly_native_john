Before You Choose Your First Electronic Cigarette: Heres What You Need to Know

Are you ready to try electronic cigarettes but unsure how to choose the type thats right for you? These tips will get you started.

The first thing you need to know is that you will encounter a bewildering variety of e-cigarette brands in todays marketplace. Demand is high for this alternative to traditional cigarettes, and manufacturers are busy creating new models all the time. It may take a little bit of time, but before you buy anything, you need to do your research.

Pay attention to technology and construction when choosing your electronic cigarette

  • Battery life varies; you want to choose a product that will hold up to a full days use without needing to be charged. Each puff uses battery power, so if youre a pack-a-day smoker, youll want something that will last for at least 300 puffs. For when you do need to charge, its best to choose an option that allows you maximum flexibility. If youre at your computer all day, you can buy an electronic cigarette which has batteries which can be charged with a USB cable. If youre constantly on the move, youll want to look into portable charging cases and wall and car adaptors so you can charge your e-cigarette no matter where you are.

  • Older e-cigarettes all contained three parts: the battery, the atomizer, and the cartridge. Each time the cartridge was emptied, it needed to be manually refilled and the atomizer cleaned to avoid clogs. These models are still on the market, but they have been joined by newer two-piece cartridge style e-cigarettes which include disposable atomizer/cartridge units pre-filled with an e-liquid. If you dont have time to constantly refill your e-cigarette cartridges, or prefer not to, youll want to go with a cartridge style e-cigarettes . No matter which you buy, make sure the cartridge will last for as long as you need it toat least a full days worth of liquid, more if possible.

Choose the electronic cigarette that complements your lifestyle

  • Are you looking for something that will mimic the look and feel of a traditional cigarette as much as possible, or are you looking for a more novel experience? Things to pay attention to here include e-cigarette flavor, appearance, and vapor volume. E-liquid , the nicotine-containing liquid inside an e-cigarette cartridge, comes in a wide variety of flavors ranging from tobacco, to menthol, to coffee, chocolate, and fruit. Youll need to experiment to find the flavor that works best for you. Some e-cigarettes look very much like traditional cigarettes, while others come in different colors, styles, and lengths. Have fun customizing your experience by choosing one that matches your personal style. E-cigarettes also vary in terms of how much vapor they produce; if you love the look of blowing smoke, make sure the model you choose is able to replicate it.

  • You can get e-cigarettes for a cheap price, but be aware that there is sometimes a trade-off in quality. Make sure that if you do go for a less expensive option, you research it well and find out if other customers have had problems with it, such as low battery life or flimsy construction. One easy way to try several options at once without spending a lot of money is to order a sampler kit from the manufacturer; these come with several cartridges in a variety of strengths and flavors, so you can see which you prefer.
