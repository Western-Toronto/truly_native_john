Ben And Jerrys Flavor Graveyard: All The Ben And Jerrys Flavors Youll NEVER Get To Taste

by Mitchell Friedman
Associate Editor
Posted on September 24, 2014, 4:42 PM
More: Ben And Jerry's , deserts , food , Thursday Morning

Starting today, Ben and Jerrys released the third of four SNL-inspired ice cream flavors. In June, they gave us a taste of Lazy Sunday (vanilla cake batter ice cream with chocolate & yellow cupcake pieces & a chocolate frosting swirl)and Gillys Catastrophic Crunch (chocolate & sweet cream ice creams with caramel clusters, fudge-covered almonds & a marshmallow swirl). Both concoctions sound incredible but, unfortunately, theyre not available in pints. The only place you can get the new flavors is at a Ben and Jerrys Scoop Shop . The same is true for their newest SNL-themed flavor, Two Wild And Crazy Pies.

To try these amazing sounding ice creams, you can use their shop locator, linked here for your pleasure . But the exclusive aura with which Ben and Jerrys surrounds their speciality flavors is nothing new. In fact, they have an entire Flavor Graveyard full of tasty ice delights that were once available for purchase, but have now been retired forever. And believe it or not, this graveyard is an actual location you can visit .

Now, to be fair, it appears that the official Flavor Graveyard is more like a retired ice cream hall of fame. Below, we list all the flavors Ben and Jerry have officially put to rest, but we do know of at least a few other flavors they didnt include according to Mental Floss and there might be more. Perhaps they werent proud of jalepeno lime ice or bloody mary sorbet, so instead of honoring them with a fancy resting place, they threw them off a cliff and hoped no one would notice.

Feast your eyes below on all the Ben and Jerrys flavors the company has officially discontinued and laid to rest. All descriptions come from the Ben and Jerrys Flavor Graveyard .
