Perfect gift for the Masshole on your holiday shopping list

Photos 11/29/2013
Perry Eaton
  • Share
  • Tweet

Locally-based print collective Trifecta Editions showcases area artists influenced by everything from comic books to landscape design. One such artist, David Buckley Borden, has issued a series of drawings which are now one of Trifectas most successful prints, including the hilariously-titled Masshole. The piece features a Cape Cod soil profile with both real and fictitious layers, a hat tip to the commonly-used term and New Englands multi-tiered history.

Masshole is just one of many prints that Trifecta Editions is including in a special Cyber Monday sale, but visit the collectives blog for a special coupon code that they will post today to get a head start . The gallery below includes work from various artists, as well as Trifecta Editions commentary.

< >
"Masshole Classic" by David Buckley Borden
Masshole Classic is a witty and campy print inspired by geologic illustrations and Davids studies in landscape design. This classy print brings new meaning to the term Masshole.
"60 Drawings" by Gupi Ranganathan
This screen print is based on genomic drawings Gupi completed during her residency at The Broad Institute of MIT & Harvard.
"Aumento" by Greg Lamarche
This print is based on a drawing from Gregs sketchbook, in turn inspired by a comic he found in Italy.
"Crystal Magik Kerchief" by Earth Magik
Finished with handmade tassels, this kerchief features crystals, nautili, tree rings and the phases of the moon.
"Forest Magik Scarf" by Earth Magik
The metallic fern, maple seed, pinecone and tree ring designs on this kerchief were all originally hand drawn and cut from rubylith.
"Fort Buckley Postcard Pack" by David Buckley Borden
This set of seven screen printed postcards features the flag of Fort Buckley and highlights from Davids Ecological Engineer series.
"Goggle Kids" by Farel Dalrymple
This six-color, glow-in-the-dark print is based on a drawing from Farels sketchbook.
"Studio Original: Koji Uehara"
Trifecta created their first studio original print to celebrate the incredible 2013 Boston Red Sox.
Trifecta worked with Boston-based artist Nick Z to create a limited-edition screen print, postcard pack and stickers to release on Black Friday.
"Ying Yang I" by Nick Z
Nicks signature style jumps out of this neon print in the form of twisting creatures, hidden faces and surreal nature imagery.
