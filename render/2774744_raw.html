The Job Market is Changing

We live in a world that feeds on the frenetic energy of competition. Our societies consume competition the same way we use gas, oil and water: with a reckless fervor and no consideration of tomorrow. As individuals, we engage with competition in every facet of our lives, including hiring. The competition is never ending. But what if were looking at the competition the wrong way? What if the competition is over us?

Click To Enlarge

Via AkkenCloud

Whether you believe it or not, the hiring shift is coming. If companies ignore the free agent market, in two years they may wonder why theyre so dissatisfied with their recent hires. If employees dont put a little bit of faith that the job market is really changing, they could miss out on the opportunity of a lifetime.

What are you waiting, start to acquire more skill to be part of this shift in job market.
