    50 shades of Brad Pitt
  • Dec 17, 2013
In honor of Brad Pitt's 50th birthday on Dec. 18, 2013, we could think of no more fitting tribute than to find 50 of our favorite photos. It wasn't easy, but USA TODAY's Ann Oldenburg had the oh-so-difficult task of culling through the Brad archives. Here Brad is at the Governor Awards on Nov. 16. Does he look (almost) 50 to you? Jon Kopaloff, FilmMagic
And now we go back. Way back. Brad was doing the Justin Bieber pose way before the Biebz was born. Here's sexy Brad in 1988. John Paschal, Celebrity Photo
Brad famously dated Gwyneth Paltrow, one of three high-profile celebrity unions he would go on to have. Here they are on July 12, 1995, in Los Angeles. John Paschal, Celebrity Photo
Brad and Gwynnie were a hot couple at the Oscars on March 25, 1996. Kevork Djansezian, AP
Brad found someone else to pose with on the red carpet on March 13, 1997. He and Harrison Ford smiled for cameras at the premiere of their movie, 'The Devil's Own' in New York. Albert Ferreira, AP
A random Brad snap, taken Nov. 10, 1998. Janet Gough, Celebrity Photo
Brad broke up with Paltrow in 1997 and fell for Jennifer Aniston in 1998. Here they are laughing their way through the Emmys on Sept. 12, 1999. Michael Caulfied, AP
The two were the hot couple at Vanity Fair's Oscar Party on March 26, 2000. Did Jen start the side-boob trend? Eric Charbonneau, Berliner Studio
Newlyweds Brad and Jen share their famous wedding photo, taken in Malibu on July 29, 2000. Michael Sanville, AP
Brad suited up for the premiere of 'Snatch' in London on Aug. 23, 2000. Adam Butler, AP
Brad and Jen hit the Golden Globe red carpet in Beverly Hills on Jan. 20, 2002. Aniston was nominated for best supporting TV actress in 'Friends.' She won in 2003. Dan MacMedan, USA TODAY
Brad could have been playing for the Boston Red Sox or the Zac Brown Band with this beard. Here he is at the premiere of 'The Bourne Identity' in Los Angeles on June 6, 2002. Kevin Winter, Getty Images
Who are these tourists? Brad and Jen arrive at the 'Full Frontal' premiere in Los Angeles on July 23, 2002. Tsuni, Image Direct
Brad and Jen kept it casual at the Independent Spirit Awards in Santa Monica on March 22, 2003. Jon Kopaloff, Getty Images
Looking lovey-dovey, Brad and Jen watch the 2003 Spirit Awards. Chris Pizzello, AP
Brad gazed deeply into our eyes for a portrait in New York on April 30, 2004. Robert Deutsch, USA TODAY
Brad and Jen again on May 10, 2004 at a 'Troy' premiere, this time in New York. Paul Hawthorne Getty Images
Brad and Jen were still going strong at the premiere of 'Troy' on May 13, 2004 at the Cannes Film Festival in France. Patrick Gardin, AP
Brad and Jen were the cool Emmys couple on the red carpet on Sept. 19, 2004. Dan MacMedan, USA TODAY
Brad's wedding ring was on display as he arrived at the 'Ocean's Twelve' premiere on Dec. 8, 2004 in Hollywood. Carlo Allegri, Getty Images
Bad lighting for Brad when he posed in Rome to promote 'Ocean's Eleven' on Dec. 10, 2004. It's OK, Brad, we still love you. Franco Origlia, Getty Images
Brad caused a stir when he arrived at Narita International Airport on Jan. 12, 2005 as news erupted that he and Aniston were separating. She filed for divorce in March 2005. Itsuo, Inouye, AP
Brad looked pretty bad-you-know-what at the premiere of 'Mr. & Mrs. Smith' on June 7, 2005. He and Angelina Jolie co-starred in the film, which is said to be where their romance started. Kevin Winter, Getty Images
We forgot Brad's hair was ever this dark. Here he is with Angelina Jolie at the World Economic Forum in Davos, Switzerland, on Jan. 26, 2006. Jolie is pregnant with the couple's first biological child. Shiloh Jolie Pitt was born on May 27, 2006. Michel Euler, AP
Brad smiled during a news conference in New Orleans in this Aug. 31, 2006 shot. Classic Brad. Alex Brandon, AP
Dad Brad emerged in 2006. Here he is in Mumbai on Nov. 12, 2006 with Angelina and her kids, daughter Zahara, whom Jolie adopted in 2005 in Ethiopia, and son Maddox, adopted by Jolie in 2002 in Cambodia. AP
Brad shows up at 'The Good Shepherd' premiere in New York on Dec. 11, 2006. Evan Agostini Getty Images
Brad and Angie were the epitome of cool as they navigated a busy street in downtown Ho Chi Minh city on Nov. 23, 2006. STR AFP/Getty Images
Brad and Angie were pure glam at the Golden Globes on Jan. 15, 2007. Mark J. Terrill, AP
They worked the red carpet at the premiere of 'A Mighty Heart' in New York on June 13, 2007. Stuart Ramson, AP
Brad had a heavy load when he visited a playground with kids Zahara and Maddox in New York City on Aug. 26, 2007. James Devaney, WireImage
Brad attends a press conference for the Global Green USA's first house project in the 9th Ward district on Aug. 21, 2007 in New Orleans. Nice. Mark Mainz, Getty Images
So in love. The two were on their way to mastering the art of togetherness on the red carpet. Here they are on May 24, 2007 in Cannes, promoting 'Ocean's Thirteen.' Pascal Le Segretain, Getty Images
Their cuddling was on display at the Critics Choice Awards at the Santa Monica Civic Auditorium on Jan. 7, 2008. Chris Polk, WireImage
Their love blossomed as a pregnant Angelina posed on May 15, 2008 at the premiere of 'Kung Fu Panda' in Cannes. She delivered twins Knox and Vivienne on July 12, 2008, who joined siblings Maddox, Zahara, Shiloh (born in 2006) and Pax, adopted in 2007 in Vietnam. Matt Sayles, AP
On July 2, 2008, Brad carries Zahara and Shiloh (left) into the Lenval Hospital in Nice, southern France, where Angelina had checked in for the birth of their twins. Lionel Cironneau, AP
Brad's bud George Clooney joined him to hype 'Burn After Reading' at the Venice Film Festival on Aug. 27, 2008. Joel Ryan, AP
True love. Brangelina at the 'Salt' premiere on July 19, 2010 in Hollywood. Jason Merritt, Getty Images
Time for another solo shot. Brad attends a gala screening of 'Burn After Reading' in Toronto on Sept. 5, 2008. Evan Agostini, AP
They look like they're on a movie set, but they were on the red carpet for the German premiere of Brad's movie 'The Curious Case of Benjamin Button'' on Jan. 19, 2009 in Berlin. Clemens Bilan, AFP/Getty Images
Brad caused buzz on Capitol Hill when he posed with House Speaker Nancy Pelosi on March 5, 2009. Brad and Nancy chatted about his project for affordable, environmentally sustainable housing for low-income residents of the Lower 9th Ward in New Orleans. Susan Walsh, AP
And Brad bonded with former president Bill Clinton during the 2009 Clinton Global Initiative Annual Meeting on Sept. 24, 2009 in New York. Stephen Chernin, AP
We say "oui" to this hunky shot of Brad at the 'Megamind' premiere on Nov. 29, 2010 in Paris. Jacques Brinon, AP
When in Rome ... cuddle for the cameras and promote 'The Tourist' on Dec. 15, 2010. Elisabetta Villa, Getty Images
This may have been the start of his long-hair era. And we like it! Brad laughs during a 'Moneyball' press conference at the Toronto International Film Festival on Sept. 9, 2011. Darren Calabrese, AP
This is a somewhat rare shot of the entire globe-trotting family, taken Nov. 8, 2011 at Haneda Airport in Tokyo. Toru Yamanaka, AFP/Getty Images
Angelina made big headlines at the 2012 Oscars by sticking her leg out of her dress. Leg-bombing went on to become a favorite pastime. Dan MacMedan, USA TODAY
The two were tight on Feb. 13, 2012 at the Cinema for Peace Gala ceremony during the 62nd Berlin International Film Festival. Andreas Rentz, AFP/Getty Images
Not our favorite Brad look. But what do you think? Here he is back in Cannes on May 23, 2012. Joel Ryan, AP
Brad went for really long hair during his 'World War Z' promo tour. Here he is on June 17, 2013 in New York. Dave Kotinsky, Getty Images
Did we say 50 photos? Well, here's No. 51, an extra candle on the cake for you, as Brad smiles and sports a ponytail for the '12 Years a Slave' premiere (he's one of the producers of the film) on Sept. 6, 2013 in Toronto. All together now... Happy Birthday, Brad! Jason Merritt, Getty Images
