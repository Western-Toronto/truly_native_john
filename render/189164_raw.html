Sometimes I think that summertime recipes get overshadowed during the year by the spices and pumpkin recipes of fall or the Christmastime fudge and cookies. Homemade ice creams, light and fluffy trifles, and endless berries, cobblers, and crisps! Summer flavors are really just some of the best that the year has to offer.

These Peaches and Cream Sammies combine so many of the great flavors of summer in an easy, creative, and delicious way.

Kings Hawaiian rolls and buns are the obvious bread choice for these cute, summery sammies. I think we can all agree that these soft and fluffy little buns are basically the official bread of summertime. Bread can sometimes feel so heavy in the heat, but the sweet and soft combo of Kings Hawaiian just seems to fit the bill no matter the weather. And, as it happens, they are perfect for grilling!

You know how donuts get super extra soft and melt-in-your-mouth delicious when you eat them warm? Kings Hawaiian Bread seems to work the same way. When you heat them up on the grill, you get this incredible little bit of crisp at first bite and an undeniable melt-in-your-mouth buttery softness.

Add to that a sweet cream cheese spread, juicy fresh peach slices, and a sprinkling of cinnamon and you have yourself one amazing summer time dessert that is absolutely perfect for BBQ season.

Really, words just dont do these little sliders justice. I know Ive already said melt-in-your-mouth twice, but its really the best description I can come up with. This whole combination of fresh fruit, sweet cream, and lightly grilled sweet bread does just that: it melts in your mouth .

Peaches and Cream Sammies

This whole combination of fresh fruit, sweet cream, and lightly grilled sweet bread is melt-in-your-mouth delicious. Perfect for your next BBQ or summer potluck!

Yield: 12

Ingredients:

12 King's Hawaiian dinner rolls

8 ounces cream cheese (notneufchatel), softened

2 tbsp sour cream or greek yogurt

1/2 cup sugar

1 tsp vanilla extract

4-6 ripe peaches, pitted and thinly sliced

Cinnamon or cinnamon sugar for sprinkling on top

Directions:

Split all of the dinner rolls and set aside.

Use an electric hand mixer or small food processor to blend together the cream cheese, sour cream, sugar, and vanilla extract.

To assemble and serve: place the split roll on the hot grill for a few minutes until the roll has warmed through and there are crispy grill marks on the bread. Spread some of the cream cheese mixture on the roll, top with a few slices of peaches, and sprinkle with the cinnamon. Place the second half of the roll back on top, and eat!
