About this project

Smack^P is not only the next evolution of fabric but it's also the next evolution of Smack Sportswear .

What is Smack^P?
Smack^P is the next step in the evolution of technical fabrics, similar to the introduction of moisture wicking technology in the 90s. It brings together two great partners, Smack Sportswear and Phiten, to create a new line of athletic apparel for volleyball players and general athletes alike.

This new line of Phitenized apparel (Smack^P) will incorporate Phiten technology into apparel for the first time with a U.S.-based vendor. Smack's Phitenized team apparel will initially be offered exclusively for volleyball, but in the near future, will be expanded into other sports as well. The core items can be worn by all athletes.

Smack^P apparel combines seamlessly with the technical benefits of Phitens AQUA-METALS Technology and the companys philosophy of health, energy and well-being. The purpose is to help athletes excel during activity and to support a healthy and active lifestyle.

What is Phiten AQUA-METALS technology?
Phiten, established in 1983, through years of research, has developed a number of wellness technologies that are now being applied to various product categories. AQUA-TITANIUM is based upon Phiten technology for making hydro-colloidal metal. Traditionally, titanium is a lightweight and insoluble metal used in medical devices due to its non-corrosive and hypoallergenic properties. However, titanium in its solid state is not suitable for high-intensity, exercise-specific applications; this fact led Phiten to develop AQUA-TITANIUM.

AQUA-TITANIUM is utilized as a dye and embedded into fabric by permeating individual strands of fiber and becoming a component of the finished product. Essentially, these metals are broken down into microscopic particles dispersed in water.

Does it work? If you watch a Professional Baseball game youll see more than half the players wearing Phiten necklaces. Professional athletes in sports such as Basketball, Soccer, Golf, Running, and Softball are also wearing them religiously. Can you imagine how having this technology in contact with YOUR ENTIRE BODY could do to improve YOUR performance?!

Revolutionary Fabric

1) Phiten AQUA-TITANIUM Technology:Phiten focuses their energy on developing products that allow you to perform at your best, and this technology is the key.Now, this technology will be used in core performance, team and beach apparel, allowing people to Feel the Difference!

2) Moisture Wicking: An advanced, integrated channeling process is used to wick moisture from your body more than any other apparel on the market today.

3) Soil Resistant: Helping to ensure the longevity of the product through the most grueling activity.

4) Anti-Microbial: Preventing odor-causing bacteria.

5) Designed and Tested by Top Athletes: All Smack^P products are designed then tested by some of the top athletes in the world.Once they meet their approval, Smack Sportswear produces for the masses.This allows for a better fit and feel, allowing athletes to perform at the highest level.

6) Made in America: Smack does not outsource any part of its printing, dying, or development. All Smack garments are made in their factory in Torrance, CA. We support the U.S. economy we hope you will support us!

Risks and challenges

The Smack^P line of beach and indoor volleyball apparel is the next evolution of products from a company that has been around since 1983. Currently, we have the manufacturing process down to a science and are ready to go! The founder personally sees all pieces of the Smack^P line through each stage of manufacturing, and there is nothing more important to Smack than making sure that supporters receive a product that exceeds their expectation.
