About this project

Each Square = Person, Group of People, Type of Contact or App

You want to know who's calling, emailing, texting, facebooking, WhatsApping, tweeting you. You're getting pretty sick of checking your phone all the time.

Glance over at a small, modern box that looks like a Rubik's Cube and know immediately who has been reaching out.

Simply setup LEDmeKnow and it'll do the rest! You'll never miss any important phone call, email or tweet, because you'll know what's worth your attention.

* Notifications stay lit until you check your phone*

LEDmeKnow displays up to 9 different events simultaneously. And all notifications stay lit until you check your phone.

* Go a month between charges *

LEDMeKnow uses Bluetooth 4.0 Low Energy Protocol, so it only has to be charged once a month. So this is... you know... rocks!

This small and elegant cube is a result of our continuous research, meticulous development and thorough testing. See how it evolved through the production cycle.

The LEDmeKnows you see were made by a CNC-machine and by our own hands with love :-). However when dealing with the materials intended to render an optic effect it is hard to reach the perfection when assembling manually. Thats why the mentioned devices have somewhat nonuniform backlit, black dashes as well as other minor inaccuracies.

We list these issues here to underline that manufactured product is going to be free of them.

It is important for us to deliver the solid and perfect-looking product, as we are perfection freaks!

We've been very busy and now everything is almost ready. Your pledges will help to schedule the first production batch by the end of October and start shipping the rewards in November.


Risks and challenges

We don't have any significant concerns or caveats. Our prototype is solid, our supplier (the team behind iBlazr) is reliable, and we've been making electronics for a decade.

FAQ

  • Does it work with my smartphone?

    LEDmeKnow will work with any Bluetooth 4.0 compatible smartphone. You will need to download a free application to configure your device. We'll release an iOS app first. Android 4.3+ and Windows Phone 8.1+ apps will be developed later on.

    Last updated: Tue, Jul 1 2014 11:41 am EDT
  • Can the box handle notifications from more than one device at a time?

    One LEDmeKnow can display notifications from only one smartphone at a time.

    Last updated: Tue, Jul 1 2014 11:41 am EDT
  • Can my smartphone connect to more than one LEDmeKnow device at a time?

    Yes! If you have several smartboxes around you, your phone will connect to all paired devices available within the range of the Bluetooth signal.

    Last updated: Tue, Jul 1 2014 11:41 am EDT
  • What are the battery charging options for the device?

    LEDmeKnow package includes a mini-USB charger. The mini-USB slot is on the bottom side of the device, and you will never see it when the device is charged. When charging, simply put the device on the left / right / top side and connect it to the charger. A built-in accelerometer will sense the rotation and adjust the lights so you won't notice the difference.

    Last updated: Tue, Jul 1 2014 11:41 am EDT
  • How often do I have to charge LEDmeKnow?

    On average, once a month.

    Last updated: Tue, Jul 1 2014 11:41 am EDT
  • Can LEDmeKnow display notifications from WhatsApp, Viber etc.?

    Yes, it will display alerts from any application.

    Last updated: Tue, Jul 1 2014 11:41 am EDT
