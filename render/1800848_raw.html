The Top 10 Deadliest Jobs

Throughout the world some workers navigate deadly work environments just to bring home a steady income. These workers must follow strict safety training and protocol in order to stay alive. A safety audit and/or a job hazard analysis and training should be conducted with every employee. A safety consultant would observe a worker in their environment to adequately assess the workers training needs. These employees should be trained not only on how to perform their job safely but also on how to operate within a hazardous environment. The following is a compilation of the ten deadliest jobs. The ranking is based on deaths per hundred thousand, ordered from greatest danger to least.
