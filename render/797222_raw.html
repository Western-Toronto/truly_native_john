The History Of Baking Infographic

Posted on December 11, 2013 by MakeBake


Here at MakeBake we have been busy discovering the history of baking! We have put together the below infographic which will take you back in time to 4000BC when the Egyptians pioneered leavening right up to the late 1900s when cake decorating innovations happened.

Youll discover interesting facts such as how the word cake came from Scandinavia and how the baking process has evolved over the years. Oh and if you were around the 17th century in Europe you wouldnt be having a glass of milk with your cake, no, youd be having a glass of sweet wine or tea! Whod of thought it!
