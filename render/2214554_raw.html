Description

WFD or World cup Football Dribbler presents to you some of the most epic moments in Football World cup history in a game Dribble the ball away from great footballers! Manage to keep the ball in your control till each round ends. Sounds simple but it has lots of twists and turns. There are spikes that can puncture the ball so careful when you dribble. There are coins that you can collect so don't dribbler aimlessly. There is a head flying to give you a head butt , teeths ready to bite you and a god's hand to stop you. Will you surpass all these obstacles and dribble your way to glory for the ultimate title of WORLDCUP FOOTBALL DRIBBLER !!!!
