  • Dr. Delilah: Intro

    Im very definitely a woman and I enjoy it.

    - Marilyn Monroe

    Unleash your inner desires in complete anonymity.

    Welcome. Im thrilled that youve come to alterD8. Im Dr. Delilah, and here Im a psychologist. (I just love hearing what people have to say behind closed doors!)

    I wanted to provide a secure space where people could engage in anonymous avatar chat to create fulfilling avatar relationships without disturbing their offline existence.

    I was inspired when checking my Facebook page one Sunday evening before I went to bed

    I yearned for authentic connection

    My Facebook page was filled with photos of my life-long friends second wedding. So many gorgeous men! I just love smiling men in suits.

    The photos were generating all kinds of generic comments, such as You look so lovely! and Everyone is so happy!

    I yearned to write about what touched me in the photos. I didnt. Not with my name, face and location exposed.

    If only I could have been anonymous

    Under the photo of the thirty-something man, with the gorgeous green eyes, who was smiling and dancing with a little girl, I would have written:

    What a beautiful man. Who else thinks so and how can he be contacted?

    Under the photo of the Father of the Bride, if no one knew who I was, I would have left this comment:

    Who thinks the Father of the Bride is incredibly dashing? But look at his face as he walks his daughter down the aisle for the second time. What do you think hes feeling? Whats he thinking?

    Under the photo of the brides sister who smiled all night long, I wanted to write:

    What a tragically beautiful devoted wife and mother. What did she think about that night, once her kids were in bed and her husband had flown out of town for yet another business meeting?

    I didnt make any comments at all. Instead I had a bath. And as I soaked in the steaming water, alterD8 came to me. A space where faces, photos, names and locations would not place limits on discourse and where giving voice to desires would not affect offline relationships.

    Thrill yourself. Have anonymous adventures. Make authentic connections.

    Now Im inviting you to join me. Lets explore ourselves together. Getting started takes just a few minutes. I want you to have as fun much as I had becoming Dr. Delilah.

    To become who you want to be choose:

    • a name youve always wanted to try on
    • a body, style and appearance that thrills you
    • friends, partners and connections who could be anywhere in the world

    Im going to wear power red. Heels that reach the sky.

    Its easy to build your avatar and connect with others right away. Well walk you right through it.

    P.S. Ill be looking for inspiration amongst the profiles. Intrigue me. See you inside the site.

    Dr. Delilah
