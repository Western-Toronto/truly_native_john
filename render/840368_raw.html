Save Money By Splurging on a Custom Suit? Its True

February 17, 2015 9:53 am Published by Pierre Mohan 4 Comments

Sources: Michael , Beatrice Murch

Imagine this:

You buy a beautiful new suit off the rack. It fits great and gives you a killer silhouette. Whats not to like? Fast forward five years and it suddenly looks not-so-new. Its color is faded and it seems to have lost its shape.

Maybe this has happened to you. Unfortunately, not all suits are built to last.

If youre a price-conscious shopper, those department store sales can look pretty inviting. Sometimes the cheaper option really is a better deal, but when it comes to suits, making an investment in higher quality is ultimately worth it. In the long run, a well-made custom suit can actually save you money. Heres how.

They Are Durable

A good custom suit is perfectly fitted to your unique shape, so the material wont be stretched when you move and sit down. This will prevent pulling around the stitching, which can visibly damage cheaper suits. In fact, custom suits traditionally have reinforced seams for maximum durability.

The highest quality suits also have a full canvas layer . This means there is an extra layer of material hand stitched between the fabric and lining in the jacket. Canvas layers are traditionally made from horsehair, but can also include materials like wool and cotton. Cheaper suit manufacturers generally skip this step, opting to glue or fuse the lining to the fabric instead. Deterioration of the glue over time can lead to unsightly rippling in the chest and lapel areas.

Canvassed suits drape better and will fit perfectly to your body even after multiple wears. Basically, they do a better job of flattering your figure, and they last longer. Its a win-win.

They Are Made to Withstand the Elements

When you get fitted for a custom suit, you will work with the tailor to choose the best materials for your lifestyle and preferences. Wool is a common choice.

Worried about walking in the rain? Need a suit you wont sweat through? No problem. And when you choose from luxury materials, you dont have to worry that they will fade or look dull over time.

They Give You Better Value

With a customsuit, you truly are getting more for each dollar you spend. Yes, your custom suit will last longera lot longerbut it also offers superior comfort and looks great every time you put it on. This is not to mention the time you save by going to just one tailor instead of to 10 different stores. Your time is valuable.

Many people say they feel more comfortable and self-assured in custom suits. If youve just landed a job interview or youre shooting for that promotion at work, knowing you look great might just give you the confidence boost you need.
