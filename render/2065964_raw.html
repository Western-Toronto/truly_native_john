Self service OData feeds from Oracle, DB2, Sybase, Hadoop, Postgres

by Sumit Sarkar | Sep 5, 2014 | OData

From recent consulting projects, Im hearing from both IT and business users interested in OData production from on-premise business systems. This self service need is in contrast to my last article on OData production using the Entity Framework for .NET developers .

The demand is partly due to mobile application development , along with the growing OData ecosystem where were seeing adoption from the likes of Progress Software, Microsoft, IBM, SAP, Tableau Software, Apple, and Salesforce.
