  • Rules

    Submission Period Voting Period Judging Period

    1. ENTER THE CONTEST

    • Submit a video, meme or written story.
    • You can submit as many different entries as you want. It's all about getting shares & votes.
    • The more effort you make, the bigger the prize:

    1. Video Grand Prize: iPhone 5S + more
    2. Meme Grand Prize: Mophie Battery Pack + more
    3. Story Grand Prize: Bose Earbuds + more

    Submit your broken phone story Now >

    2. SHARE THE SHAME

    (over 3 weeks May 5 - 25, 2014)

    • Share your entry within social networks during the submission period.
    • Once you reach a minimum of 50 views, you are in the random draw to win the weekly prize.
    • *Video views will be counted by a combination of views within the contest site and on YouTube.
    • *Views for memes and written stories are only tracked by those who open and view your entry landing page.

    3. GET VOTES TO WIN

    (over 2 weeks May 26 - June 8, 2014)

    • Share for Votes during the Voting Period.
    • Top # of votes per category will be shortlisted for judging period.

    4. JUDGING

    (over 1 week June 9 - 15, 2014)

    • Judges score the finalists from the Top 5 Voted in each category.
    • Judges criteria: Creativity/Humour, the story, relevance, popularity/sharing, overall Impression.
    • 1 Grand Prize winner per category determined by votes & judges scores.

    5. WINNERS

    (Announced week of June 16)

    • Prize winners will be announced on campaign page, Facebook, Twitter and Google+, as well as be notified by email with instructions on how to complete the claim process.

      • Other Regulations

      • Must be 18 years or older to enter the contest.
      • Must have a valid United States / Canada mailing address to be eligible to claim a price.
      • Contest starts on May 5th, 2014 continues until June 15th, 2014 at which time it will be renewed, altered or ended.
      • Changes to the contest will be announced on wondershare.com campaign page.
      • Submissions MUST be original works of entrants to qualify.
      • Submission of content constitutes consent for use by Wondershare Technology Inc. with no recourse for compensation.
      • Wondershare.com reserves the right to disqualify any contestants for any reason and at its sole discretion. No inappropriate content will be tolerated and will be deleted immediately.

      Check the full story of the My Broken Phone with Dr.Fone contest in Wondershare News>
