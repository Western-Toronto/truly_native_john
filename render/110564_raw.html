WP-120 Waterpik Ultra Water Flosser

Where to Buy

The Easy and More Effective Way to Floss

Works with a shaver socket!

The Waterpik Ultra Water Flosser features state-of-the-art water flossing technology and provides the ultimate in performance.

It includes ten pressure settings, a contemporary design that is smaller and quieter than previous models, a covered reservoir with in-lid tip storage, and six unique tips. And the electric Ultra Water Flosser features a two-pin plug for use with a UK shaver socket.

The Waterpik Water Flosser is clinically proven to be more effective than traditional dental floss and Sonicare Air Floss (Model HX8181) for reducing plaque and gingivitis. And it's perfect for anyone with braces or implants , and those with crowns, bridges, and veneers .
