Day 24 Migraine and Headache Awareness Month Blog Challenge

Today is the 24th day of Migraine and Headache Awareness Month and Day 24 of our MHAM Blogging Challenge.

Welcome!

Today's MHAM Blogging Challenge is:

"Fighter" Have your Migraines or Headaches made you a fighter? : Watch the Christina Aguilera video below and listen to the song lyrics. How have your Migraines or Headaches made you a fighter?

If you want to embed the video in your post, it's available on YouTube.

About the Blogging Challenge:

  • Each day of Migraine and Headache Awareness Month, we'll post a new prompt like today's above.
  • The challenge is for bloggers to blog about the topic on their blogs.
  • THEN come back to the day's blog here and post a link to theirs in the comments for the day's post.
  • The next day, we'll Tweet the links people have posted.
  • If you're on Twitter, please post both the URL for you blog post AND your Twitter ID.
  • Please use the Twitter hashtag #MHAMBC for the blogging challenge and #MHAM for Awareness Month.
  • When you go back to the prompt, please take some time to tweet what others have blogged. You'll also be able to retweet them from @AHMAOrg or @TRobert.

At the bottom of each post you write for the challenge, please add:

"The Migraine and Headache Awareness Month Blog Challenge is organized by the American Headache and Migraine Association ."

You can find a list of the month's blogging challenge prompts on our Awareness Month page .

Headache and Migraine Patient Conference Days Away

Our annual conference for people who have Cluster Headaches, Migraine, and all other Headache disorders, as well as their family and friends is just days away.

About the Conference:

When : Sunday, June 21.

Where : Washington, D.C., at the Grand Hyatt

Conference Sessions Include:

  • The Oversensitive Brain
  • Medication Overuse Headache
  • A Scientific Approach to the Management of Chronic Migraine
  • Support and Information for Family and Friends
  • Patient Involvement in Research
  • What We Should All Know About Cluster Headache
  • Building Productive Patient/Doctor Partnerships
  • Panel Discussion / Q&A for topics not discussed in other sessions.

A buffet lunch is included, and care has been taken to provide a range of foods so participants can avoid food triggers.

Because fragrances of any kind can be strong Migraine triggers, this is a fragrance-free conference.

Please do not wear perfume, cologne, after shave, or any scented product.

For more information, or to register,
see our conference page .
