How can you create additional space for your lifestyle and belongings when you live in a small dwelling ? Simple, just whip up more room . To get you inspired we roundedup our favorite space-making Ikea hacks . These stylish ideas will show you how to maximize your living and storage square footage so you can pack more life into your home.

1. A Murphy Bed That's Also a Desk

Problem: Your home office needs to moonlight as a bedroom.

Solution: You need a Murphy Bed Desk but the ones sold in stores can cost $5,000 or more. Vanessa from Vanessa Shaffer Designs and her hubby saved a bundle by building their ownout of plywood and Ikea table parts.

Whywelike: Its a double-duty solution that maximizes floor space.

Theicingonthecake? Its practical and stylish. Here's the tutorial.

2. Build a Small Space Sofa

Problem: You re having a hard time finding an affordable sofa that will fit in your shoebox-sized home.

Solution: Hack one out of a children s bed frame . Gabriella the retro-fantastic blogger behind Sewing is Hard transformed an Ikea GULLIVER bed frame into a charming sofa and get this, the project cost less than $250!

Whywelike: It s super easy to DIY and its awesome price tag comes with bragging rights .

Theicingonthecake? The pretty upholstery was created out of a $25 Ikea duvet cover . Here's the tutorial.

3. A Console That Expands into a Dining Table

Problem: You wish you had a dining table that would disappear when not needed.

Solution: Build a double-dutyconsole. Chiara,an Ikeaaficionado, created an expandable dining table out of two slim MALM tables.

Whywelike: When the table is folded, it can be used as aconsole,desk or an entrywaylanding strip. When its time to entertain, it can be expanded into a dining table that seats up to 10 people.

Theicingonthecake? While this hack seems complicated to DIY,it's not. To put together all you need is apower drill and a couple of long hinges. The tutorial is posted on Ikea Hackers.

4. Transform Dead Space into Shoe Storage

Problem: You need whip up spaceforshoe storage in a cramped foyer.

Solution: Upcycle oldtables. When the mysterious M who pens the site Redesigned by M couldnt find a shoe storage system for her abode's entryway,she repurposed three Ikea LACK tables.

Whywelike: The project kept three tables out of the landfill and it took less than two hours to build.

Theicingonthecake? Thehack also creates a convenient spot to parkhouse keys, mail, and grab and go accessories like hats and sunglasses.Here's the tutorial.

5. An Eye-Popping Book Display

Problem: You re looking for an organized and visually appealing way to display your book collection.

Solution: Ikeas TROFAST storage boxes are usually used to store toys and assorted odds and ends . Rosandra , the crafty design enthusiast behind Mommo Design, used them to create an organized home library that includes built-i n lighting .

Whywelike: This floor to ceiling idea allows books to shine while avoiding the visual clutter a standard bookcase can create .

Theicingonthecake? Rosandra created built-in lighting by putting LED light into closedTROFAST boxes. This gives the book display a bright and an airy appearance. Here's the tutorial.
