This Super Mario BART Map Makes Public Transit Almost Fun Again

San Francisco
Jenna Homen December 10, 2014 0

What happens whenSuper Mario takes over theBay Area Rapid Transit system? The coolest map ever, thats what.

Chicago artist Robert Bacon has turned the Bay Area into a worldstraight out of Super Marios Mushroom Kingdom, except instead of rescuing Princess Peach from Bowser, your real-life missionis tosnag a seat and avoid BART germs.

Complete with pixel versions of the Golden Gate Bridge, Alcatraz, and the USS Thompson (dont fret, Redditors complained before you had a chance to and its going to be moved to its proper location on the poster), this map makes public transportation fun! Well, almost.


You can view a larger version and/or print yourself off a copy here , or order a bigger poster online .

Check out all of Bacons maps here others include New Yorks subway system and Chicagos L Train. You can also follow him on Twitter too!
