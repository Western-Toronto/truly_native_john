schedule an Appointment

Please use the form below or call us at 1.800.691.0952 to schedule your personalized one
hour visit with a jewelry specialist in our San Francisco or Los Angeles showroom. We recommend
you make your appointment at least three business days in advance and specify the items that you
would like to see during your visit. Please note that the full Brilliant Earth collection may not be
available for view at each location.
