by John Sichel | Last Updated Jun. 9th, 2015

  • Tweet
  • Tweet

Whether you are making a New Years resolution to live healthier, a doctor has suggested you make some lifestyle changes, or you are tired of being tired, there are thousands of apps available to help you achieve your health and wellness goals. From intense workouts jammed into just a few minutes to meditation exercises, there is an app available in Google Play or the App Store to guide you to a healthier, more well you. The problem is, you could spend hours finding just the right app for your situation.

To help you on your health and wellness journey, we have scoured the Android and iOS app world for apps thatreceived high marks or awards from users and industry experts. We have included only those health and wellness apps that rate at three stars or higher, and we focused a great deal of attention on apps that have been reviewed by scoresof users. To make our list, the apps also have to have simple interfaces, long lists of practical features, and quality images, illustrations, and/or audio. Youll find we have included apps for all levels of fitness, from beginner to expert, and we even threw in a couple that make getting healthy fun and exciting. So, start browsing and choose the apps that are best suited to the first steps of your health and wellness journey.

Please note, these apps are listed in no particular order.
