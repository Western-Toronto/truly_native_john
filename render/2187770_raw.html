Prepare to be creeped out by how Zeds Dead are launching their new single

Thu 22nd May, 2014 by Katie Cunningham in News

You can find some weird shit in the Craigslist personal sections. In amongst the the usual NSFW adverts, theres Avicii concert-goers trying to reconnect with the potential soul mate they met in the VIP section, jilted ex-lovers pouring their hearts out anonymously and one guy looking to purchase your worn, dirty socks (for real!). Or if you really dig, you might just find the latest Zeds Dead single.

It sounds weird, but we think DC and Hooks are teasing their new track on Americas most notorious classifieds website. If you head to the missed connections section of Toronto Craiglist now, youll spot an ad unearthed by Reddit , naturally filled with what could very well be some lovelorn song lyrics. Email the poster and youll be sent back a link to the video below.

The 39-second long clip is soundtracked by part of a song weve never heard before and features illustrations of what looks like Hooks and DCs facesand towards the end of the video youll see the text Zeds Dead x Twin Shadow briefly reflected in a lake. So, yes: it seems like the Toronto duo (yep, theyre from the same city this ad was posted to) have collaborated with Golden Light hitmaker Twin Shadow.

If the track is indeed the work of Zeds Dead, its certainly a change of pace for the pair perhaps thanks in part to Twin Shadow, the song leans a lot more towards house than it does the duos usual bass rumbles. So far, theyve kept quiet about the new collab on social media, but you can make your own mind up below.
