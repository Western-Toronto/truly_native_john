Whether youre taking a weekend trip nearby or spending a few weeks travelling the globe, youll need to create a budget for your holiday. Once you have chosen a destination and length of stay, youll have to decide how youre going to get there and what youre planning to do. To help you travel for cheap, we have 5 simple steps on getting the most out of your budget.

Infographic Roundup

Step 1

Save, Save, Save

If possible, save as much as you can before you depart. This will increase your budget.

Travel Tidbit: Open a savings account specifically for your travels.

Step 2

Transportation: Planes vs. Trains

Once you have narrowed down your destination, you have to research the best way to get there.

Planes and trains are the two most common ways to travel in the UK. But did you know that its cheaper to fly than take the train 90% of the time? 1

Ditch the train fare and book a flight!

Travel Tidbit: If you have to rent a vehicle while youre there, visit website PetrolPrices.com to calculate fuel expenses.

Step 3

Lodging: Hotels vs. Hostels

Other than airfare, lodging is usually the most expensive cost. However, if youre on a budget, theres no need to book a fancy hotel.

Instead, you can stay at hostels throughout the UK for as low as 9 per night! 2

Travel Tidbit: Book a hotel or hostel that offers a free breakfast thats one less meal you will have to pay for!

Step 4

Food & Drinks

You dont need to dine out for every meal just because youre travelling! So when you arrive at your destination, find the nearest supermarket and stock up on snacks and pre-made meals. Store them in your room and pack them in your bag while youre on the go.

Travel Tidbit: Since youre on holiday, you will want to try the local cuisine where youre visiting. Choose one restaurant a day to splurge!

Step 5

Entertainment

Before arriving at your destination, look up the prices of museums or parks that youre interested in visiting. If possible, plan your trip around when they have free admission! Visit Groupon.co.uk for cheap activities!

Travel Tidbit: Create a daily allowance. Set aside money everyday in individual envelopes to enjoy your trip to the fullest.
