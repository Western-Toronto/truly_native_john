App Review: OKDOTHIS

February 18, 2015 Kelly Photo Intern

Regardless of whether youre a professional photographer or an amateur, we all value nice photographs but sometimes lack the motivation and inspiration to create them. OKDOTHIS has a solution for all of us.

OKDOTHIS is a photography social media app created by world-renowned photographer Jeremy Cowart , that allows users to get a real hands-on photo experience. Its broken up into four main components that streamline all the content and make it very easy to navigate.

The discoversection allows users to search through posts from the OKDOTHIS community. This app has users from all over the world so its great exposure for photographers of all skill levels. Trending and recent show the popular DOs of the day as well as those that were just added to the feed.

The DO of the Day is what really sets this app apart from others. Users from the community can post a DO (a challenge for fellow photographers like something beautiful out of focus) then its up to you to go out and get creative. Once youve taken a photo that falls under that category keeping in mind this is all about your interpretation you post it for the world to OK.

As if it wasnt already cool enough, the editing functions are excellent. It offers a very wide range of crops and filters as well as a more detailed editing section where you can change aspects of the photo manually.

The OKDT community is extremely positive and supportive. There isnt any judgement or criticism of you or your skill level its just photo-lovin people lovin on some photos and having a great time doing it.

What we love most about OKDOTHIS is that it champions the idea of creatively using photography to tell a story or answer a question . That is so close to the heart of Fracture and why we love helping people celebrate those beautiful moments and stories and adventures in a physical, lasting way.

This is an app we would encourage every photographer to download. It presents creative challenges in unique and fun ways so coming up with imaginative concepts doesnt seem like such a chore. You can make it even more exciting by inviting your friends and making things a little more competitive.

The best part about all of this is that OKDOTHIS is FREE! For zero dollars you can have all these fantastic features and activities right at your fingertips. So what are you waiting for? You can download OKDOTHIS in the iTunes app store.

Once you have OKDOTHIS set up, be sure to follow drewatfracture where our marketing director Drew Allen is enjoying DOing life with photos!
