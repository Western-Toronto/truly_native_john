Product Description

AN ADVANCED APPETITE SUPPRESSANT & WEIGHT LOSS SUPPORT SUPPLEMENT

A stark truth: The common cause of diseases is the accumulation of waste and poisonous matter (toxins) in the body which results to great extent from overeating. The majority of people eat too much for their overly-sedentary lives, which in turn results in insufficient and improper utilization of this larger quantity of food.

This surplus overburdens the digestive and assimilative organs and clogs up the system with impurities or poisons. Digestion and elimination become slow and the functional activity of the whole system is imbalanced. The news today has become clear eat less, live longer. Caloric restriction is the simple answer to health and longevity.

The key to successful weight control is to take an holistic approach.

APPETITE AWAY is an effective tool to take care of the critical areas of hunger control and metabolic function to facilitate faster weight loss. It is an advanced diet support supplement that helps to reduce hunger, helping lower daily caloric intake, and helps increase metabolic function so your body burns more calories per hour whatever the activity.

It also helps lower cortisol levels, and increase glucose utilization, so your body more readily converts fat stores into energy. And most importantly, it supports the body with key nutrients to protect against stress as you lose weight.

From Scientific American Magazine:

As researchers on aging noted in a position statement this past May, no treatment on the market today has been proved to slow human agingthe buildup of molecular and cellular damage that increases vulnerability to infirmity as we grow older. But one intervention, consumption of a low-calorie yet nutritionally balanced diet, works incredibly well in a broad range ofanimals, increasing longevity and prolonging good health. Those findings suggest that caloric restriction could delay aging in humans, too. for maximum benefit, people would probably have to reduce their caloric intake by roughly 30 percent, equivalent to dropping from 2,500 calories a day to 1,750. (SA, Aug 2002)

A simple fact:

The basic principle of losing weight really does come down to calories in versus calories out how many calories you consume per day and then how well your body metabolizes or burns up those calories (so how active you are and how efficiently your body functions.) So if we could stimulate your metabolism to burn extra calories per day, and then help reduce daily caloric intake, this is a weight loss program bound for success.

Simple but effective.

Doctors, dieticians, and other experts all agree that the best way to lose weight fast is to eat fewer calories and increase your metabolism so you burn more energy. APPETITE AWAY is an advanced weight control support supplement to decrease hunger, raise energy usage & production, burn fat for faster weight loss, and thereby help achieve ideal weight as well as body image.

The long and the short of it is caloric restriction is the primary path to wellness and longevity.
