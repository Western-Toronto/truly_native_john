Part of our practice includes skin care rejuvenation services to help our patients look better. We work with our patients to find the best combination of services and products to help them look their best.

Facial Peels . The VI peel will:

  • Improve the tone, texture and clarity of the skin
  • Reduce or eliminate age spots, freckles and hyperpigmentation, including melasma
  • Reduce the appearance of pore size
  • Soften lines and wrinkles
  • Stimulate the production of collagen and elastin for firmer skin
  • Clear acne skin conditions

Botox Cosmetic (onabotulinumtoxinA) is a prescription medicine that is injected into muscles to temporarily treat moderate to sever frown lines between the brows of adults 18 to 65 years of age. It works by blocking nerve impulses to the injected muscles. This reduces muscle activity that causes those lines to form between the brows.

Dysport is a prescription injection used to temporarily improve the look of your moderate to severe frown lines without changing the look of your whole face. The untreated facial muscles still work normally, allowing you to freely show facial expressions, such as smiling, in untreated areas. Dysport is a formulation that has been shown to deliver temporary improvement in moderate to severe frown lines, even with repeat treatments. So, the effect of repeat treatment with Dysport may be comparable to your first use.

Juvederm XC injectable gel instantly smoothes parentheses lines along the sides of your nose and mouth and lasts up to 1 year with only 1 treatment. Juvederm XC injectable gel treatment is customizable to ensure smooth, natural-looking results. If you are pregnant, trying to get pregnant, breastfeeding or are under 18 years of age, you should not use Juvederm or other injectables.

Perlane can add volume and fullness to the skin to correct moderate to severe facial wrinkles and folds, such as the lines from your nose to the corners of your mouth. Perlane is made of hyaluronic acid, a substance that is naturally present in your skin but diminishes as you get older, causing your skin to lose volume and form wrinkles and folds. The hyaluronic acid in perlane is specifically formulated to act like your own natural hyaluronic acid and replace lost volume for a more youthful appearance. If you are pregnant, trying to get pregnant, breastfeeding or are under 18 years of age, you should not use Perlane or other injectables.
