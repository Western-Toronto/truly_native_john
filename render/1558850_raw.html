LIVE LISTENING PARTY @ Musideum

February 21st, 2014 / 8pm SHARP / Musideum, 401 Richmond W, Suite 133, Toronto, ON
BRAND NEW SONGS
Vote For Your Favourites
AND WE'LL RECORD THE TOP 3

We've been writing up a storm and are eager to get back to recording again, but which songs should we choose? That's where you come in! :) On February 21st, we'll be throwing a Live Listening Party at Musideum in Toronto for a small audience of friends who would like to come hear us perform our new tunes, provide feedback on each of the songs, and vote for their favourites to be recorded.

TICKETS ARE VERY LIMITED and it is recommended that you call Musideum to reserve your seats as soon as possible (416 ) 599-7323 or place an order online here.

If you can't make it out in person, we are recording the show and you can still participate by ordering the MP3 with online voting forms emailed to you or have a CD with paper voting forms snail mailed to you. Just place your order with the buttons on the right :)
