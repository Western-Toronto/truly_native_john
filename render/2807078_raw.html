    Fisherman in Siberia were headed down a river, when their boat bumped into what they figured was just a really big rock. They were shocked when they looked closer and saw this toothy grin!

    Photo Thanks: Wild North Fishing Club

    The fishermen took some pictures and sent them to experts and were told that this was most likely a lizard-like dinosaur from 150 million years ago.

    Paleontologists are now on their way to examine this potentially very significant discovery.

    So what do you think this is? An ancient crocodile? A dinosaur? Or just a rock?
