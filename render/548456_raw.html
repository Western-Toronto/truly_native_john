Football Frenzy Evening

Lachezar Stamatov - 09 Jul 2014 - Team building

Choosing a destination for your next event can be one of the most challenging tasks. Whether you are charged with organised a conference, a team building activity or a corporate day out, half the secret of making your attendees happy is simply selecting the best destination for the purpose. Pick something at random and you risk getting a lot of eye rolls. Pick something backed by good reviews and your event could be a hit.

So lets take a look at some proven and time-tested popular event destinations in the UK:

London

@Doug88888 / Creative Commons Attribution-NonCommercial-ShareAlike 2.0 Generic (CC BY-NC-SA 2.0)

No surprise here. The populous and extremely diverse capital of the UK is peoples first choice when it comes to corporate events.

In preparation to host the 2012 Olympic and Paralympic Games, London saw a strong boost of city-wide improvements. A lot of them affect the event planning industry transportation is easier and faster than ever, there are lots of new accommodations to suit every taste, and theres an ongoing effort to provide new venues and breathe new life into old ones.

Add to all this the fact that London is the best of both worlds: it is one of the most history-rich cities, while being a popular hub for modern ideas. Doing a team building event in London will leave no one wondering what to do in their free time.

But nothing makes a stronger case for London as a top events destination, as much as this inforgraphic by London & Partners . According to it, London has:

  • more unique spaces than anywhere else in the world
  • over 1,000 venues
  • 116,000 hotel rooms
  • lots of expert speakers, thanks to its 45 universities and colleges

Moreover, if you organise a team building in London, youll have plenty of exciting activities to choose from. Our top pick? The Italian Job Mini Adventure, where attendees drive around London in Minis aiming to complete a top secret mission.

If something could be said against London as an event destination, it would be that the city can be a bit pricey compared to the rest of the UK. And even though it is a large city with good infrastructure, it can be a bit overcrowded and require some extra planning.

Birmingham

Birmingham is not only the second largest city after London, its also the second most popular destination outside London for meetings, conferences and events, according to last years British Meetings & Events Industry Survey (BMEIS) .

The survey is based on the responses of more than 600 event planners and organisers, from both the corporate and the non-profit sector. According to Ian Taylor from Marketing Birmingham , business tourism has played a major role in setting a record number of visitors to Birmingham in 2012 33.8 million in total.

So why choose Birmingham for your next event? Birmingham provides a bustling nightlife and many cultural activities, such as museums and galleries , thatll keep your attendees engaged even after the indoor team building portion of the event is over.

In fact, a lot of these cultural venues are available for hire as team building venues, as well. Some out-of-the-box event venues include the Birmingham Botanical Gardens or the National Motorcycle Museum . If you are looking for something even more creative, be sure to check out the Custard Factory .

Birmingham is currently seeing another round of improvements that will benefit the events industry. When completed, youll be able to add Resorts World Birmingham, abig leisure and entertainment center, to your list of possible venues.

Manchester


pmorgan / Creative Commons Attribution-NonCommercial-NoDerivs 2.0 Generic (CC BY-NC-ND 2.0)

Manchester, another UK city laden with history, is a worthy rival to Birmingham when it comes to planning great events.

If you decide to organise your conference or team building in Manchester, you will quickly find that the city offers some unique benefits of its own. For example, despite being a large city, Manchester has quick access to the magnificent Cheshire area . This is a great opportunity if you want to include outdoor activities in your event. Alternatively, you can do sailing activities along the 36-mile Manchester Ship Canal .

Manchester is not Englands second largest city (in fact, its the ninth largest), but polls actually show that in peoples perception it is Englands second city. That can largely be attributed to Manchesters rich cultural life. Manchester has been known for a while for its legendary music scene, giving birth to world-renowned bands such as Oasis and Joy Divison.

Manchester has a well-developed infrastructure, making it an easy destination to reach by all means of transportation. When it comes to finding a venue, the city has a lot to offer. The Bridgewater Hall is popular for music and art events, as well as conferences, while the Imperial War Museum North and the Manchester Art Gallery are great venues for naturally infusing a little culture into your event.

Quite importantly, Manchester is the birthplace of the popular BBC show Its a Knockout, which has turned into one of the most popular team building activities in the UK. Why not pay Manchester a tribute by including the activity as part of your team building event?

Nottingham

Situated at the heart of the UK, Nottingham is another great option to consider, especially if your delegates are hailing from different parts of the country.

Nottingham has a well-developed public transportation system, although its not always necessary as the city is compact and much of it can be seen on foot. But a great location and good infrastructure is far from everything that makes Nottingham an attractive event destination . The city has been awarded a Purple Flag , an accreditation for town centres that meet or surpass the standards of excellence in managing the evening and night-time economy.

Nottingham is also a great place to impress people with history and culture. It is the birthplace of Robin Hood, D. H. Lawrence and Paul Smith. It is also home to the oldest pub in the UK Ye Olde Trip to Jerusalem . Other places of interest include the Nottingham Castle and the Galleries of Justice .

If you are organising a team building in Nottingham, you can take advantage of the citys great venues, such as Albert Hall or the Nottingham Forest Football Club . If planning an outdoor activity, be sure to consider the magnificent Sherwood Forest.

Glasgow

Foter / Creative Commons Attribution 2.0 Generic (CC BY 2.0)

Last but certainly not least in this list is the Scottish city of Glasgow, the largest city in Scotland and the fourth largest in the whole of the UK. The city is visited by 2.2 million people each year, of whom 750,000 come specifically for an event.

Whatever the nature or size of your event, Glasgow has got you covered. The city has been known for a while for its art and design scene, so it easily lends itself to cultural events. The city has even been chosen to host the prestigious Turner Prize in 2015 .

Sports and outdoor activities are also easy to plan, since Glasgow has been voted among the top 10 sporting cities in the world in 2012 and continues to rank high today. This year the popular Commonwealth Games will be held in Glasgow.

As for night life, the city has plenty of cafes, bars and close to a 1,000 restaurants to choose from. A great activity to try when organising a team building in Glasgow is wine tasting .

But theres one thing that makes Glasgow particularly stand out as a compelling destination for events. Its called the Major Events Charter . The Charter was adopted in 2010 in an effort to make Glasgow a more attractive destination for events of all kinds. Adopting the charter, the city commits itself to help event organisers in finding and securing the best venue, achieving the goals they set and easily accessing Glasgows business networks. It will also help you find the best accommodation rates and transportation solutions.

Concluding Remarks

All five of these cities provide spot-on solutions to meet the needs of event organisers of all kinds. Whether you are an HR manager organising a team building event or an event organiser holding a conference, these places will help you hold an event your attendees will remember.
