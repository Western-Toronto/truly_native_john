  • Introducing the New FollowUp.cc Chrome Extension for Gmail

    Kevin is all-seeing.Dont you wish you were Kevin?

    You could be. With the new version of FollowUp.ccfor Chrome , you can track your Gmail to see who opened your messages, and when. That means you can follow up with contactsand prospectsat just the right moment to keep relationships strong and deals moving toward a close.

    In other words, you can rule.

    Heres what new.

    Open Tracking. Now you can ensure that your emails have been received and opened. It is as simple as a click.

    Side Window. When your email is opened, you will see the action in your new side window. If you dont want a side window, it can collapse completely.

    New Reminder Calendar. Set Follow Up reminders with a click. If you like the existing dropdown menu for setting reminders, it is still there. And you can always use keystrokes to set recurring reminders and set tasks.

    Send Later. Love to work at midnight, but want to make sure your email is delivered at 8am? No problem - you can now schedule emails to be sent at any time with Send Later.

    And as always, Webpage Bookmarking and Sharing. When you see a great article that you want to read later or share with a friend, click on the FollowUp.cc Chrome icon and easily set your reminder.

    Some of these feature are only available on Basic and Pro plans, but we wanted to give everyone a chance to try them out. So enjoy then and let us know if you find them helpful. We love getting your feedback and making FollowUp.cc better for you. :)
