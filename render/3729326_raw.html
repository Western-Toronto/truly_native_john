Fall is here. Is there actually a better way to serve drinks at a party than out of a pumpkin? This DIY will get you there.

Materials Needed:

  • Pumpkin
  • Beverage to fill the pumpkin
  • Pumpkin cleaning gear (knives, spoons, trash can, over-sized plastic ponchos they sell to tourists on rainy days at Disney World)
  • Plastic spigot
  • Dry ice for added effect (if desired)

The Process:

Wash the outside of the pumpkin with dish soap, then cut around the top as if youre planning to make a jack-o-lantern. Clean out the inside of the pumpkin, taking care not to get any on your party clothes (hence the ridiculous poncho). Rinse several times to eliminate any remaining pumpkin goo. Place plastic spigot in pumpkin. Fill with your favorite beverage. Add dry ice if desired. Enjoy the party with your pumpkin drink dispenser!
