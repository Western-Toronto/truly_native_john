Science Kit

Science Kit

Kids love science experiments.

FLUX series gives them the gift of discovery with a Science Kit that engages their curiosity and sparks a lifelong love for learning. Gain hands-on science experience with a Microlens, a Thermal probe and mymaga earphones.

Microlens

Microlens

With the Microlens, the webcam also works as a microscope for scientific assignments and supports inquiry-based learning with close-up views. The Microlens operates as a low-powered microscope, allowing students to examine things like leaves and bugs up-close.

Thermal probe

Thermal probe

The Thermal probe expands the scope of exploration and allows students to conduct experiments that no other consumer tablet can. It detects fluctuations in ambient temperatures, transferring the data received to the IES platform via audio jack.

Earphones

Earphones

Furthermore, you can also enjoy rich audio with mymaga Earphones! You will have the best of both worlds by listening to your favorite music or using the Text-to-Speech function to listen to eBooks or simply study while doing something else. FLUX series is focused on designing tools that bring learning to life, helping young learners become more engaged.
