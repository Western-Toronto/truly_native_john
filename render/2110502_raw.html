How can Push Monkey help?

Zero coding required

Fast and easy to set up and no coding or API configuration required. The monkeys do all the pushing so you can focus on your content and readers.

Stats

Beautiful and easy-to-understand statistics for the Push Monkey performance are available directly in your Wordpress Dashboard

Automatic

Sent automatically when you publish a new post. Or not. Its your choice, thanks to granular filtering

Why Push Monkey?



Increased Engagement

Readers can be informed about your content at all times: when reading other websites or while working in other apps, with the browser closed. Even when OS X is not active. The moment OS X wakes up, it displays the missed notifications.


No Monkey Business

Sending notifications on a big and reliable scale is not trivial, but our solid high-availability cloud back-end delivered millions of notifications...and it's not stopping here.

Native outreach

The readers don't have to install any additional apps or plugins. Readers just accept receiving notifications from your website and voil!


Advanced Granular Filtering

You can select which post categories or custom post types don't send push notifications, as easy as you can say ba-na-na. No spam around here!


CTA Banners

Even though this feature is supported only on Safari right now, your other readers coming from OS X are not left out. Chrome or Firefox readers Will see a customisable banner, informing them about Desktop Push Notifications and how to subscribe.

Non-intrusive

Push notifications reach a sweet balance between informative and non-intrusive. The notifications appear above all other windows and if not clicked on, it disappears after a few seconds.


Visually branded for your needs

Push Monkey is fully white-label. Your reader don't get to see any crazy bananas from us. Your logo, your text, your rules!
