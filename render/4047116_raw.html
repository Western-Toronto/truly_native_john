Key Lime Pie Green Juice

0 Comments

Green juice. It's SO popular! And, rightly so; it's an uber-shot of healthiness to your body.

Ever have Key Lime Pie flavored green juice???

Try it! It's a Floridian vacation in a glass! Errr... Mason jar!

Recipe:

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup of chilled or frozen green grapes
  • 1 cup of spinach
  • 1 1/2 cups water
  • 1/2 t vanilla extract
  • 3 drops doTERRA lime essential oil
To make your Key Lime Pie Green Juice, all you have to do is spin all of the ingredients together in a high-powered blender. So easy!

Feisty Fiesta Casserole

6/7/2015

0 Comments

Hola!

For some reason, Summer is the best season to eat Mexican-inspired food. Know what I mean? It's like, "Oh, it's June? Time to shove Mexican food in my mouth repeatedly!". Heeheehee :)

Next time a Mexican food craving hits you, try this Feisty Fiesta Casserole. It won't let you down!

It's delicious.

It's packed with healthy fiber.

It's vegan.

It gives you a flavorful dose of doTERRA's lime essential oil. (Yes, you can bake with certain doTERRA essential oils! How neat is that?!?!)*

And, it's FEISTY!

Recipe:

Ingredients:
  • 15 oz of refried beans
  • 1 standard-sized packet of generic taco seasoning
  • doTERRA Lime essential oil
  • onion powder
  • chilli powder
  • garlic powder
  • 2 cups of frozen broccoli
  • 6-8 small red potatoes
  • jalapeo slices (I used jarred)
  • 12 oz of diced tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup almond milk
  • salt
  • 1 T olive oil
Preheat oven to 375 degrees Fahrenheit.
Boil potatoes in lightly salted water until tender, (about 20 minutes). Drain potatoes then mash with olive oil, 1/2 the packet of taco seasoning, 2-3 drops of lime essential oil, & 1/8 t salt. Reserve.

In a blender, blend your diced tomatoes, milk, 5-6 slices of jalapeo , 1 t onion powder, 1/2 t garlic powder, 1 t chilli powder, 2 drops of lime essential oil, & dash of salt. Reserve.

Now, mix your refried beans with the remainder of your taco seasoning. In an 8x8 glass baking dish, spread the refried beans. Layer the frozen broccoli on top of the refried beans, then layer your mashed potato mixture on top of the broccoli. Drizzle your reserved Fiesta-inspired tomato concoction over the layer of potatoes. Finally, if you'd like your casserole VERY feisty, top your assembled casserole with a few slices of jalapeo. Bake for 30 minutes.

Once baked, eat a yummy slice of your Feisty Fiesta Casserole!

Slim & Sassy Sunshine Smoothie

2/27/2015

0 Comments

It's still Winter. Snow. Ice. Freezing, FREEZING, FREEZING(!!) temperatures.

Because of this, I'm posting my new favorite Slim & Sassy Sunshine Smoothie recipe. It's the perfect pick-me-up for a day like today. It's invigorating, slimming, nutritious, & will make you happppppyyyyyy.


Recipe:

  • 1/4 cup raw almonds
  • 1 1/2 cups water
  • 1 serving of Slim & Sassy protein powder *
  • 2 peeled & chopped carrots
  • 1/4 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1 packet of stevia (or about 1/4 of a teaspoon)
  • 1 frozen banana
  • 1/2 teaspoon of vanilla extract

How to make it? Throw the almonds & the water into your blender & whirl until the almonds have broken down to create an almond milk. Now, add the rest of the ingredients & whirl again. Pour the smoothie into a mason jar. Then, sit by a sunny window & drink up! Winter blahs cured.

You can purchase the Slim & Sassy protein powder here: www.mydoterra.com/oilystephanie

What's Slim & Sassy protein powder? It's made by doTERRA & is pretty awesome. It has protein, fiber, & ashwagandha in it. Ashwagandha is an herb that suppresses appetite & aids in metabolism function. Pretty neat, right? :)
