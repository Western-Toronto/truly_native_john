About VIP Cars

VIP Cars is an international brand that has successfully established itself within the heart of rental seekers, across the world, with its magnificent booking system that is accessible online as well as via mobile app. We give you a brilliant car renting platform designed & customized to make rental booking not only easy & swift, but also user friendly for all type of customers.

Our intelligible booking engine has been built keeping 'ease' and 'search usability' in mind, thus you can find your car based on either location preference, budget preference or the car size/features.

Our Suppliers

We, VIP Cars, take immense pride in being associated with some of the biggest, most reputed and well established rental providers worldwide. Our wagon of car hire suppliers has more than 500, big and small, brands that have been known for their dedication & contribution in car renting industry. From Avis, National, Budget, Dollar, Thrifty, Europcar, Sixt, Alamo, Hertz to Enterprise, there are many popular and recognizable names we work with to bring best of car hire services to you, including top cars, best locations and low cost deals.

Our collaboration with such esteemed car hire suppliers is how we assure you of lowest deals and best rental quotes, for every corner of the globe.

Location Coverage

Our network of rental providers located across the globe has given us the opportunity to bring to you as many pick-up, & drop-off, locations as possible. We have listed over 20,000 car rental locations for you to select, including every major international and domestic airport, popular train stations, important city squares, streets, downtown areas as well as metropolitan and suburban cities.

Rental Cars

Working with best in rental industry has its own benefits and one of such added advantage is the car fleet. Our suppliers believe that every rental seeker has its own need when it comes to booking a car, thus they cover all the car major segments to serve both economical and luxury rental bookings. When searching a car with us, you get to enjoy an impressive fleet of mini, compact, intermediate, vans, SUVs and sedans to choose from. From automatic transmission to manual transmission, there is a vehicle for every type of driver.

Booking process

The well crafted booking engine we take pride in gives you simple 3-step booking system:
"Select - Search - Book".

  • There is a gigantic collection of countries to select from; once selected you now enjoy luxury to choose your pick-up and drop off location as per your itinerary comfort and convenience.
  • It doesn't matter from which country you are from; the multicurrency option keeps you close to your bank.
  • Once you have selected the location, you get access to cars available as per your query details like time, date and location.
  • Our booking engine lets you find cars in order from low-priced to high-priced or vice versa, as well as on the basis of segment options like SUVs only, mini only etc. If that's not enough, there is a search option available for those seeking only air conditioned cars or automatic or manual transmission vehicles.
  • Every rental booking made with VIP Cars is guarded by extremely secured payment portal. Transactions are protected with SSL encryption, thus leaving no place for any security concern.
