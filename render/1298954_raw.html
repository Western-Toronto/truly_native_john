15 Great Fitness Gifts for the Holidays 2013 Edition

The holidays are a great time to bestow friends and loved ones with awesome gifts and what better than to get them something that will help contribute to their health and fitness. Here is my list of 15 great fitness gifts to consider for the fitness buff in your life:

1) VibraRoll
All the benefits of a foam roller with the addition of vibration for even deeper relaxation. You can use this foam roller with or without vibration if you prefer a more traditional experience.

2) Valslide
The Valslide discs can be used for a variety of sliding exercising from simulating gliding on a slide board to using them as a booster for ab training.

3) Power Wheel
Used to enhance your core training, the Power Wheel can be used with your hands or feet for a variety of challenging ab and core work.

4) The Equalizer
A set of Equalizer bars can take your workouts from boring to fun. Use them as dip bars, push up bars, stretching tools and more.

5) TRX Home Suspension Training Kit
This home TRX kit allows you to get a great suspension workout from the comfort of your home and includes 6 digital workouts to get you started.

6) Travel Bands
Travel Bands are the perfect exercise tool to carry on the go. Pack them into your suitcase for lightweight resistance training anywhere.

7) Superbands
Superbands can be taken on the go just like the Travel Bands. Easy to pack and carry, they offer resistance and flexibility training in a range of difference weight tensions.

8) Aeromat Fitness Mat
The Areomat Fitness Mat is the perfect companion to any floor exercise. It offers a comfortable amount of cushion without creating an unbalanced surface. Use the Areomat Fitness Mat for yoga, Pilates, stretching or any other exercise where you need a little cushion on the floor.

9) Spikey Ball
Spikey Ball is the perfect companion for post-exercise or rehab work. Use it for SMR exercises or general massage. The spiked texture is a great stimulator for the bottoms of your feet or other tired, sore areas.

10) Tiger Tail Massager
The Tiger Tail Massager is another great tool for post-exercise or rehab work. The hard surface is perfect for working out knots and sore spots in your muscles.

11) Stackable Steps
Stackable Steps are an indespensible tool for aerobic step work, plyometric jump work and your Cross Fit WOD. These steps can also double as a platform for raised push-ups, dips and other body weight workouts.

12) Magic Speed Jump Rope
Made with a swivel-bearing system in the handles the Magic Speed Jump Rope is the perfect tool for a serious cardio workout. Use your jump rope at home, outside at the gym or take it on the road for travel.

13) ABC Agility Ladder
When youre looking to improve your speed and agility theres no better tool than an agility ladder. Use it for agility, balance and coordination training through running, jumping, walking and hoping.

14) Battling Ropes
Battling ropes are a hot trend in getting ripped. These battle ropes offer weight and resistance for cardio and strength training in a new and exciting way. Use them at home, outside or at the gym.

15) Bodyblade
The Bodyblade offers variable resistance in an oscillating bar for a great core and upper body workout. This new trend in fitness uses the power of vibrations to help develop stability. This tool is being used in gyms across the country, but you can join in on the fun from the comfort of your home.

This list of 15 great fitness gifts for the holidays includes many of my favorite fitness and exercise tools. I hope you find something on this list for the special someone in your life that loves to exercise!
