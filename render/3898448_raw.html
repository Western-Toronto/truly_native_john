The Ultimate Fantasy Premier League App

The unrivaled Fantasy Football app for players of the official Barclays Fantasy Premier League (FPL) game. Makes managing your team on the go a breeze and ensures you'll never forget to make those last minute transfers wherever you are. Packed full of features, news, tips & tricks that will ensure you get the edge over your managerial rivals and climb to the top of your mini leagues.

Download now for iPhone

Features

Manage

Drag and drop management of your team. Full transfer functionality including wildcards. Latest points for the current and previous gameweeks.

Leagues

All of your important mini-leagues accessible in one place. See how you are doing compared to your FPL managerial rivals.

Players

Full player database search. Interactive filtering of players ensures finding the next transfer target is effortless. Suspension and Injuries default views. Add any player to your personal watchlist.

Fixtures

Full fixtures and results built in to the app. Watch results come in on matchday and see who has scored those all important points. View of next 3 fixtures for all teams facilitates careful transfer selection.

News

Get all the latest team news, tips and tricks from a selection of the best fantasy football resources from around the world.

Social

Full Twitter and Facebook integration. Ask your followers to rate your team for the upcoming gamweek. Gloat or commiserate about your gameweek score.
