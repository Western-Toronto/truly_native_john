Top 10 Smart Watches Gift Ideas for this Christmas

Best SmartWatches Gift Ideas

Since Pebble was launched in 2013, a smartwatch revolution has started, and today almost every smartphone brand has its own model on the market.

Much more than just the common functions of a classic watch, a smartwatch can be connected to your Smartphone or tablet and give you access to hundreds of new features and notifications and they are not only incredibly useful, a new trending appeared in 2014 improving their design and bringing a new contemporary look, proving that fashion is just as important as technology.

Millions of new apps and features are being created for this new gadget and it's clear just the beginning. Smart watches started with similar functions of the activity trackers, with lots of functions and apps for fitness and sports activities, but we are seeing developers announcing new apps every day. With the power to have all their notifications in their wrist, users are now picking up the smartphone far less during the day, having hands-free and even using the watches to make and receive calls accessing all your the information they need using only the voice command.
