The 15 Worst Mountain Bike Products Ever

Here at Dirt we normally like to celebrate the very best mountain bike products, as we recently did in our 15 Things That Have Changed Mountain Biking post, but sometimes to truly appreciate the good things you need to remind yourself of those things that were so bad all you wanted to do was smash them to smithereens. Unfortunately some of the products in question had often already taken care of that themselves.

All of us seem to have our own product loathings, but after a quick chat in the office certain names kept cropping up again and again, so those are the ones that have the dubious honour of making it into our 15 Worst Mountain Bike Products Ever list. We are sure you lot will have some of your own to add to the list, so feel free to put them in the comments section below. But now, without further ado, and in no particular order, here are the products that wed like to consign to room 101
