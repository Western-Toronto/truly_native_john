Jana Kramer

Jana Kramer

You might know Jana Kramer from her role as Alex Dupre on One Tree Hill (20092012). But thanks to the multi-talent's self-titled debut album released last year, the brunette with model looks and a killer songwriting wit to match has quickly emerged as a bona fide country star in her own right. Kramer's first two songs, "Why Ya Wanna" and "Whiskey" premiered on One Tree Hill , and quickly made their way into the Billboard Hot 100. Worry not, OTH fans, Kramer, 29, is still actingshe's appeared in episodes of Grey's Anatomy and CSI: NY . This summer, though, she'll be exclusively on the road opening for The Voice star Blake Shelton on his Ten Times Crazier Tour. Listen for her latest radio single, "I Hope It Rains."

Dustin Lynch

Dustin Lynch

In the two years after he signed with Broken Bow Records, Tennessee native Dustin Lynch wrote more than 200 songs, including his would-be breakout, the slow-burner of a single "Cowboys And Angels," which was later nominated for Single of the Year at the 2012 American Country Awards. The tracks success marked the start of a whirlwind year for the 28-year-old: His eponymous debut album cracked the top 20 on the Billboard 200 album chart, his video for "Cowboys and Angels" was nominated for Breakthrough Video of the Year at the CMT Awards, and next month he hits the road on a nationwide tour with Keith Urban and Little Big Town.
