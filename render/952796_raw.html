12 Red Appliances to Help Brighten Up Your Kitchen

September 30, 2014 by: Jeff Flowers 0

No, its not your imagination. Red is popping up in kitchen-related catalogs, store displays and e-commerce sites everywhere. You probably even have a neighbor or family member with a red appliance in their kitchen.

From deep crimson, ruby and berry to the more fiery hues of chili and scarlet, the options are limitless for those seeking a splash of color to brighten up their kitchen or just make cooking more fun.

Here are some of our favorite red appliances that will make you feel like youre seeing your kitchen through rose-colored glasses!

1. Magic Chef 0.9 Cu. Ft. 900 Watt Microwave

Dont let this compact-size microwave fool you! This hot little number offers 10 power levels, three pre-programmed one-touch cook settings, auto-defrost settings, auto-cook menus and a child-safety lock.

Perfect for dorms, studio apartments and other small living spaces, this red Magic Chef microwave is compact and capable of cooking your favorite meals without taking up too much space.

[ Learn More About This Red Microwave From Magic Chef ]

2. Smeg 9.22 Cu. Ft. Retro Refrigerator

This retro, candy-apple red fridge is a gorgeous piece of art! With a high-gloss finish and sleek, rounded edges, its beautiful on the outside and surprisingly spacious on the inside.

Features of this fridge include four glass shelves (three are adjustable), an antibacterial interior, an adjustable wire bottle rack, transparent fruit and vegetable drawer, a freezer and several adjustable bins.

Sure to be a vibrant focal point of any room, this red fridge from Smeg is perfect for small homes, apartments, cottages and man caves.

[ Learn More About This Red Smeg Refrigerator ]

3. Keurig Mini Plus Brewing System

Coffee is always a focal point, but this funky red brewing system will appeal to more than your taste in beans. In two minutes flat, youve got a great morning or mid-day jolt of java in 6 oz., 8 oz. or 10 oz. It comes with a variety pack of K-cups to get you percolating right out of the box.

Ideal for a single cup on-the-go or to keep at your desk to fuel you through your day, the Keurig Mini Plus Brewing System is easy to store, takes up little counter space and is guaranteed to make the perfect cup of coffee.

[ Learn More About This Red Keurig Brewing System ]

4. Gaggia Espresso Color Machine

If espresso, lattes or cappuccinos are more your cup of tea , uh coffee, then youll love this shiny espresso maker that makes you look like an experienced barista.

One step ahead of the red Keurig machine previously mentioned, this semi-automatic espresso machine uses a stainless-steel boiler to brew ground coffee or pods. Features include a Pannarello frothing wand attachment; filter baskets for brewing shots, double shots or pods; and a passively heated cup warmer.

Ideal for home baristas and espresso lovers in need of another shot of caffeine, this espresso machine rolls out the red-carpet treatment for all coffee fans.

[ Learn More About This Red Espresso Machine ]

5. Dash Chef Series Premium Digital Blender

Crush, blend, puree and juice with ease, thanks to the 1,400-watt, professional-grade motor in this powerful red blender from Dash.

One of the most popular blenders amongst professional chefs, this durable kitchen appliance is an easy way to prepare delicious and nutritious recipes for you and your family.

Features include stainless steel blades, anti-skid feet on the base, BPA-free & dishwasher safe 2-liter pitcher with etched measurements, auto-shutoff safety and six preset settings. With this blender, you can spend less time cleaning and more time enjoying your savory smoothies, soups, dips, sauces, baby food, and more.

[ Learn More About This Red Blender ]

6. Kalorik Red Heart Waffle Maker

What better way to make someone feel loved than with heart-shaped waffles? This easy-to-clean, compact waffle maker has six temperature settings and heating indicator lights so you know when your masterpiece is ready.

Perfect for making delicious, heart-shaped waffles whenever you want, this red appliance is sure to win your heart.

[ Learn More About This Red Waffle Maker ]

7. KitchenAid Artisan Series Tilt-Head Stand Mixer

Perhaps no small appliance exudes elegance like the stand mixer. Add to that the fact that its Empire Red, stainless steel and part of the Artisan Series from KitchenAid, and youve got countertop royalty!

Features include a 325-watt motor, 10 mixing speeds with slide control, pouring shield, flat beater, wire whip, dough hook and power hub for additional attachments. The 5-quart mixing bowl is large enough to prepare 108 cookies, 7lbs of mashed potatoes or 4 loaves of homemade bread.

[ Learn More About This Red KitchenAid Stand Mixer ]

8. Metrokane Mighty OJ Juicer

Although 600 pounds of pressure sounds like a migraine description, its a good thing when it comes to getting every drop of juice out of your citrus fruit, without any unwanted zest or oil. Thats just one of the features of this red-and-chrome retro juicer.

The classic design highlights a time when small kitchen appliances were simple, yet effective. Easy-to-store and even easier-to-clean, the Metrokane Mighty OJ juicer is a great way to add a splash of red to your kitchen.

[ Learn More About This Red Juicer ]

9. Chefs Choice Professional Knife Sharpener

The deep crimson color will help make your kitchen cutting edge, but its sharpening abilities will make this piece a coveted appliance. The three-stage system sharpens, strops and polishes your kitchen, serrated, butcher and hunting knives, as well as your cleavers.

Utilizing 100% diamond grit-abrasives, this professional knife sharpener features a weighted base with non-skid feet, rocker power switch, durable red housing, and a manually activated cleaning tool.

[ Learn More About This Red Knife Sharpener ]

10. Kalorik Red Food Processor (with attachments)

This sassy, red 2-liter food processor from Kalorik can hold its own in the ring with products twice its size, thanks to its 500-watt motor.

With two speeds, seven attachments, and removable stainless steel blades, the options to mince, mash, chop, beat, crumble, fold-in and cut are enough to make any cutting board turn beet red. This food processor includes a device to automatically detect overheating, as well as a safety interlocking switch to protect both you and the unit from improper installation.

Easy-to-store with dishwasher safe parts, the 8-cup food processor from Kalorik will have you prepping food in style.

[ Learn More About This Red Food Processor ]

11. Nostalgia Electrics Empanada Maker

Impress your kids with homemade pockets of deliciousness, thanks to this spicy-red empanada maker. The fillings are only limited by your imagination, and the non-stick surface is incredibly easy to wipe clean until your next culinary adventure.

In less than seven minutes, youll have perfectly cooked homemade empanadas that are sure to satisfy the craving of your family, friends and dinner guests.

[ Learn More About This Red Empanada Maker ]

12. Nostalgia Electrics 64-Ounce Margarator Margarita and Frozen Drink Maker

Make half a gallon of frozen drinks at once and look great doing it with this fiesta red frozen drink maker. Its simple size is designed for ease of use. Simply add some ice, margarita mix and tequila for a perfect drink every time.

Unlike traditional blenders, this frozen drink maker has a patented grind and shave mechanism guaranteeing you never have big chunks of ice in your drink. And its serving spout is a convenient way to prevent messy pours while keeping your guests satisfied.

[ Learn More About This Red Margarita Machine ]
