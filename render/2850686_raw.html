Terryberry Acquires MeritShare, Growing Social Employee Recognition

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

December 2013, Terryberry announced the acquisition of Social Recognition Platform Provider MeritShare, an innovative employee recognition software company located in Seattle, WA. MeritShare was founded with a mission to make recognition simple, fun, and social . It was a great fit with Terryberrys ongoing vision to create exciting and effective employee recognition platforms. Terryberrys established leadership and global presence in the employee recognition industry created an opportunity to help MeritShare customers grow their employee recognition programs and social employee recognition efforts.

About the great relationship that developed with Terryberry, MeritShare founders Kevin Nakao and Travis Pearl said, Weve proven that peer recognition works, and were very happy Terryberry will help MeritShare customers take their recognition programs to the next level.

Its been exciting to welcome MeritShares customers into the Terryberry family. Clients been delighted with the transition to Terryberrys Give a WOW platform for social-media style employee recognition. Additional features like Group Recognition, single sign-on, and tangible awards, combined with Give a WOWs robust administration capabilities have been embraced enthusiastically.

We look forward to continuing to help businesses achieve great results with their employee recognition programs. Read the full news release about Terryberrys acquisition of MeritShare.

About Terryberry and MeritShare

As an established global leader in the employee recognition business, Terryberry shares a passion for developing innovative solutions for employee recognition. Give a WOW is Terryberrys social-media style platform which offers additional features including more award options, an approval process, smartphone apps, your company branding, and much more.

Through the partnership with Terryberry, MeritShares customers will gain many additional benefits and options -- including integrated service awards, global presence, local customer service, and an expanded selection of recognition awards and products.

We stand ready to help you determine which employee recognition software solution has the greatest impact on your people. Let us know how we can help!
