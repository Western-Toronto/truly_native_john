Stuck

StuckFree


Green grass,blue sky,dark grey building with drawings on the wall.I walked into that place passing the gate as a happy man having no idea that his life was going to change dramticallyI went to school for the first time.The first day of school,I remembered,I thought I was going to someplace fun with my parents but instead left alone at this super systematic place,school.Please dont leave me I told my parents,crying and holding on to my dads foot.

That day till this day,changed me.From a nice,kind,forgiving person to a tough,ruthless and an emotionless person.

Robots Factory


School for me was bureaucratic.We all learned the same things,expected to understand the same things and execute the same things when test arise.Albert Einstein said it best Everybody is a genius. But if you judge a fish by its ability to climb a tree, it will live its whole life believing that it is stupid.

What happens when people who knows the same things compete with each other?People who were a slave of school.Well they all eventually become Losers.Who by their mid-life crisis sits down and wonder how did those college drop-out became world famous?.Thats because they didnt follow those dos and dont teachers teaches you.Its because they dared to be risky and followed a Vision that they had.Its also because they did what they truly believe in,not what they are told to believe in.

Caged


Walking in to school everyday sucks,with all those people with limited thinking ideology of how things Have to be,around you.Whats worst is knowing that you're way of thinking is unique,which is good if youre an adult,but sadly thats not the case.Bullied and being left out was one of the things I am known for,at school.I dont want to pretend to care about things I dont care about just to get into groups or gangs.No way.So people leave me out,call me weird.They might not know it,but weird to me is a compliment not an insult,why be normal like everyone else when you could be a unique weirdo.

Your ideas are Stupid


Living feeling Stuck like that was something that I was not going to let ruin my everyday life.School is not 24 Hrs,when schools over,my life begins.I run my own online business,a Youtube channel and a blog.But like they say it Anything that happens online cannot stay hidden for long.Eventually people at school found out,not that I was hiding it from them,but those big bullies definately didnt appreciate the things I was doing.Maybe they were scared that the bubble shell will pop and others will know that theres alot more out there and maybe those big bullies will loose respect.So of course they teased me,called me names and tried to disrespect me in any way possible.

At the End


Being crazy is not a bad thing as long as you dont harm anybody.I realized after being tormented for some-time that these bullies doesnt really matter at all.They are insecure,moreover their jealous pathetic loser.But they will never go way,as time passes by these bullies changes form,to haters,untrustworthy friends,etc.When dealing with bullies,haters and pessimist you need to have one thing in you,that is self confidence.Dont go on thinking that these people are right about you and your a worthless human being because your not.You need to trust in your decision and have faith in your capabilities,its hard no doubt,but this world isnt made to be easy,its made for winners.
