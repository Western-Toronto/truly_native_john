Published on Mar 23, 2012

This recipe is a Californian twist on the breakfast dish, Egg in the Hole. Instead of using bread, I used a thick slice of avocado. Yum!

** RECIPE
- 1 Hass avocado
- 4 eggs
- Olive oil
- Sea salt and pepper to taste

Prep time: 10 minutes
Cooking time: 10 minutes

** SHOW NOTES

FACT: All Hass avocado trees descend from a single "mother tree" raised by a mail carrier named Rudolph Hass, of La Habra Heights, California.

FACT: It's a mystery to what species the Hass tree actually descends from. Rudolph Hass patented his tree back in 1935 which died of root rot in September of 2002.

Avocado Facial Mask
1 avocado
1 egg yolk
1 tsp olive oil
1 tbs warm honey

TIP: Lift up the avocado slice using a spatula while cracking the egg into the center with one hand.

EDIT: Cook the egg for about 3 to 4 minutes total, despite my lack of math skills in the video.
