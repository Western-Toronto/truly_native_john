Hilary Duff Dyes Her Hair Blue

Former teen queen Hilary Duff has dyed her normally long blonde hair a striking shade of blue. Yes, Hilary has decided to buck other hair trends and rock a blue (almost greenish) hair colour. It kind of reminds us of Kylie Jenners recent hair colour (keep in mind Kylie is 17, Hilary is 27). Hilary showed off her new hair colour on Instagram last night thanking her glam squad for turning her into a mermaid. Were not quite sure what prompted her to go for the striking colour, but if she was hoping for attention, she seems to have got it.
