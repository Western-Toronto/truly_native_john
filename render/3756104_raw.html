ABOUT DESIGN FATHER

Good design cannot be described or even measured in a finite way. Therefore, it is thin as air, ageless, sometimes childish, stubborn or even loud. And just like with all eternal kids, one not only can but furthermore should definitely enjoy playing with. In this sense, Design Father is nothing but a backyard, huge and green, filled with the usual suspects.
