Healthy Food Finds: Brown Bag Lunches

  • Previous
  • Next

  • All too often hectic mornings leave no time to pack something nutritious for lunch. Then when lunchtime rolls around you're forced to settle for fast food or order something less than nutritious or worse yet, skip the meal altogether! Luckily we've rounded up some better-for-you convenience foods that require little to no prep, are healthier choices than takeout and delivery, and have staying power that will keep you satisfied until dinner. Stock your kitchen with these brown-bag staples and take the guesswork out of lunch prep on days when going homemade just isn't an option!

  • Applegate Organics Turkey

    Go-to Flavors: Organic Herb Turkey Breast, Organic Smoked Chicken Breast

    Sandwiches are the standard go-to meal for lunch. But ordering from delis or restaurants often means little control over ingredients and lunch meat is notoriously high in sodium! Skip the bloat and make a quick sammy with Applegate Farms deli meat. With just 50 calories a serving, the fat-free organic turkey has no fillers, artificial ingredients, sweeteners, or by-products. Spices like parsley, sage, rosemary, and thyme up the flavor without adding calories or sodium: Clocking in at just 360 mg, the sodium level is half that found in typical cold cuts.

  • Sargento Cheese

    Go-to Flavors: Ultra Thin Provolone, Reduced Fat Medium Cheddar, Reduced Sodium Colby-Jack

    What's a sandwich without cheese? Prevent the slice from taking your sandwich off the calorie deep end by opting for Sargento's slices. Whether you're watching your fat intake or more worried about sodium, the brand has a line to fit your dietary concern. The Ultra Thin variety boasts just 40 calories a slice, while the reduced-fat and reduced-sodium slices still deliver the flavorful punch of your full-fat and sodium-packed cheese favorites.

  • Amy's Light & Lean Pizza

    Go-to Flavors: Italian Vegetable Pizza, Cheese Pizza

    Does the local pizza joint know you by name? Ditch the oily cheese and calorie-heavy dough and stock your freezer with Amy's lighter take on your favorite slice. With just 6 grams of fat and 270 calories, the thin-crust pizza will still satisfy your cravings but save you some major post-lunch guilt. Sneak in some veggies with the Italian Vegetable variety (topped with a delicious combo of spinach, artichokes, mushroom, and cheese), or go simple with the plain Cheese Pizza. Both pack a hefty dose of protein simply pop the pie in the microwave for a filling midday meal.

  • Campbell's Go Soup

    Go-to Flavors: Chicken & Quinoa with Poblano Chilies, Moroccan Style Chicken With Chickpeas

    Soup can be a comforting, delicious lunch option, especially in the colder months. But many handheld and on-the-go varieties are more liquid than anything, which is less than filling and leaves us vulnerable to making a four o'clock vending-machine trip! Luckily, Campbell's line of to-go soups takes the meal from skimpy to satisfying packing every microwavable pouch with zesty spices, vegetables, and chunks of meat for a bold flavor that'll appease your taste buds and your grumbling stomach. Grab a pouch in the morning for a meal that can be ready in just two minutes!

  • Dr. Praeger's Pancakes

    Go-to Flavors: Zucchini and Carrot, Root Vegetable, Spinach

    Pancakes aren't just for breakfast anymore Dr. Praeger's has reinvented the brunch staple by packing them full of delicious veggies and spices and serving them up in the freezer section. Each variety is less than 100 calories and made up of just a short list of all-natural ingredients. Free of cholesterol, saturated fat, and GMO's, the microwavable cakes are a breath of fresh air in the freezer aisle, where preservatives and sky-high fat and sodium levels fill the shelves. Keep a box in the freezer at work, and you'll have six patties on hand for a quick and filling lunch in a pinch.

  • GoPicnic Ready-to-Eat Meals

    Go-to Flavors: Black Bean Dip & Plantain Chips, Turkey Pepperoni & Cheese, Hummus & Crackers

    Have a mini picnic at your desk with these ready-to-eat boxed meals. All 15 varieties are free from trans fats, high-fructose corn syrup, MSG, and artificial flavors. Plus the line features meals designed for specific dietary needs as well, whether you follow a gluten-free, vegan, or kosher diet, or you avoid certain ingredients because of food allergies. Each variety is composed of five individually wrapped foods (plus utensils!), rounding out a nutritionally balanced meal with a major convenience factor.

  • Green Giant Just for One

    Go-to Flavors: Broccoli, Carrots & Italian Seasoning; Corn & Peas in Basil Butter Sauce

    You may think you're making a smart choice by ordering veggies from a local deli or restaurant, but after being sauted in oil and doused in butter sauce, many veggie dishes are major calorie bombs. But what if you could enjoy a portable, single-serving veggie dish for just 40 calories? It's true! Green Giant's line of steam trays combines delicious whole vegetables with savory seasonings and sauces for a super low-calorie microwavable meal ready in minutes. The Broccoli, Carrots & Italian Seasoning variety packs a flavor punch into a tiny 40-calorie serving. And if it's a buttery sauce you crave, you can enjoy the Corn & Peas in Basil Butter Sauce for a guiltless 80 calories. Pair with brown rice for a filling midday meal.

  • Healthy Choice Baked! Entres

    Go-to Flavors: Slow Roasted Turkey Bake, Roasted Chicken and Potatoes

    If you tend to crave a heartier midday meal, these microwavable dishes will fit the bill. The savory mix of lean turkey or chicken with vegetables and a light, flavor-packed sauce rivals any takeout dish, with just a fraction of the calories and fat. No variety in the line has more than 350 calories (and that includes pasta-dish favorites like fettuccine Alfredo and lasagna!). The Slow Roasted Turkey Bake delivers an explosion of fall flavor with juicy turkey medallions, sweet butternut squash, green beans, and roasted potatoes topped with gravy for only 180 calories!

  • Minute Ready to Serve Rice

    Go-to Flavor: Black Beans & Rice

    Minute Rice has long been a lifesaver come dinnertime, but it has now earned a place on the lunch menu too with the new line of single-serve cups, ready in just 90 seconds. The Black Beans & Rice variety has one cup of beans and one cup of rice packaged together, flavored with a Latin-inspired sauce including tomatoes, onions, and green and red peppers. After heating in the microwave, you simply pour the beans over the rice for a protein-packed meal, full of flavor.

  • Peanut Butter & Co. Single Serving Packets

    Go-to Flavors: Smooth Operator, The Bee's Knees

    Mix up your weekday meals by going a little nutty. Peanut butter can make for a filling, healthy lunch when eaten in the right portions, and these all-natural single-serving packets have already done the work for you! Stash some in your purse, desk drawer, or car for a healthy lunch option in a pinch. The protein-rich portable packs pair perfectly with whole-grain crackers or whole-wheat toast, or you can use as a dip for celery and apples.

  • Ready Pac Bistro Bowls

    Go-to Flavors: Mediterranean, Cranberry Walnut, Italiano

    Many of us have the good intention of eating a salad for lunch, but when schedules get hectic, packing your own at home can be too time-consuming, and ordering salads out can be expensive! For days when you want to eat healthfully but are strapped for time, have a Ready Pac Bistro Bowl in the fridge to grab on your way out the door. For fewer than 300 calories, each bowl is perfectly crafted with a flavorful blend of ingredients that delivers major flavor and a dose of protein for an easy and nutritious lunch option.

  • Healthy Choice Caf Steamers

    Go-to Flavors: Pumpkin Squash Ravioli, Tortellini Primavera Parmesan, Sweet Sesame Chicken, Beef Teriyaki

    Another home run by the Healthy Choice team, these steamer meals are a great alternative to ordering delivery when you're craving ethnic cuisine. Surprisingly flavorful for a microwavable meal, these preservative-free steamer bowls come in an assortment of appetizing varieties all under 300 calories. The Italian-inspired meals are 100-percent natural with a fresh taste, making the perfect easy lunch choice for those who usually indulge in carb-heavy pasta meals. And the Asian varieties give you the tangy, savory flavor of your favorite dishes from beef teriyaki to General Tso's chicken in smart portion sizes that allow you to indulge without the guilt. Plus, they deliver a healthy dose of protein to fill you up.

  • Tandoor Chef

    Go-to Flavors: Channa Masala, Chicken Tikka Masala

    If Indian cuisine is more your style, stock up on these microwavable meals from Tandoor Chef. At just 190 calories, the Channa Masala is a better-for-you way to enjoy the spicy, flavorful cuisine. Made with all-natural ingredients you can recognize, the dish features chickpeas sauted with onions, tomatoes, peppers, and flavorful spices like turmeric and ginger for a flavor-packed vegetarian dish. For meat lovers, the Chicken Curry and Chicken Tandoori With Spinach both offer up the meaty zest the cuisine is famous for, for around 300 calories each.

  • Bumble Bee Sensations

    Go-to Flavors: Sundried Tomato & Basil, Lemon & Pepper

    Tuna is a lunchtime favorite, but dealing with a can on the go can be too much of a hassle, and ordering a sandwich from the deli often results in the addition of fatty mayo. Stock the pantry with a few of these Bumble Bee seasoned tuna medleys for a super-easy grab-and-go lunch option no can opener needed! The quick-open lid allows you to enjoy lightly marinated tuna fish in a snap, for less than 200 calories a bowl (and more than 20 grams of protein!). Enjoy straight up or spread on whole-grain crackers or whole-wheat toast.

  • Cedar's Salads

    Go-to Flavors: Quinoa Salad, Aegean Edamame Salad, Chickpea Salad

    Craving a healthy salad but don't have the time to assemble it at home? A container of your favorite greens, plus one of Cedar's premade salads, equals a delicious, healthy meal in under a minute! At just 40 calories per two tablespoons, the flavor-packed recipes are the perfect way to bulk up your lettuce when you have no time for chopping and dicing. The varieties feature delicious combos of simple, fresh ingredients like grains, beans, vegetables, and spices. Lightly dressed with oil and vinegar, these salads even have your dressing covered!

  • Verlasso Salmon

    Go-to Flavor: Smoked Salmon

    Eating at your desk doesn't have to mean boring food. Up the class factor of your meal with these perfectly portioned three-ounce smoked-salmon pouches. All-natural and preservative-free, smoked salmon makes a delicious, diet-friendly meal, with just 90 calories per serving, 3.9 grams of fat, and 14 grams of filling protein. Plus, the convenient slices are ready to eat right from the package! Layer on whole-grain crackers for a satisfying lunch that packs a healthy omega-3 punch!
