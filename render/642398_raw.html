Take Corporate Incentive Trips to the Next Level in Thailand

Your employees work hard for you and when their hard work leads to success, you want to reward them with intrigue, indulgence and inspiration. No matter which combination of these three rewards you're seeking, the best place to take your next corporate incentive trip is Thailand.

With legendary destinations, award-winning hospitality and a centuries-old culture that's as sophisticated as it is soothing, Thailand has everything you need to show your employees how much their work and dedication means to you. Here are some ideas of corporate incentive trips you can take in Thailand.

Luxury and History in Cosmopolitan Bangkok

Bangkok is not only one of the premier hubs of Asia's MICE industry it's one of the most inspiring, dynamic cities in the world. Start your incentives trip off on a high note by inviting your employees to one of Bangkok's world-class hotels, from modern ones, to classic ones, to serene ones.

After settling into your luxurious accommodations, show your guests the splendor of Bangkok's Rattanakosin Old City, explore heritage sites like the Grand Palace, Wat Pho (Temple of the Reclining Buddha) and Wat Arun (Temple of the Dawn). Relax on a cruise down the Chao Phraya River and dine waterside at the Asiatique entertainment complex, where you can enjoy live performances, shop for traditional Thai goods and even ride Thailand's largest Ferris wheel as the sun sets over the city.

All of Bangkok's top hotels include on-site spa centers, so invite your employees to end the day with a treatment, whether they choose an invigorating facial infused with herbs, a relaxing Ayurvedic oil massage or, for those who aren't faint of heart, a traditional Thai massage. No matter which specific trajectory you choose for your corporate incentive trip to Bangkok, the "City of Angels" is sure to inspire and intrigue.

Wildlife and Serenity in the Jungles of Chiang Mai

Chiang Mai , is situated in the country's mountainous, jungle-filled north, the perfect place for your employees to relish in the indulgence of their achievements. Many luxurious accommodation options exist in northern Thailand, properties unmatched not only in their high quality of services, facilities and amenities, but also in the perfect way the staff's hospitality and the pristine surroundings blend together.

After enjoying a cocktail, snack or even a relaxing massage as the sounds and the sights of the jungle wash over you, travel into Chiang Mai's old city center to explore its bustling markets, and to enjoy the soothing flow of the Mae Ping River. Venture up into the hills to traipse through Doi Suthep, an ancient Buddhist temple that literally makes you feel as if you're walking in the clouds. On your way back down to the city, stop at the Chiang Mai Orchid and Butterfly Farm , where peace and serenity meld with the fluorescent hues of tropical orchids and butterflies.

Of course, Chiang Mai isn't all flowers and butterflies it's great for adrenaline junkies, too! Tap into your employees' wild side by taking them to Tiger Kingdom, where they can get up-close and personal with big cats, or instead visit the gentle giants at Mae Sa Elephant Camp. Or, enjoy a more human expression of the wildness within us when you catch a Muay Thai boxing match. You can even sometimes get lucky enough to see one right in Chiang Mai's Night Market, where you can enjoy a relaxing foot massage.

Relaxation and Reflection in Phuket, Pearl of the Andaman

Thailand is world-famous for its beaches and you'll be famous in your company if you take your most valuable employees to Phuket for their corporate incentives trip! Only one thing matches the beauty of Phuket's turquoise waters, emerald hills and swaying palm trees and that's the impossible luxury of its many hotels and resorts. Go all-inclusive with your employees and indulge, or lavish in your own private beach at boutique hotels.

If you can drag yourself away from the beach for a second, it'll give you a chance to explore some of Phuket's many other delights. Or, we should say, beyond the beach and into the sea. Day trips to nearby islands such as Coral Island and the Phi Phi Islands present opportunities to discover the fishes, coral and other marine life that lives beneath the sparkling surface of the Andaman Sea.

Beaches are just the beginning of the inspiration you can find in Phuket. Once you've had your fill of cocktails and fresh seafood for a beachside lunch or sunset dinner, head to Siam Niramit , which hosts an incredible traditional Thai dance performance, providing your employees with a level of intrigue they won't find anywhere else in the world. Speaking of sunset, there are few better places in Phuket to take it in than at Phuket's aptly-named "Big Buddha," a hilltop statue that provides a memorable panorama of one of the planet's most memorable incentive destinations.
