Learn to Automate OSINT Collection


An online course for journalists, investigators, business intelligence analysts and information security practitioners that teaches you how to automatically extract information from the web & social media.

Some Great Things You'll Learn

We will take you from manual search techniques to crafting code to do the following:


Monitor

Watch geographic locations, keep an eye on websites, and hunt for specific keywords in your very own database.

Visualize

Create graphs, maps and dashboards to visualize and explore the information you have collected.

Target

Identify interesting individuals & influencers. Mine through their social media, blog posts and other digital breadcrumbs.

Alerting

More than just web scraping, this course will teach you how to create information pipelines for competitors, investigation targets & news sources.
