Yay or Nay: Goth Lily Collins Goes Pantless

28 comments
Tweet
Thursday October 3, 2013 by Alexandra C.



So originally, this was supposed to be a 'Face of the Month' post because I couldn't get over that oxblood gradient lip paired with the slicked back hair and smoky eye. She's all scary sexy, witchy goth style and I like it. Then my eyes wandered down and I was all, 'dayum girl where'd your pants go?!". Obvi we gotta talk about the whole pantless look.


First of all, that Givenchy Bambi oversized sweater ? Kind of amazing. Disney got all dark and broody and I'm right there with them. Now, as far as Little Miss No Pants goes, I'm on the fence. To be honest, I'm the kind of person who would totally wear this. The whole time, knowing full well that I looked totally ludicrous and it was completely unflattering (being real here, pants are the worst! They are so constricting!). That being said, if anyone is going to look good pantless, it's gotta be Lily Collins and those gams of hers. I do think this might be just a touch more appropriate with a leather mini perhaps? Bonus: shoes are super cute and simple.

Now, on to the face. LOVES IT. I want that lip. I love the smoky eye and those brows are to die for. It's a bit much, but it works with what she's going for. It's chic and edgy and modern. Perfect for a young starlet!

So what do you think? Is it wayyyy too much and over the top? Should Lily put on some pants? Would you ever wear this?
