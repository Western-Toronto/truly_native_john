Featuring NARS Dual-Intensity Blush

Makeup is an art form. It involves color and light and words like sculpting, and certain techniques call for specific tools. There are those with predisposed talent, yes. True artists.

But makeup, like painting, can be learned and on the flip side, taught. Thats where beauty vloggers have changed the game. You want to look like Left Shark from Katy Perrys super bowl performance? A beauty vlogger can and will show you how.

We partnered with NARS on the launch of their new Dual-Intensity Blush (its a double color palette that you can mix together or wear alone, apply wet, dry, while doing a handstand, etc.) and decided to film our own makeup how-to.

Turns out, however, that beauty vlogging is also an art.One thats hard to master. Luckily for Leandra, the blush was the easy part. (We can thank its light texture and naturalfinish for that. Shes still working on her artistic technique.) Watch the video above you cant say we didnt try! and rest assured that were keeping our day jobs.

Except for Simon Doonan. He may have found a new calling. Youll see!

Special thanks to Toast of Toast Meets World , Ahmir Questlove Thompson , Simon Doonan , Michelle Phan , and Pinterest !Video by Jay Buim , makeup by Jenny Smith, Lead Makeup Stylist for NARS Cosmetics.

In partnership with NARS Dual-Intensity Blush .Leandra iswearing Panic Dual-Intensity Blush, Pore Refining Primer , Finland & Alaska Pure Radiant Tinted Moisturizer, Vanilla , Custard & Honey Radiant Creamy Concealer, Eden Pressed Powder, Altai Matte Multiple, Copacabana Multiple, Larger Than LifeVolumizing Mascara , and Bettina Velvet Matte Lip Pencil.
