    International Holocaust Remembrance Day
  • Jan 27, 2014
A visitor walks past an inscription that reads, "Arbeit Macht Frei," which means "Work Leads To Freedom," at the gate of Sachsenhausen concentration camp, whose gate and remaining buildings stand as a memorial, on International Holocaust Remembrance Day on Jan. 27 in Oranienburg, Germany. Sachsenhausen operated from 1933 until 1945. Sean Gallup, Getty Images
A visitor lights a candle in front of a black marble wall with the names of the Hungarian holocaust victims in Budapest. International Holocaust Remembrance Day marks the 69th anniversary of the liberation of Auschwitz-Birkenau. Attila Kisbenedek, AFP/Getty Images
Simcha Rotem, 90, a former Jewish underground fighter, speaks with a visitor as he attends an exhibition on the Warsaw Ghetto Uprising. Abir Sultan, epa
A woman stands between flowers and wreaths in front of the barbed wire of the former concentration camp Buchenwald near Weimar, Germany. Jens Meyer, AP
Two visitors walk inside the snow-covered Holocaust Memorial in Berlin. Markus Schreiber, AP
Former Auschwitz prisoners carry candles as they approach the International Monument to the Victims of Fascism during a ceremony in remembrance of the liberation of Auschwitz. Andrzej Grygiel, epa
Simcha Rotem, 90, a former Jewish underground fighter, looks at a photograph of the entrance to the Birkenau camp during his first visit to the Yad Vashem Holocaust Memorial museum in Jerusalem. Abir Sultan, epa
A boy visits the Hall of Names at the Yad Vashem Holocaust museum in Jerusalem. Uriel Sinai, Getty Images
A delegation of the Israeli Knesset visit Auschwitz marking the 69th anniversary of the liberation of the extermination camp. Andrzej Grygiel, epa
Greek-Jewish Holocaust survivor Isaac Mizan, center, pays his respects during a memorial service at the Holocaust monument in Athens. Roughly 86% of Greece's Jews lost their lives in concentration camps during the course of World War II. Alkis Konstantindis, epa
A Holocaust survivor places a lit candle at the Monument of the Victims during the ceremonies. Czarek Sokolowski, AP
Russian colonel Vitaly Novoseltsev lays down a wreath in front of the entrance of the former concentration camp Buchenwald. Jens Meyer, AP
The sun sets behind the cloudy sky and the crematory at Buchenwald concentration camp near Weimar, Germany. Jens Meyer, AP
A Holocaust survivor arrives for a ceremony to mark the 69th anniversary of the liberation of Auschwitz. Czarek Sokolowski, AP
Wreaths are placed at "Station Z" at the memorial site of the concentration camp Sachsenhausen. Bernd Settnik, epa
