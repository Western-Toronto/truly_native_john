REVIEW: Sony Handycam GWP88 Waterproof Camcorder

Posted by Dennis

Its been a year since we shared our review for the waterproof camcorder, Sony Handycam GW77 . Now with Sony Handycam GWP88 , this series of waterproof camcorders just gets better, with more waterproof depth of up to 10 meters which also comes with a pico projector that lets you project your videos on to any surface under dim lit rooms. All these features for only S$999 ( S$100 cheaper than the GW77 during launch ).

The Sony Handycam GWP88 still maintains the good video quality from GW77 but comes slightly bulkier because of added functionality such as the pico projector. However, it still fits into any small pouch or bag ready for you when you need it. Sony has also made a couple of tweaks to improve the user experience on the camcorder. It has added a front facing record button for selfie video recording and there is a zoom dial for smoother control when you need to zoom in closer to your subject and a mini stand on the base to place it on table tops when needed.

Pros:

  • C risp and sharp video quality with amazing audio even when recording under a loud speaker
  • 10x optical zoom be it where you are standing i a concert, the 10X Optical zoom and 120X Digital zoom will come in handy when you need it
  • 29mm wide angle view landscapealwayslook stunning with wide angle
  • Waterproof, Dustproof, Shockproof your everyday adventure camcorder
  • Photo snapshots turn on smile shutter , the camera will automatically capture an image when making a video
  • Light weight and Compact pop it into your bag carry, when you need it you have it
  • Low LUX mode brightens up video taken during low light
  • Touch Spot Focus and Touch Spot Metering simple & easy way to operate Manual controls, no more overexposure or underexposure on the subject
  • Subject Tracking Auto Focus never lost focus on a moving subject
  • 16GB internal memory for fast recording can expand with up to 32GB microSD/ microSDHC/ Memory Stick Micro card slot
  • Stylishly designed Many people cannot believe it is waterproof. General reaction will be Wheres the waterproof case?
  • USB Charging now you can use your smartphone juice pack to charge the camcorder when the battery is running out
  • Pico Projector you can start screen your video right off the camcorder when ever you want
  • Image Stabilization works very well even when recording at 10X Optical Zoom

Cons:

  • Touchscreen controls are not sensitive when your hands are wet
If you can looking for a camcorder that is NOT SO SERIOUS looking while able to take fantastic quality videos of your favourite concerts or gigs, Sony Handycam GWP88 is the one to invest in. You will not regret your choice. Here are the videos we have took with the Sony Handycam GWP88:
