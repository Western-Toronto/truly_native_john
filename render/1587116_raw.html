Giantbomb the largest video game database

Giant Bomb is an American video game website and is the largest video game database online. The site is created by former GameSpot editors Jeff Gerstmann and Ryan Davis.
Content on Giant Bomb is split between its small staff providing written articles, reviews and video content and the community where all registered users can add to the ever expanding database.
