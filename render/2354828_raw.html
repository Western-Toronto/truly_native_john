Garcinia Cambogia: Just Another Suspicious Weight Loss Supplement Or Real Deal?


Garcinia Cambogia has been in the spotlight for quite some time as it is the worlds latest successful aid for weight loss. The question many have been asking is certainly about its benefits. What seems to attract the attention of nutritionists, scientists and the public?

Known as Malabar tamarind , the gamboge trees are grown in India, west and central Africa and in the southeast parts of Asia. The tree produces sweet, pumpkin-shaped fruit, and fruits in turn produce resin, which has been used as an appetite suppressant in alternative medicine.

The fruit has been widely used in cooking as well, especially the dried gamboge rinds (for curries) in Asian and Indian dishes. It is difficult to eat it raw due to the sour flavor, however dried fruits are very popular.

It is known that the fruit is used as an aid in digestion, menstrual problems, rheumatism and edema in the Ayurvedic medicine. It is also used to make medicine.

In the late 1960s, scientists identified a substance called hydroxycitric acid (HCA) in fruits rind, which had some health quality that required further investigation. This is the key ingredient in Garcinia Cambogia, according to many.

HCA is a derivate of citric acid, and it hinders the work of citrate lyase, which is the enzyme responsible for changing carbohydrates into fatty acids. Due to this ability, HCA blocks fat and produces weight loss.

As a supplement, a very popular one today given the praise it received from celebrities ( Oprah Winfrey, Kim Kardashian, Britney Spears, and Doctor Oz) , Garcinia extract is referred to as an effective weight loss aid because it is a natural suppressant with fat-blocking properties.

However, with so many supplements on the market, one needs to know that not all the products are created equal, and many buyers have been victims of low quality products. With this in mind, researchers have provided information on how to recognize high quality products in order to save the reputation and restore the faith in such quality supplement.

Research analyst Cindy Walters emphasizes:

When youre shopping for Garcinia Cambogia extract online (or right in your own community), make sure that you choose a brand which offers premium Hydroxycitric Acid levels. This will be the best way to ensure that you get the easy weight loss results that youre looking for.

As a rule of thumb, a brand that contains sixty percent Hydroxycitric Acid (which is also known as HCA) will offer the most weight loss benefits.

Conversely, brands that come with lower levels of vital HCA (such as thirty or fifty percent) will probably fail to measure up to your expectations or to the over inflated marketing claims of their manufacturers. So, avoid wasting your money on products which feature lower levels of active ingredient.
