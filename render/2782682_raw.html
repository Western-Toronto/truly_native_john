How Hologics Technology Is Helping Doctors Detect Diseases Earlier

5-part series
Sponsor Content: Goldman Sachs

When Hologic, a healthcare technology company, wanted to strengthen its leadership position in early detection solutions, it needed expert advice on how to grow. For many years, Goldman Sachs has worked closely with Hologic, helping them grow through strategic acquisition from a two device company to become a leader in womens healthcare and beyond. Today, Hologic is helping doctors diagnose and treat diseases earlier and with more accuracy, leading to longer, healthier lives for patients.
